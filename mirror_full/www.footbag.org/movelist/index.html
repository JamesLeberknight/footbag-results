Freestyle Moves and Resources

This is our collection of freestyle footbag moves, collected over the years and contributed by various footbag players. Thanks in large part go to Adrian Dick, Damian Coventry, and Stuart MacFerson of the AFFC club (in New Zealand). We have integrated their "move database" here and will be making improvements over time (so check what's new for updates). Last updated August 10, 2013. 303 moves on file.

2005 Rankings - Open Singles Net.pdf The 2005 year-end rankings for Open Singles Footbag Net were determined using a points system similar to the systems currently in use by other professional sports (see ATP or WTA). A detailed description of the system can be found at http://www.footbag.org/forum/viewtopic.php?t=1243

To upload this file:

  • On Windows, position your mouse over the name of the file above, and click the right mouse button. Select the obvious choice.

  • On Mac, hold down the control key (ctrl) and click on the name of the file, then choose "download link to disk" or similar option. On older versions of Mac, just hold down the mouse cursor while clicking on the name, and the options should appear.

You can also just click on the name above, depending on whether or not your browser recognizes the type of the file. Don't bank on that, however. :-)

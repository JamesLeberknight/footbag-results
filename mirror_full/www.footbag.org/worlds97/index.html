25 Year Celebration!

While 1997 will mark the 18th annual World Footbag Championships, it's also the 25th Anniversary of the sport of footbag, which was created in Oregon in 1972 by Mike Marshall and John Stalberger. (For more information on the history of footbag, click here.)

This World Championships is a special celebration of this major milestone, since the main event in this sport is "coming home" to Oregon to mark the occasion!

Watch this space for any last-minute updates or other information, or check What's New on Footbag WorldWide.

Click HERE for information and results from last year's 1996 World Championships which took place in Montreal, Canada.

Net Finals at The Bite of Portland Fri.-Sun., Aug. 15-17, 1997

Net finals will be held in conjunction with The Bite of Portland, one of Portland's largest civic events. The Bite is represented by all of Portland's finest restaurants, continuous live music, stage shows, etc., and proceeds will benefit the Oregon Special Olympics. See the schedule for more information about dates and times.

Freestyle at World Trade Center Fri., Aug. 15, 1997

The Freestyle finals will be held at the World Trade Center, across Front Avenue from The Bite site. It is an open-air, covered area with beautiful acoustics and atmosphere. See the schedule for more information about dates and times.

Host Hotel Silver Cloud Inn

The host hotel is the Silver Cloud Inn, at 2426 NW Vaughn (just a few minutes away from downtown). Rooms with two queen beds, as well as rooms with one king bed, will be available. There are hide-a-bed couches in every room. The flat rate for the event is $75 for two queens and $65 for one king. With the rooms come free continental breakfast, free local phone calls, laundry service, a fridge in every room, a jacuzzi (and there are jacuzzi rooms available), and a conference room for the freestyle jam.

Call 800-205-6939 or (503) 242-2400 and mention Sole Purpose to get the Worlds rate.

NOTE: the host hotel is current full. If you need an alternative, see the hotel information on the competitor information page.

The friday night will see the singles going through the quarter-finals, at
Lafontaine Park.

The saturday will see the doubles going through the quarter-finals, the semi
and finals for the singles and the mixed going through the semis, at
Lafontaine Park.

The sunday will see the completion of the tournement at Maisonneuve park.

All doubles are classic scoring and all singles are straight scoring.

 FINAL UPDATE...and DIRECTIONS
Attention Kickers...JerseySmurf is an open enjoyment of footbag. There will be no competition, no prize events, just the three Fs: Footbag, Friends, and Fun. NO entry fee, nor any prizes. The focus here is getting together, playing tons of footbag, and catching up with / meeting new friends. Players, and anyone interested in our sport (whether they are fans, brand new players, or retired from playing) can come and enjoy the festivities! Note: I should be able to accomodate everyone...I will have a very large tent in the event that all space is taken in the house. be sure to bring a sleeping bag or some other things for sleeping outdoors!!

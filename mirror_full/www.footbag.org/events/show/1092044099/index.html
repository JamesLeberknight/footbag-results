Russian Open Footbag League (ROFL) starts its final run for 2004. This is the last "challenger" event this year and we hope to make it as big as possible. ROFL cooperates with the huge "Sliyanie" festival and sport expo. The expo is one of the biggest ones in the country and we are hoping to have at least 20000 people coming there every day. The organizers managed to get two sites - one for Net and one for Freestyle - in one of the best parts of the expo. We hope to have a great event with a lot of spectators. We as always welcome foreign competitors. Pre-registration at www.league.profootbag.ru

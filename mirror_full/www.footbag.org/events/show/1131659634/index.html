We are glad to invite the Footbag world for a chance to win our prestigious GREEN CUP! Yves Archambault and Manu Bouchard are our guests of honor for the 2006 Green Cup Presented by Chaos! If you are a net player this is an event you won't want to miss!
Event Details:
The return of GREEN CUP!

All Player information will be on the Footbag Net Forum. Please post if you
are coming and we can 
accomodate you! 

http://www.footbag.org/forum/viewtopic.php?t=3285

This will be the premier US tournament for NET!


Here are some important things to know if you are planning on coming!

1. PLAYERS WILL OFFICIATE POOL MATCHES 
2. WE WILL ONLY PLAY TOURNAMENT MATCHES WITH PRO-Kicker 32 panel bags! 
3. PLAYERS MUST MATCH WITH SUITABLE CLOTHING, UNIFORMS ARE MANDATORY!

Manually Entered Results

Another edition of Zocha Jam tournaments has finished. We managed to gather more
then 30 players from Poland and Europe, who showed the highest level of play
while competing for the top places. We hope that you won't forget this weekend
and you will come to visit us on the next Wroclaw's tournament!

 We are also glad to see that the Polish scece is developing and the players are
rising to higher levels. In the finals of the disciplines all the performances
were really good, and choosing the best players was not an easy task. On the
next big european tournaments Poland should represent well.

  Congratulations for all the winners!

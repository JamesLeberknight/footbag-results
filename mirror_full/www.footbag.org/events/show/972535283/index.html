Directions:
Coming from I-5 north or south, take the West Seattle Bridge exit. Follow 
the West Seattle Bridge and then take the Delridge exit. Go straight off 
the exit and straight through two lights. After the second light, turn right 
into the parking lot of the Delridge Community Center. You're there!

$50.00 for a private room. Their number is (206)-340-1222 and their 
address is 1525 2nd Ave.

blocks. Then look for bus number 20 to somewhere, via Delridge, on the 
west side of the street. The bus will take you to the Delridge Community 
Center. Ask the driver, so you make sure you get off at the right stop. 
The Community Center should be right across the street!!

Rules for the contest are as follows

You have 20 seconds to bust the best 10 trick string you can muster. 
Your score is derived from this nifty little equation right here...
Points of Diffuliculty (Adds, pretty much) + PD/contact ratio + unique 
moves. 

PD+ratio+uniques

For the pro's you only get points for moves that are 3 PD or higher. 
For the intermediates, you only get points for moves that are 2 PD or 
higher.  
You can kick/toe stall in the middle of your string but it counts as a non-
unique trick you don't recieve points for. If you don't complete your 10 
tricks. You don't get a zero, your score will be tabulated as is. You get to 
keep your highest score from either day.

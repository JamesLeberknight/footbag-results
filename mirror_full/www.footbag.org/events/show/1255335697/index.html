Next Era of Prague Footbag is here. Czech Footbag Association would like to invite all the Net players from countries around for a nice day at the 1st Prague Net Open. We secured a nice indoor venue with 4 courts for you to battle over its Nets. Awards Ceremony Party will take place in the same location only few meters on the 'Spike It Bar'. Accomodation will be secured at local players houses, flats and villas. Arranging a better option Hotel or Hostel is not a problem.

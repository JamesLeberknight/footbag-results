Footbag Oulu-Open 2000

Footbag Oulu-Open festival is held up north in Oulu, Finland. This year everything is going to be bigger and better and many European top players are participating.
Website at go.to/oulu- open. (only in finnish)
Festival is located in Oulu city marketplace area. (Kaarlenväylä 2)
 
Events are:
-freestyle
-net (single and doubles)
-consecutive

 
Festival starts at 12.00 pm.
Event Details:
Saturday 29.7.2000




12.00 Intermediate freestyle 1st and 2nd round
15.00 Pro freestyle 1st and 2nd round
16.00 Single and double net qualifying (pro and intermediate)
20.00-> Party!




Sunday 30.7.2000

12.00 Net competitions continue
15.00 Intermediate and Pro freestyle finals
hanging out...
18.00 Closing ceremony :)


NOTE: There might be some changes in the schedule, so please check 
this info later.

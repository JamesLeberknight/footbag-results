Murfreesboro can finally make a name for itself and it's not just because we're the Geographic Center of Tennessee, or because we possess the Worlds Largest Cedar Bucket, (which coincidentally was burned to ashes by a gang of arsonists last year) but due to the fact that it's hosting the 2006 Southeast Regionals. Thanks to Freedom Footbags & Prokicker for sponsoring the event. Spread the word people!
Event Details:
Murfreesboro, TN is 30 miles southeast of downtown Nashville 
& 25 miles southeast of Nashville International Airport.

Barfield Crescent Park in Murfreesboro (about 3 miles from the 
interstate exit). Directions from Nashville East on I-24. First 
take exit # 81A South on 231 and continue south for 2 miles. 
Turn right on Barfield Cresent Rd. and the park is 1 mile 
ahead on the left. Follow the signs toward the disc golf course 
and Shelter #7 is just across the street.  

THERE'S A $10 COMPETITION FEE FOR ONE OR ALL EVENTS.

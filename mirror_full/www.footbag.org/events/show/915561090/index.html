World Footbag Championships will be held July 12-18, 1999. Finals for net are at Montrose Harbor in conjunction with the Mayors Cup Soccer Tournament.

READ ALL ABOUT IT on the WORLDS HOME PAGE!!!

Event Details:
The finals of this event will be held on Chicago's spectacular lakefront at 
Montrose Harbor, in the heart of Chicago.  World Footbag Championships will be 
held in conjunction with the Mayors Cup Soccer Tournament, a 5-on-5 free 
tournament drawing 5,000 participants and over 10,000 spectators over the two 
day period.  

More specifics are on the Worlds '99 Website.
Please be sure to visit there for the latest information.

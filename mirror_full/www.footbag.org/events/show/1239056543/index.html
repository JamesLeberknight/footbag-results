The first edition of the AkiSphere (2009) was a tremendous success. The set- up was great, a lot of spectators sat in the bleachers and watched, amazed, some of the best players of the world showing their best performance. Players were well taken care of. They were offered lodging, food, Gatorades, bananas and even foot massages at will, along with a T-shirt of the event, wall plates and good prize money for the top three of each discipline. The trophy itself, the “AkiSphere” is certainly the main highlight of the event – a marvellous 5 inches diameter 32 pannels steel sphere, carved with the AKIQ logo, on a socle where the names of the winners will be carved for posterity. This was the first summer event organized by AKIQ and any player that was there would say the same: “I’ll sure be there next year”. We intend to make this event the most acclaimed footbag showcase ever, and the way things went show that we may actually succeed!

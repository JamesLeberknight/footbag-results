WELCOME TO FREIBURG

At the foot of the Black Forest Freiburg welcomes you to a Footbag-Event definately made with love! As Freiburg is said to be an nature-alternative-Hippie-city we stick to the stereotype! The eventsite is located next to a relaxable river Dreisam. Green grass an hopefully lots of southern sun will wait for you. If not, lets heat up the sauna . . .

EVENT SCHEDULE

Coming in advance:

Feel free and welcome to come to Freiburg in advance! Enjoy a good time with hiking, park sessions, a relaxed lifestyle or help preparing and promoting the event. Private accomodation within limits. (Please contact Tim Heun timsalabim@gmx.de / +4917631620354)

Friday:

19:00 Meeting at Black Forest Hostel. Good weather option: city crash > campfire and outdoor cooking action (pizza) > party (optional) rainy weather: pizza session and hanging loose at Black Forest Hostel ( kicking / shred / foursquare / pool billard / jam sessions )

Saturday:

12:00 tournament starts (on time for net players:) / food support provided

19:00 dinner for everyone

22:00 Freestyle Finals @ Schmitz Katze (best club in town) > After Show Party @ Schmitz Katze

Sunday:

12:00-16:00 net tournament and pick up games / freestyle session / special contests

16:00-22:00 Freiburg Freestyle Jam @ Schmitz Katze (optional)

GETTING THERE

-intenational flights go to BASEL/MULHOUSE airport

-cheap travelling is also provided by several bus companies (Meinfernbus, Flixbus, Deinbus)

-sometimes German railway also offers cheap tickets

-or you enjoy a beatiful ride over the speed- unlimited Autobahn

Super nice hostel close to the city, event side and moountains! Lot's of nice Gimmicks provided: (big kitchen / table tennis / pool billard / bike rental / shred space / chill out area)

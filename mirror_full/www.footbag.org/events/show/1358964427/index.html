Thanks to all players who made it to Jyväskylä!

JFK March Mayhem - Your annual wintertime footbag net tournament at Jyväskylä (Finland) since 2008. This year it's ONE DAY and DOUBLES NET ONLY. Open doubles for sure and also women doubles and intermediate doubles if enough competitors sign up.

We will use rally scoring, best out of three sets to 21 points (max 25 points).

JFK March Mayhem is a player-friendly event where matches are started in time so you know when to rest, when to eat, when to warm up and especially when to play! For you who won't make it to the tournament this year, we will provide results online very soon after each match is finished.

Register your team (you & your partner) by sending email to hallitus@jfkfootbag.fi and let us know if you need accommodation or help of any kind to make it to JFK March Mayhem this year. LAST DAY TO REGISTER is on Sunday 17-March.

Entry fee 15¤, needed to cover site rental expenses (no T-shirts of prizes included). As an IFPA sanctioned event, you'll be rewarded with IFPA points for your whole day of hard fighting at the court.

For aftergames, Jyväskylä serves you with 39000 students (out of total population of 133000) in a small geographical area which guarantees that the city is lively in the evenings.

Warm welcome to cold Central-Finland!

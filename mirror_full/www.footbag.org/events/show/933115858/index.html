SHARPEN YOUR BLADES!!! Come one, come all to the first of many shred symposiums in Boulder, Colorado. Shred with the local crew and other world- class freestylers for a killer weekend of freestyle shred, including two rounds of 45 second shred contest with prize money. ATTENDEES- Definites: 'Big Add' Chad Devlahovic (CA), Ryan 'Regul8or' Mulroney, Eric 'Wicked' Windsor (CA), Sunil 'Tsunami' Jani (CA), Scott 'Enlightener' Davidson (IL), Richie Abshire (CA), Lon Smith (CA), James Widman (KS), Chris Ryan (AL) and Steve 'Br@t' Goldberg (CA)! Considering: Peter and Lonn Irish (MD)? The Locals: Rippin' Rick Reese, Daryl 'Genzu' Genz, Dave 'Highlander' Holton, 'Shred' Red Husted, 'Galactic' Eli Piltz, 'Jedi' Jonathan Schneider, Paul 'Mr.Sandman' Mestas, 'BigBad' Brad Kaplan, Shay Wright, and more
Event Details:
--LOCATIONS: Saturday/Sunday- Toadstool Playhouse 2475 Mapleton (corner of 
Mapleton + Folsom).  Look for the giant igloo/dome.  11am-6pm.  Monday- CU 
campus, UMC building, rm.157A+B, at the corner of Broadway and Euclid.  11am-
6pm. 
--EVENTS: shred contest starts at 2 pm Saturday and Sunday.  Monday will be
left open for jammin'!  45 second shred contest will be judged by video 
review, with a formula of:  Adds + (add ratio x unique moves) = TOTAL SCORE.  
Unique moves are guiltless for pros and intermediates.  Your 
best score from the two rounds will be your final score.  2 minute routine 
DEMOS (no prize money) will also take place if enough people want to do 
them.  
--DIRECTIONS: a quick word, you will not get lost in Boulder; just remember 
the mountains are west.  Another quick word, public transpo is great here.  
TOADSTOOL PLAYHOUSE- Coming into Boulder from Denver 
on Hwy 36, which transforms to 28th St when you hit town, go North on 28th, 
and make a Left at Arapahoe Ave (one light past Colorado St).  Turn Right on 
Folsom (a couple blocks down), now you're heading north again.  Mapleton Ave 
is three blocks past Pearl St; at the NW corner of Folsom + Mapleton you will 
see the GIANT IGLOO; turn left at Mapleton and park in the lot behind the 
building, not on the street.  Coming from Golden and/or I-70, Hwy 93 turns 
into 
Broadway once you're in Boulder.  Heading north, take Broadway to Mapleton Ave 
and turn Right (East).  UMC 157A+B- By day three you will likely be familiar 
with Boulder.  It's at the corner Broadway + Euclid.  Call Eli for more info

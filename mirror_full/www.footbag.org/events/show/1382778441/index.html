The 12th edition of Austrian Footbag Championships takes place in Vienna again. This event will be open to foreigners as well. Accommodation will be provided on first-come, first-serve basis, we try to accomodate everybody for free. Registration and qualifications will be on Friday in the WUK (like 2011). On Saturday we have two beautiful gyms (pictures), one for the net competition and one for the freestyle events. Finals will be held on Saturday evening in the TÜWI again.

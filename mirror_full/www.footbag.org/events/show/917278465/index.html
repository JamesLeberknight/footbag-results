JUNE 19. 1999 

is kick off time for the first European Footbag Meeting in a long while.
Hosted by the Footbag Conspiracy, FC Footstar Berlin e.V.  

We invite Players of all nations and ages to join us in Berlin for a weekend
of fun and competition. 
This is the firs tournament we are hosting but we think it is about time for
the european footbag comunity to get together for an exchange 
and measuring of skills.

Check out the European Masters homepage graciously supplied by Footbag 
Amsterdam at http://move.to/footbag for detailed information.

You can also find a REGISTRATION FORM on this page. 

DEADLINE: 20th of May!!!!

keep kickin!!!

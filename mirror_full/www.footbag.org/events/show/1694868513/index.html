It's been a while since we last hosted an event, but the time has finally come again. FC Footstar welcomes you all to Berlin for the German Footbag Open 2023! The event will take place on the weekend of November 11-12 in the familiar gym at Palisadenstrasse. There will be competition in Open Doubles and Mixed Doubles  Net, as well as a Freestyle Jam. If there's enough interest, we might have some Freestyle competition as well. And then there's 2-Square, above all. Online Regsitration is now possible at www.fcfootstar.de.

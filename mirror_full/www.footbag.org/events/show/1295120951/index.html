Chaos is pleased to announce that the 2011 Green Cup Presented by Chaos will be held on Memorial Day Weekend, May 28-30, in the San Francisco Bay Area. This event is the second-largest footbag net tournament in terms of quality of play in the world! This event is not to be missed!
Event Details:
The event site for Net on Saturday and Sunday is at Oakland's Lake Merrit 
(near Ferryland). This is where all net prelims will be held. On Sunday 
afternoon, net competition will move to the Bushrod Recreation Center to run 
in conjunction with the Freestyle Event.

The event site for Freestyle on Saturday and Sunday is in Oakland's Bushrod 
Recreation Center (560 59th St, Oakland). 


The final day, CHAMPIONSHIP MONDAY, will once again be held at Peacock Meadow
in Golden Gate Park. Our center court will be surrounded by seating, shade 
structures, and a beer garden! We expect thousands of spectators throughout 
the day and promise that the action will not disappoint. Many thanks to our 
net sponsors Pro Kicker, Lagunitas Brewery, and Tommy's Mexican Restaurant!

Event Details:
****Special Instructions for Late Arrivals****
(General details follow)

If you are arriving after dark to the tourney site PLEASE NOTE:

WILLAMETTE MISSION STATE PARK is a "day use only" park.  That is, for 
everyone *except* those at the Beav'.  We have a special dispensation 
to camp and do crazy stuff.  

SO: After dark the park gate is "locked."  AND the gate is about 1 mile 
from where we camp.  That's a long walk, late at night.  Just ask Steve 
"Jungle Juice" Dusablon and Dave "Why did I Drink That Stuff?" Bernard.

HOWEVER:  The gate is actually "dummy locked" which means that unless 
yer a card-carryin' dummy, you can still get in.  

HERE'S THE SECRET:  On the gate there is a small cast-iron box and if 
you reach up under that box, you'll feel a padlock - a padlock that is 
*not* locked!  Take the padlock off, open the gate, and close the gate 
afterward, replacing the lock as you found it.  (It's best to drive your 
vehicle through the gate before doing this.)

SPECIAL NOTE: IF you LOCK the padlock after you close the gate it is 
NO LONGER "DUMMY-LOCKED" - then it's what we call; "LOCKED BY A DUMMY."  
Anyone arriving later than you will have a long hike to camp and will 
have a few choice words with you in the morn.

TO SUM UP:  Don't be a dummy.

****General Details****

TWO OPTIONAL DAYS at the same low price!  You can come early to the 
park and set up (and camp) on Sept 3 & 4.

This is a CAMPING EVENT! Call us if you have questions on what you
should bring.

Pre-registration is STRONGLY but pleasantly requested by August 15!
(After August 15, t-shirts are not included with entry.)

Entry Fees:  Adults, $50.00, Ages 6 - 13, $15.00, Under 6, Free (but
w/no t-shirt unless ordered seperatley.  Per-night fee for those only
able to attend one night, $20.00

Mail Pre-entry to:
L.J. Conover  11745 SE Wood Avenue, Milwaukie, OR  97222

Entry Includes:  Camp site, t-shirt, beverages, breakfast muffins,
 fruit, coffee, campfire munchies and meals as noted; Saturday
Dinner - Pasta, Sauces, French Bread - Potluck salad (bring a salad to
contribute)  Sunday Dinner; baked potatoes, corn on cob - bring your
own meat for grill.

Adopt-A-Hole:  For a mere $10.00, adopt a golf hole and decorate it in
any wild and wacky way you want.  Adoptors from previous years have
first choice on this event.

Directions:  7 miles North of Salem Oregon.  Take the Brooks Exit off
I-5, go West until the road T's at Wheatland Ferry Road.  Turn right
and follow the signs.

More info available on tournament flyer.  Call if you'd like a copy
mailed to you.

Other things to bring:  Bubbles, twiddle sticks, pig-mania, juggling
apparati, skateboards, snakeboards, bikes, Beaver memorabilia,
citronella candles, beaver-wood carving tools, unused barf-bags, discs
and other flying objects, cameras, gaudy lawn decorations...

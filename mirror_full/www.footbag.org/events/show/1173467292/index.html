Montana Freestyle Jam

The Freestyle Jam is a collaborative display of freestyle arts.

  • Juggling
  • Footbag
  • Fire Poi
  • Breakdancing
  • Slacklining
  • Playboating

The 4th annual Montana Freestyle Jam will be happening in Missoula, MT Saturday April 28, 2007. This event has been gathering more and more momentum each year.

Saturday will be a day of demonstration and workshops to educate the public on all the particular activities. Live music will be played, excellent local beer and food will be sold, and an infinite supply of water will be available (and I'm not talking about the river).

The event site will be the same as it was last year, Caras Park. The location is easily acccesible for all types of transportation, especially on foot, bike, or skate via the Clark-Fork river trail

"If you throw it, they will come."

For more information r if you would like to participate in the event, contact Hugh Healow of freeStyle Media:

  • hugh@freestylemedia.net
  • (406) 855-1538

Hosted by freeStyle Media and the Missoula Footbag Alliance.

Nowe Miasto Footbag Association has the honor to invite all of you to Polish Footbag Championships, which will take place 19-21 August in P│o±sk. This is for the 11th time that Polish Footbag players will gather in order to choose the national champion.

The main organizers of this year's tournament are Dorota Wojtasiuk and Jakub Grabarczyk, working in cooperation with local authorities. The location is highly favorable (P│o±sk is situated no more than 70km from Warsaw, the capital city of Poland) and the venue provides us with perfect conditions for both Freestyle and Net events. Furthermore, there will be a cheap accommodation within walking distance.
As the work is in progress, we can claim that the oncoming event is in a position to be the best Polish Championships ever.
Without doubt, you shouldn't miss it!

Tournament website (attention: it's Polish only): www.plonsk2011.zoska.eu

Venue:
The main tournament area will be the Municipal Sports and Leisure Center in P│o±sk, ul. Kopernika 3. Both Freestyle and Net events will take place in there. Apart from the sports hall, the center consists of: swimming pool with a slide, body building gym, sauna and canteen.



The nearby playing field with artificial turf will also be at our disposal.

What's more, on Saturday night there is going to be a Footbag Videos Festival "ZOCHA". We're trying to organize an event similar to Berlin's film festival and award the best footbag-related videos of the season.



If you want to take part in this competition, please send your video to: zocha@zoska.eu until August 14th. NOTE: Only new, unpublished on-line videos can be submitted, unless they took part in some other film festival this season. For more criteria, contact Jakub Grabarczyk.

ACCOMMODATION:

We propose you two accommodation options. In order to book your room in one of
those, please contact Dorota Wojtasiuk: d.wojtasiuk@gmail.com .

Hotel Martex***
As this three-star hotel became an official partner of Polish Champs, all
players and guests are given a substantial discount: you'll pay only 45 PLN
(~11 EUR) per person per night, no matter which room you chose.
Number of beds: 37, number of people per room: 2 or 3.
Hotel's website: http://www.hotelmartex.pl/

Dormitory
Economicals may chose to sleep in a dormitory belonging to the Complex of
Schools situated very close to the venue(ca. 3-minute walk). If offers rooms
for 2, 3, 4 or 5 people in good price: 27 PLN for the first night and 14 PLN
for next ones. There are 42 beds in total. 
NOTE: You have to bring your own sleeping bag if you chose this option.

Latest news: in case of the lack of beds, dormitory offers additional room
able to accommodate 10 people on the floor. If you arrange a mattress/foam pad
and a sleeping bag, you could benefit from this accommodation possibility for
half of the standard dormitory price: 14 PLN and 7 PLN.

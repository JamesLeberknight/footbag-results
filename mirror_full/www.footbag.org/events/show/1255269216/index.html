25th Annual Midwest Regional Footbag Championships - December 27-28, 2008
-Sunday and Monday
-Free to All Spectators!
-Rooms at the Hyatt (not a requirement), usually $300+, only $89ish
    (See details below.)

Enter this competition (25th MWRFBC), and compete in these events:
    -Freestyle Routines (Open & Intermediate)
    -:30 Second Shred (Open & Intermediate)
    -Freestyle Jam Contest (Novice), 
    -Consecutive Challenge (Novice), 

This year's event takes place at McCormick Place in Chicago (as a part
of "Chicago Sports Fest" - New name for event, and now only 2 days long),
Illinois on December 27th  and 28th.  This event also runs concurrently with the 
New Years Jam Footbag event.

Our host, the Chicago Sports Fest is an event designed to introduce all kinds of
sports to the general public, and each sport has an interactive area where
kids can try them out.  Some sports also have major tournaments going on,
such as Volleyball, Soccer and 3-on-3 Basketball, and of course, footbag.

With Free Admission and discounted parking for the event, this is a great event
to bring the family to.

I want to thank the generous sponsors who I anticipate will be on board
this year: Freedom Footbags, footbag.org and Foot-C Net.  Regardless of
whether they sponsor the event, we are greatful that they have shown such 
consistent support of the sport of footbag over the years, and I believe they 
deserve such recognition.

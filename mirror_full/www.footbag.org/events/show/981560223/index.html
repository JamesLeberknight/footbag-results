Sleeping info:

For all of you coming from out of town we've arranged a cheap place to 
stay. It's 50 Beds in an Airraid shelter at 15,- Sfr. a night (it's got toilets, 
washroom, big diningroom and a sleeping dorm). If you're coming earlier 
than Saturday contact us!

Registration:

You will be required to register for Singles Net by Saturday 12.00 latest! If 
you're only competing in doubles Net register before 14.00 on Saturday 
and if it's only freestyle for you you have to be registered by Sunday 
13.00 latest.

You will be required to pay your registration fee (15,-Sfr. for both, 10,- for 
only freestyle or net) and your accomodations directly at registration (no 
exceptions!).

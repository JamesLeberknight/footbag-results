June 30 - July 1, 2012. Park reserved. Make your plans now!
Event Details:
We always offer competitive net play for pros, intermediates, and beginners. 
We have been the largest Women's tournament for many years now, so ladies come
on out!

WIDMER BREWING is our beer sponsor for the 8th year in a row!  Thanks WIDMER!!
Fun, friends, and good times in a beautiful riverside park.  Experience
footbag in the Great Northwest.  You haven't enjoyed a footbag summer until
you've been to the Northwest!  Everyone welcome.

Beautiful waterfront park has been reserved.  Flat playing fields right on the
river and close to downtown.  Grocery and shopping center one block away. 
Easily the most convenient site on the footbag tour!

Entry Fees:

Open:  $50
Women's Open:  $40 (special rate!)
Intermediate:  $30
Athletic Supporters:  $20
Beginner:  FREE Foot-C play and Net Instruction

**Singles Net:  Player?s meeting and Open matches begin promptly at 10:00am.

Doubles events will be pool format, sideout scoring.

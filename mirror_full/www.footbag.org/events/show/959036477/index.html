PLEASE READ-- Updates have been added on 06/22/00.  ALSO, 
PLEASE READ THIS PAGE BEFORE EMAILING WITH QUESTIONS.
This will be a weekend of fun and footbag. There is not an entry fee, nor are there any prizes. Basically, this is the second coming of the Philly Open, minus the official tournament stuff. The focus here is getting together, playing tons of footbag, and catching up with / meeting new friends. Come hang out and enjoy the vibe as a warm up for the summer tournament scene! As of today (06/09/00) it looks like we have about 25 people planning on attending. The more the merrier! Players, and anyone interested in our sport (whether they are fans, brand new players, or retired from playing) can come and enjoy the festivities! Note: ALL inside space has already been booked, and there is not any additional room. People are bringing extra tents, but we can always use more to make sure that everyone is accomodated. In other words, bring a tent-- that is the intent (no pun intended) of this weekend anyway.. Stay outside and enjoy the weather!! See you there!
Event Details:
Players can arrive as early as friday night at 6:00pm.




Note:  If you are coming by train, plane or bus into philadepphia..  You can 
catch the "Septa R-6" train to Norristown, and from there catch a bus to my 
address (151 west main street, trappe) for limited cash (about $10 if I 
remember correctly).


Host hotel: La Maison Riefer
In other words, this is going to be a campfest complete with a bonfire and 
tunes galore.  If possible bring extra tents.

not be scared off if you do not have your own tent, people are bringing

Confirmed groups of players:  I've heard from some of the DC All stars, the

LOTS OF BRAND NEW PLAYERS ATTENDING!!
See you all there!

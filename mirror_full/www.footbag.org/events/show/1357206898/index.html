The almost legendary footbag tournament Todexon will kick on friday 12th of April and will kick off at sunday 14th of April. This year we have few new things prepared for you. For the first time in the short history of Todexon we will offer Net events and that is Singles on saturday and Doubles on sunday.

Fred Touzelet will be in the house filming a documentary about footbag for his friends project called www.21lifestyles.com. Bring your best shorts and shirts and memorise your part of the footbag story to be told ;)

Accomodation: Please email at dexter(at)footbag.cz if you want us to arrange you a bed. If there would be enough people that write soon, we can arrange a players discount at a nice Hostel near a good transport connection to the event. If you dont write us soon, we cant quarantee a good option. We shall accomodate few of you in player houses as well.

Registration:

Go to he event site listing at: http://www.footbag.org/events/show/1357206898 The registration fee is 400,- CZK and players pack includes tournament t-shirt, saturday and sunday breakfast at the event location, water and fruits during the tournament, small goodies.

Event Location:

The next new thing on this years Todexon is the event location. Every player who has ever attended our sport contest knows that it took place in usually a smoky club room. This year we are moving to a huge hall with plenty of space and people as well. People? Yes, this year Todexon is a part of Freestyle Arena at the biggest Youth, Family and Teen Trade Fair in the country.

The location:

PVA Letnany Beranových 667, 199 00 Praha 9 - Letňany Loc: 50°7'41.662"N, 14°30'51.679"E  Metro station: Letnany (red line – C)

Schedule: IFPA Sanctioned = get ranking points. Note that the schedule times might change – its footbag.

Net Schedule Saturday 10.00 – Players meeting, breakfast and late registration 10.00 – 19.00 Singles Net Sunday 10.00 – Players meeting 10.00 – 18.00 Doubles Net

Freestyle Schedule Saturday 10.00 – 11.00 Players meeting, breakfast and late registration 11.00 – 13.00 Intermediate, Women and Open Freestyle Qualification Rounds 13.00 – 15.00 Shred Break, Shows, Big 1, Shred 30, Sick 3 15.30 – 17.00 Intermediate, Women, Doubles and Open Routines Final 17.15 – 17.30 Awards Ceremony -Shred till you fall-

Sunday 10.00 – 11.00 Players meeting, breakfast 12.00 – 14.00 Circle Qualification 15.00 – 16.00 Circle Final 16.00 – 17.00 Request Contest 17.15 – 17.30 Awards Ceremony

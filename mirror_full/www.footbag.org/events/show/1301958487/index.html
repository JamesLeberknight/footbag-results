2011 US Open Freestyle Footbag Championships

We are proud to announce the 2011 Us Open Freestyle Footbag Championships presented by elitefootbag.com and Fun in the Sun Events (John Stalberger) The Us Championships will be a 3 to 4 day event that will culminate during the Gold's Gym Sports, fitness, and arts festival.

When: August 11th-13th 2011

Where: Settlers Park, Meridian Idaho

Fees: $45 For open ($40 without a shirt), $25 for intermediate (20 without a shirt) $2 for the novice kicking competition

Events:Novice kicking competition(last man standing) Big 1, Big 3, Request, Circle, And routines.

Lodging:

Country Inn & Suites

rate: $75 a night (up to 4 people to a room)

free continental breakfast (normally $10)

shuttle to and from the event site

3355 E Pine Ave.

Meridian Id 83642

208-639-3300 (Use this number to book a room/s) Ask for "Fun in the Sun"

Hotel Reservations NEED to be made BEFORE July 15th. You dont need to pay now you just need to reserve it.

Tentative Schedule: (may be subject to change

Thursday:

  • 7:00pm - Registration at Daryl and Sunnys (2912 S Garden St. Boise, ID 83705)

Friday:

  • 12:30am- Late registration
  • 1:00pm- Players meeting
  • 2:00pm- Circle competition first round
  • 3:00pm- routines first round
  • 4:00pm- Big 1/Big 3
  • 5:00pm- Request contest

Saturday:

  • 12:00pm- public footbag demonstration and Novice competition registration
  • 1:00pm- Novice kicking competitions
  • 3:30pm- Circle contest finals
  • 4:30pm- Routine finals
  • 5:30pm- Rippin' Run
  • 7:00pm- Awards ceremony
  • 7:30pm- Players party at the Idaho Pizza Company ($5 all you can eat)

Sunday:

11:00pm- Casual shred,BBQ, and Disc golf at Eagle Island State Park

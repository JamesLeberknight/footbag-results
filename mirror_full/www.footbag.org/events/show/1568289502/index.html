Dear Footbag Friends, We are more than happy to invite you to the Bulgarian Footbag Open Vol.2.
Event Details:
LOCATION:
 The event will be held at the usual spot  on top of the hill of the 
beautiful "Ayazmo park in Stara Zagora. After recent renovations, the 
park is in sparkling condition. We have 9 badminton courts on our disposal 
with brand new floor and live cameras (so you can watch the live stream 
freely if you cant join). Theres a nearby restaurant as well as many 
picnic areas perfect for chilling. If youre bored you can always visit 
the Zoo or the Hall of Laughter.

LODGING:
 There are a few nice hotels near the park for those who prefer to be 
close to the courts. The other option would be to stay around the city 
center and commute with taxis. For more information on accommodation just 
contact the local players.

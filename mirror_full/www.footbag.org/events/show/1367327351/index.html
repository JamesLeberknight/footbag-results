OK due to the damage from Hurricane Sandy we have had to move our venue to the Red Hook Rec Center for both net and freestyle (about a 10 minute drive south of where Dumbo is). We will still have host housing by the Ellis group, and a free dinner Sat night at there location. Housing is $40 per person per night. Rumor has it(I spoke to him) that the man who started it all may come to this event, Rick Kaufman.
Event Details:
ENTRY FEES:
Professional - $50
Intermediate - $25
non competitors players pack $30(with t-shirt and goodies)
 
LODGING:
If you can, make an arrangement with a NYFA member.  Otherwise, the ECFC is
Renting one house for this event, located at 21727 47th Rd (not street or ave) 
Queens NY 11361.  It will be $40 per person per night. Please contact Ted or 
Jim or post on modified or Fb feeds if you are interested.
 
**Important**: Please bring bedding as this is not a typical rental and is
very discounted. Sleeping bags and air beds are a GREAT idea as the beds are
filling fast and they will go to anyone who fly's first, then people who do
not mind sleeping together. $40 gets you: a place to lay your head, a place
to cook wash clothes and I am working on the WiFi.
 
REGISTRATION:
Registration WILL take place at NYFA members houses or the ECFC house.

LOCATION & DIRECTIONS TO SITE:
All events will be held in the Red Hook Recreation Area in Brooklyn.  Net will 
be played at the softball field area (fields labeled 5,6,7,&8), located at the 
corner of Lorraine St. and Henry St. There is plenty of free street parking 
available.
 
Driving to the Red Hook Softball fields (from rental house in Queens): Take I-
495 (Long Isl. Expwy) west to I-278 (BQE) south.  Get off at Exit 26 and merge 
onto Hamilton Ave. Head straight about Ľ mile then turn RIGHT onto Clinton St.  
Take your 4th left (Lorraine St).  Site is 1 blocks up on the left.

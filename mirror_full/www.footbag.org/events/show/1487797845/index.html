Event Details:
Please check the event's website for further details, such as schedule, directions, 
venue and hotel info right now! There, you will need to fill out two forms for tournament

registration and accomodation registration. 
The registration fee for players will be 50 Euros to be paid on-site. 



ACCOMMODATION
As a registered player at this European Championships, you will be hosted for free 
during the event for 3 nights between May 25-28. The tournament producers 
reserved a contingent of rooms (mainly double rooms, some singles and triples) at All-in 
Hotel and NH Hotel in Aranjuez for all players, who actively take part in the event. Only

upon prior announcement to the tournament directors, it is possible to bring family 
members and other companions. We will try to meet your needs on this, but we will 
need to be informed directly ahead of time. 

If you're planning on arriving on Wednesday, May 24th, which will in many cases be 
necessary in order to participate in the footbag net events starting on Thursday 
morning 10.00am, or stay one more night until Monday, May 29th, you will be able to 
get a very good deal for already reserved rooms in the All-in Hotel. For this, check out 
further details about how to contact the hotel on the event's website 
www.footbag.online

You will receive food vouchers for local restaurants in addition to the 3 hotel nights 
including breakfast. 

All further information can be found on the event's website, which is online now! 
So, go there, get more information about Euros and register there right now!

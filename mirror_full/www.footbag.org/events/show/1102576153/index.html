Accommodation during Worlds 2005 in Helsinki, Finland: 

The organisation on the Footbag World Championships 2005 recommends to all
participants the following options for accommodation during the competition.
All chosen options have a criteria of easy access from the
Hotel/Hostel/Camping site to the central railway station in Helsinki and to
the freestyle and net event site, which are located at the same spot! Exact
rates of rooms, what the rates include, location on map etc. you can find from
the websites listed below, or by contacting the Hostels by phone (some of the
below listed Hostels booking offices are open only at office hours between 9am
and 3pm, Finnish time is +2 hrs GMT).


A part of the Hostels listed below take reservations online at www.hostels.com
and www.hostelz.com.


More information about the public transport in Helsinki from www.hel.fi/hkl.


A general map of Helsinki online at http://kartta.hel.fi/.


The address of the event site is Helsinginkatu 25 (Helsinki sports hall,
freestyle & finals day) and Helsinginkatu 23 (Brahe sports field, net site).

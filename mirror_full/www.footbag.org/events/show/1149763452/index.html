The French Open will be hosted for the 3rd time in a row in Paris. This IFPA sanctioned event is the biggest indoor footbag competition in Europe. Last year over 75 players from around the world from Australia to Canada came to Paris. This is a unique chance for our local Champions to challenge in France the world?s elite. Frankfort?s best net players will once again come to Paris to show their skills and defend their titles, among them World Champion Florian Goetze. Old Europe's best freestylers will as well head to Paris. The Swiss Footbag Team will be represented among others by the Daouk family and Renato Zülli. The registration fee is 30 ?, which includes 1 free tournament t-shirt, free Breakfast Friday, Saturday and Sunday, free Dinner Friday and Saturday.

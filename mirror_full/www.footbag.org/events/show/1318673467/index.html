The 10th edition of Austrian Footbag Championships takes place in Vienna again. This event will be open to foreigners as well. Accommodation will be provided on first-come, first-serve basis. As last year qualifications will be on Friday in the WUK. We've got a nice gym for Saturday and finals will be held on Saturday night in the T▄WI. Also, on Saturday some freestyle football players will join us and they’ll have a battle to choose their winner. As there still are no real net players in Austria, there will only be Freestyle competitions. Here's a map of our 3 locations (WUK, the gym and T³wi):

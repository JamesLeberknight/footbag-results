The 10th edition of Austrian Footbag Championships takes place in Vienna again. This event will be open to foreigners as well. Accommodation will be provided on first-come, first-serve basis. As last year qualifications will be on Friday in the WUK. We've got a nice gym for Saturday and finals will be held on Saturday night in the TÜWI. Also, on Saturday some freestyle football players will join us and they’ll have a battle to choose their winner. As there still are no real net players in Austria, there will only be Freestyle competitions. Here's a map of our 3 locations (WUK, the gym and Tüwi):

the 3 locations (WUK, USZ Schmelz & Tüwi) auf einer größeren Karte anzeigen
Event Details:
The 15 EUR entry fee includes a free dinner and free entry to the party on
Saturday! 
You have to register online until December 7th!! There will be no
late-registration! Please, be also ready to check in on Friday at 6 pm at the
WUK! If you aren't there, you will be scratched!  


Schedule: 

:::Friday::: 

@ WUK (Währingerstraße 59, stairway 5, first floor, 1090 Vienna) > Initiativenraum

18:00-19:00 Players Check-In (No on-site/late registration!! Only players who
registered online can compete and check in!)
19:30 Women Routine qualification
19:45 Open Routine qualification
20:45 Intermediate Circle
21:00 Open Circle Contest
22:00 End

There will be food, a christmas-tree, drinks, cookies, etc. 

The party for Friday night is still t.b.a.! 



:::Saturday:::

@USZ (Auf der Schmelz 6, 1150 Vienna) > Gym no.4

13:00 shred/4-Square/warm-up
14:00 players meeting
14:30 Shred 30 + request contest
16:00 4-square tournament
18:00 End (in the gym) 

This venue is a medium sized gym at the Sports University.  
Snacks (sandwiches) and drinks will be provided for low costs.


@TÜWI (Peter-Jordan-Str. 76, 1190 Vienna) 

19:00 free dinner for registered players
20:30 – 22:00 footbag finals (women, doubles, open routine)
22:00 Party
DJs:
dADAZ + Lefebre (with a spezial funky/disco/techno/groovy MIX)
SHROOMBAB (dnb)



:::Sunday:::
13:00 farewell brunch

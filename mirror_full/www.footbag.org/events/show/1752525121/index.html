Manually Entered Results

The last tuesday we gathered 6-7 players in Ugasko (Deusto-Bilbao) to play the VI. 
Basque Tournament of Footbag Net (Doubles). The matches were very balanced and 
although the venue may not have been the best to play, we played 2-3 quite intense 
matches. Finally the couple formed by Lander Campo and Egoitz Campo won the 
tournament. Here the results:

Friendly and fast match
David Moreno/ Joseba Zubiaurre 3-2 Egoitz Campo/ Lander Campo

First match
Egoitz Campo/ Lander Campo 15-7 Mikel Torde/Daniel Barrientos

Final
Egoitz Campo/Lander Campo 10-9 Joseba Zubiaurre/ Jon Proaño (David Bilbao)

Classification

1-Egoitz Campo (Basque Country) and Lander Campo (Basque Country)
2-Joseba Zubiaurre (Basque Country) and Jon Proaño (Basque Country) *
3-Mikel Torde (Basque Country) and Daniel Barrientos (Basque Country)

* David Moreno also played with Joseba Zubiaurre and Jon Proaño.

Congratulations to all players!


The 15th of july we will meet 6-8 players and made 3-4 couples to play a new basque tournament of footbag net (doubles). If all goes well, semifinals, 3º and 4º position match and the final.

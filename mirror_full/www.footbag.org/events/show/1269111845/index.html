DUE TO EXTREME ADVERSE WEATHER CONDITIONS (RAIN, SNOW, STRONG WINDS, MUD, AND SOOOOO COLD), THE TOURNEY HAD TO BE POSTPONED. Originally planned May 8 and 9, only few local players showed up. We democratically voted to postpone the event. There's your chance to come to Montreal. This time, it is for real!!! In 2009, Aki Quebec organised its first "singles only" net tournament. It was a success but players had to fight their way to the top on a single day (finalists ended up with cramps). This year, 3 major updates: the tournament will be held outdoors, on a two days period, and 3 crazy kicking games will be organised. Who's going to be King of the Hill in 2010?
Event Details:
25$ open. 15$ Intermediate.

Show up at the Jeanne-Mance park, between Du Parc street and the football 
court. (see Google map 45.515858,-73.586597).

11AM to 4PM: pools and up to round of 4
4PM to 6PM: crazy kicking games (prelims)

Sunday
11AM to 4PM: consolations, semis and final

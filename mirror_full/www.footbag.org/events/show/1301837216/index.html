Conclusion: Ce format de pointage a été apprécié par les joueurs. Il a permis de

Frinki

The King of the Hill was created in 2009 to restore the image of the singles discipline, which is increasingly set aside in tournaments, often for lack of time. Come try to wrest the prestigious title to Francis D.-Pelletier and do not expect direct scoring: all games will be long and hard, under a blazing sun, and you'll have trouble every step of the ascent to the summit of the mountain. To reward you for your efforts, Aki Quebec is organizing some hilarious skills contexts. A great tournament to attempt, for warriors only.

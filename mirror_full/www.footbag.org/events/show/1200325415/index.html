GRASS = Greater Rochester Area Shred Symposium

That's right people, GRASS 2008 is happening. The Rochester Footbag 
Association and the RIT Juggling Club are joining forces once again to bring 
you the largest upstate New York tournament ever, GRASS 2008.

CONTACTS:
Mike Anello - 585-455-9706
Paul Agostinelli - 585-309-1518

DATES AND TIMES: 
Friday, April 18: Day 1, Open Shred in the Clark Gym
6:00 pm - 10:30 pm - Open shred in the Clark gym at RIT.

Saturday, April 19: Day of Competitions in the Clark Gym
11:00 am - 12:00 pm (noon) - Registration for events. 
12:00 pm - 3:00 pm - Competitions. 
3:00 pm - ? - Open shred. 

Sunday, April 20: Last day of GRASS. 
? - Open shred somewhere on RIT camus. Details coming soon.

REGISTRATION FOR EVENTS: 
Saturday, April 19, 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM (noon) - Your window of opportunity. 
You can register ahead of time by sending me a
private message and indicating
which level you are competiting in (intermediate or open) and which events. 
You cannot mix levels and events, you either compete intermediate or open for
all events you choose. 
You do not have to register if you are not competing.

EVENTS:
Sick 1 - Best single trick you can execute. 
Sick 3 - Best combination of three tricks you can string together. 
TEN - Hit any move of your choice 10 times consecutively. 
Circle - Good old-fashioned circle kicking. 
Routines - 2 minute routines choreographed to music.

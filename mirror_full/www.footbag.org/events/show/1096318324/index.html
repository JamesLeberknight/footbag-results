The 3rd Annual Seattle Juggling and Footbag Festival is bound to EPIC!

This is the only gathering of it's kind in the world and it is bound to bigger than ever. Already the biggest footbag event on the west coast, this year we'll be offering even more games, competitions, shows and workshops from some of the top jugglers and footbaggers in the world.

Past attendee's, many of which have already confirmed for this year include:

  • Brothers from Different Mothers
  • Peter Irish
  • Dan Holzman
  • Allan Haggett
  • Aaron Gregg
  • Jim Penske
  • Lon Smith
  • Vanillatown Vaudevillians
  • Sunil Jani
  • Checkerboard Guy
  • Carol Wedermyer

  • Jess Monroe and more
"I wouldn't miss it for anything"

This event was featured in Juggle Magazine as a MUST GO Festival!

Last years festival was off the hook and expect nothing less this year. We have a new venue that is easier to find and will attract more people. As an added bounus, the renegade show will take place at the Historic Hokum Hall. Don't miss it.

(Festival $5, Public Show $10 and Renegade is FREE)
(Footbag Competion is $30 for pro and $25 for intermediate, both include fest and show tickets. We are too late for online registration but be prepared to register when you arrive at the Festival)

This event will be open to foreigners as well. Accomodation will be provided on first-come, first-serve basis. Qualifications will be on Friday in the WUK. We've got a nice and big gym for Saturday and finals will be held on Saturday night in the TÜWI. As there are no real net players in Austria, there will only be Freestyle competitions. Footbag Net can be played for fun on Saturday in the gym though. Here's a map of our 3 locations (WUK, the gym and Tüwi):
the 3 locations (WUK, USZ Schmelz & Tüwi) auf einer größeren Karte anzeigen
Event Details:
The 15 ? entry fee includes a free dinner and free entry to the party on
Saturday! 
You have to register online until December 5th!! There will be no
late-registration! Please, be also ready to check in at 6 pm at the WUK! If
you aren't there, you will be scratched!  


Schedule: 

:::Friday::: 

@ WUK (Währingerstraße 59, stairway 5, first floor, 1090 Vienna) 

18:00-19:00 Players Check-In (No on-site/late registration!! Only players who
registered online can compete and check in!)
19:30 Women Routine qualification
19:45 Open Routine qualification
20:45 Intermediate Circle
21:00 Open Circle Contest
22:00 End

There will be food, a christmas-tree, ginger bread, glogg, etc. 

The party for Friday night is still t.b.a.! 



:::Saturday:::

@USZ (Auf der Schmelz 6, 1150 Vienna) 

13:00 Shred & Spike (net for fun/4-square tournament/shred)
15:30 Shred 30 + request contest
18:00 End 

This is venue is a big gym at Sports University.  
Snacks (sandwiches) and drinks will be provided.


@TÜWI (Peter-Jordan-Str. 76, 1190 Vienna) 

19:00 free dinner for registered players
21:00 Women routine finals
21:15 Open routine finals
22:00 Price ceremony
22:10 Party - DJ-lineup (Dr Gre (funk food); Lefevre (Produckt) electro,
techno; Urbanfreak & Mojo (D.O.H.) dnb) 


 
:::Sunday:::
12:30 farewell brunch

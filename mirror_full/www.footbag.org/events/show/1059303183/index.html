 2nd Annual Austrian Championships - ROOTS18
Finally all the new talents can show what they have learned in the past year. Two days of footbag action, at the "perl of vienna". The Türkenshanzpark recieved this name because of its historical and kultural meaning, and its beautiful landscape.
During the day time workshops, little contests, 2 music floors, live percussion shows, and live bands will provide the right flair. An art&photo gallery for the calm, and a splash pool for the cool. Beachvolleyball court next to the streetball, and soccerfield and a BiG playground for the kids!
The nights @ TÜWI is the name of the Bar where we'll have the first Party, which will be run by 10 different dj's and at least 4 different live-concerts. Tüwi is 5 minutes walk from the Event-grounds
All money- incomes are for charity purposes - Integrationshaus
After the Party you walk 0.05 minutes to your FREE sleeping place.
Footbag videos & visuals will also start the second party night, which includes the award ceremony.(set new standards:)
Eventhough this event is planned annualy, try not to miss it. The philosophie of ROOTS18 - 'the BiG chill'´, is for YOU to have a good time!

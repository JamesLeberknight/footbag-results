For the past 5 years the Colorado Shred Symposium has been one of the largest US Freestyle events, and this year will be no different. Freestylers everywhere are encouraged to attend this epic event. This years location will be the same as last years. St. Cajetan's Church, on the Auraria Campus, is at the center of three colleges and is a 5 minute walk from Beautiful Downtown Denver. Promotions for the event are in the works and we are aiming for record attendance. Stay tuned for more regarding registration, prize money, and host hotel. Information will be up in the coming weeks.

UPDATE.... 
Sorry to do this, but the location of the Players Party has changed...for the better. 

The Party will be held at "Old Chicago" 
1415 Market St. 
Still only 3 blocks from the Host Hotel 

After looking into it, they had a much better Party Venue for us. We have rented out the
Back Bar Room for our personal use. It has open space for those who want to shred, pool
tables, video games, TV's and our own bartender. Everyone of every age is allowed in no
matter what time it is. It's bound to be a much more relaxed and fun time. 

See you all there.

HOST HOTEL:
This year's host hotel will be the Executive Tower Inn in Downtown Denver
http://www.exectowerhotel.com
Here is a printable Walking Map from the Hotel to the Event Site.
http://chrisholden.net/css6map.jpg

They were our host hotel last year and were very gracious and easy to
work with.  The hotel is only 5 minutes walking distance to the event
site and only 2 blocks from the 16th St. Mall which is home to much of
Denver's nightlife.

You can make your reservations by calling (toll free) 1-800-525-6651.
Tell them you are coming in for the Colorado Shred Symposium to get the
discount rate.

Price per night:
$69.00 + tax(US) 1 or 2 person occupancy
$79.00 + tax(US) 3 or 4 person occupancy

Driving Directions to Executive Tower Inn.
Directions: 
From Interstate 25 - Take Speer Blvd. South, (the second Speer exit).
Follow Speer Blvd. into the downtown area, and turn left at Lawrence St.
Go 2 blocks, and turn right on 14th St.. Go 2 more blocks and turn left
onto Curtis St.. The hotel is directly on the left side. Turn left into
the first garage entrance.

From Denver International Airport- Take Pena Blvd. approximately 10 miles
to I-70 Westbound. Take i-70 West approximately 15 miles to I-25 South.
Take Speer Blvd. South, (the second Speer exit). Follow Speer Blvd. into
the downtown area, and turn left at Lawrence St. Go 2 blocks, and turn
right on 14th St. Go 2 more blocks and turn left onto Curtis St.. The
hotel is directly on the left side. Turn left into the first garage
entrance. 

Guest of the hotel are eligible for a reduced parking charge that includes
in and out privileges. Just bring your ticket to the front desk and have
it validated for this special rate. The hotel has five levels of indoor
parking. 

Shuttle and Car Rental Information: We recommend a service called "Super
Shuttle" for transportation to and from Denver International Airport.
Please call 1-800-BLUE VAN. for reservations and pricing information.

Rental Cars can be arranged upon request. Public ground transportation
terminals and the local train station are only blocks away.

GREATER ROCHESTER AREA SHRED SYMPOSIUM!!!!! the dates of the event are Friday April 27th through Sunday April 29th. This is the third year of GRASS and a whopping 30th anniversary year of the juggle in. Organized this year primarily by Jeff Peden and the RIT juggling club, there is a huge list of amazing preformers from all over the world for the show on saturday night. You can get all the pertinent information for accomidations, hotel rates, restaurant information, campus maps and anything else you're interested in for the event at this link: http://www.rit.edu/~jugwww/
Event Details:

Event Details:
For the sixth time, the Polish Footbag Association brings you the Zocha Jam.
Come and compete against the best players, including the current reigning
World Champion Damian Gielnicki! We will make sure you won't be dissapointed.

VENUE:

All competitions will be held inside a sport gym called "Sport Werk". It's exact
adress is Kozanowska 69. The "Sport Werk" gym is a place with good sport
facilities and is more then suitable for an athletic event.

ACCOMODATION:

We offer free accomodation for the Saturday night. It will be held in a building
owned by Sektor 3 (a company helping non-governament organizations). The place
has bathrooms, but no showers (but you can shower in the gym). If you want to
stay there please bring your own sleeping mat and sleeping bag (it's floor space).

If you prefer other options please contact the organizers - we will try to find
 a hotel for you or come with another solution.

REGISTRATION FEES:

Open category - 30 PLN (for any number of events)
Women cagetory - 20 PLN
Intermediate category - 20 PLN

Please make sure you have the exact amount in PLN. We will not accept Euro.

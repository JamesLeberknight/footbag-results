A Few announcements:

The GOOD NEWS    -We have secured the venue and gained use of on of the large 
gyms with rubber floor for excellent traction when shredding.  We have this 
for both days
                 -Another good piece of news, I have eliminated any fees for 
Competition Entry.  If you have some skills, now it costs nothing extra to 
give it a try.  
                 -More good news, the shirts that can be purchased at the 
event have been drastically reduced in price from around ten dollars per 
shirt to just Six Dollars ($6.00 per shirt).  They are really cool and a 
great shirt to wear for kicking or going out in.  This is cost for me, so the 
price is as low as possible.

The Bad News:

The Price for admission has just been raised by our Venue, the NAC, to $15.00 
per person per day.  This is a great Venue with a large area for lots of 
shredding, over 11 (Eleven) Hours Worth of Shredding all told, water 
fountains that are easily accessible, and other accomidations within close 
proximity to the Gymnasium including restrooms and Vending machines and a 
snack bar.  The music will be great, we have pros and legends alike comming, 
and it should make for a kick ass weekend.

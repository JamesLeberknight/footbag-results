  HI FOOTBAGUERS!!!

The Spanish footbag club is proud to announce that Madrid is hosting the ?13th EUROPEAN FOOTBAG CHAMPIONSHIP MADRID 2011?.  The dates are 8 , 9, 10 of July.

The local club is doing a big effort, to have a great event; it?s the first time in footbag history that Euros are taking place in Spain.

The action is going to take place in the southern part of Madrid, the name of the place is ?Polideportivo de Orcasur? its very well communicated, 20 minutes by metro from the center of the city.
You can see some options about accommodation that we recomend to you.

We know that some of you are afraid of the weather, so we are going to provide all the players to have fresh water, and some places with shadow to rest, before or after your matches.

Madrid is a nice city to visit, with a lot of things to see. That?s why we decide to do a little tour for players one day before the event (thursday 7 of July), this day we are going to visit some monuments and also take some traditional (cańas and tapas) beers and food.

Hurry up to book your flights, it´s going to be an awesome weekend.

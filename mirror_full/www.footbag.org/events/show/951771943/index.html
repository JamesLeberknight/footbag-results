Events Offered: 
FREESTYLE!  Shredding, shredding, definitely some more 
shredding.  There will be no formal competition... but there
will be a PHAT trick/combo demo.  So sharpen up those 
blades.


Manually Entered Results

Everyone won.  Please come next year.


Event Details:
Both days are at the Millberry Student Union on the campus of the
University of California at San Francisco:

Sat., April 1 - Aerobics Studio, Millberry Fitness Center 
                (Level H of the Millberry Union) on the UCSF Campus

Sun., April 2 - Golden Gate Conference Room, Millberry Student Union
                (Level I) on the UCSF Campus
11:00 am until about 5:00 pm (as long as people still want to shred) on each 
day.

DIRECTIONS:

Within San Francisco:
Millberry Student Union is on the UCSF Campus which is just west of
the Haight/Ashbury district. Just take Parnassus west of Stanyan St.
for a few blocks and Millberry Union will be on the right. (500 Parnassus)
Park in the public deck under the Union itself (only $1.00 with gym 
validation).  

From the East Bay:
I-80 West towards San Francisco. Take the exit towards MISSION ST/FELL 
STREET. 
Merge onto US-101 N. Take the DUBOCE AVE/MISSION ST exit. Stay straight to go 
onto DUBOCE AVE. Turn SLIGHT LEFT onto MARKET ST. Turn SLIGHT RIGHT onto 17TH 
ST. Turn RIGHT onto STANYAN ST. Turn LEFT onto PARNASSUS AVE. 
Millberry Union will be on your right at 500 Parnassus Avenue.
Park underneath the Union in the public deck (just $1.00 with validation from 
the gym).

From the Peninsula:
Take I-280 to the 19th Avenue Exit (Calif. Rte 1). Follow that all the way to 
Judah St (2 blocks south of Golden Gate Park).  Turn east (right) onto Judah 
(the street numbers will be decreasing).  At 5th Ave., Judah will turn into 
Parnassus Ave, and the Millberry Union will be on your left at 500 Parnassus 
Ave. Park under the union (turn left at the "public parking" sign) for just 
$1.00 (with gym validation) in the Millberry Union garage.

Contact Sunil if you are planning to attend and need any further info.

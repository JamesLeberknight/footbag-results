ATTENTION : pre-registration is online and registration will take place on the 2nd of july. Please check the website for planning evolution. See you all soon!
Event Details:
We all look forward to see you in Montpellier next week! On Wednesday,
registration will start at 18h at The Rockstore, 20 rue de Verdun in
Montpellier. It is very close to the train station - most people know the
place, so just ask anyone.

For those of you that are driving, just follow the signs for Montpellier
Centre. Try to find a parking close to the train station (Gare Saint Roch).

From the airport, take the bus into town for less than 5 euros. The bus stops
a refreshing 10 minute walk or a short tram ride from the train station.

Arriving by train? Great, you have only 100 metres to walk!

If you arrive earlier and want to meet up, or just need a place to keep
your bags, let us know and we will help you out.

If you haven't registered yet, now is your last chance!

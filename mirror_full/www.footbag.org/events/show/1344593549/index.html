The Austrian Footbag Champs are as always open to all Footbagers around the World! You can find the basic information here! Complete information is provided at the event website: http://www.gravityfighters.at/TWINE/austrianfootbagchamps2012.html (scroll down for the english texts) for last minute information it is always a good idea to check the facebook- event: http://www.facebook.com/events/461380853906309/?ref=ts&fref=ts We will have a sweet gym for freestylecompetition and you can sleep for free in the same building (on gym mats). The venue for the freestyle finals is within walking distance! If you want to stay at a hostel just let me know - we also reserved a couple of rooms at a hostel (26-30Euros, 10min by tram). The Freestyle Finals will happen on Saturday evening at a nice (maybe the best) location in linz with 300 spectators and a big freestyle party. An additional feature will be the first screening of the footbag documentation "The Whirl is not enough" at a cinema right before the finals start on saturday. we will most likely also offer "Open Single Net"! Since we only have 3 courts on Saturday - it will only be offered if a maximum of 12 people is going to compete. But I think that's a quite reasonable number and we are looking forward to having more net players around in Linz! For latest news, check out our facebook page for the movie: www.facebook.com/freestyleTWINE

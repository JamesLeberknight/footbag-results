This is the 4th annual Air-a-Zona Footbag Net Tournament and will be held at ASU across from the Rec Center. Please note that this tournament is THREE days. It will be Saturday,Sunday, and Monday.
Event Details:
Directions to the site:
From the airport, take the 202 East	
Exit on Scottsdale Road
Turn Right onto Scottsdale Road (South)
Turn Right onto Apache
Pass the first light (McAllister) and then take first right into ASU 
(There is a special turn lane for this)
As you turn right you will notice a large parking structure to your left.  
Park there.  It's free.  Across from the parking structure (to your right) is 
the recreation center at ASU.  The tournament site is on the grass just north 
of the rec center.

My dear Footbag & Disc friends, UNITE !

The Footbag & Disc Opening 2005 for the 4th time in a row & will rock the lake ! This year (like each year) we promise the competitors and their friends an unforgatable Summer Opening 2005.

What does Opening mean ?
- Competion OUTDOOR
- Food OUTDOOR
- Party OUTDOOR
- Sleeping in tents, but OUTDOOR !

Last year we had 115 Competitors, and a 10 X 10 meter stage for the freestyle games. Now we've gained some space. A total of 45 meters with a neat centercourt for the net-games, and a DJ which will be grooving on a 3 meter high tower for us.

Don't forget to register online, since that's how you recieve these advantages !!!
I guess we'll see eachother !
footbag-vienna team

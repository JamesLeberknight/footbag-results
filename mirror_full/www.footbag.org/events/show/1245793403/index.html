Manually Entered Results

Thanks so much to all the competitors and spectators who came to the 24th 
annual Funtastik Summer Classic. We appreciate how long some of you have 
traveled to get here, your energy, and your love of Footbag.  We had 33 
competitors and plenty of Footbag friends and spectators throughout the 
weekend. The weather was wonderful, TV Channel 27 did a real nice coverage on 
the event and a good time was had by all.  

Sunday night we honored the 2009 Hall of Fame inductees at the host hotel- 
Holiday Inn.  Master of Ceremonies, Peter Irish, entertained the crowd with 
stunning juggling acts and enlightened everyone with Jane Jones-Zerbe and 
Justin Sexton?s Footbag accomplishments.  Steve Smith was honored by Scott 
Davidson Brenda Solonoski spoke about Carol Weidemeyer. Sunny Freeman Genz 
highlighted events and facts about Daryl before he received his formal 
induction.

Ted Fritsch tested the audience with his Footbag trivia quiz.  Winners were 
handsomely rewarded.  Peter started to wrap up the evening until he was 
reminded that there was one more inductee.  We didn?t really forget Jack, we 
fully intended to punk him!!! Rob McCloskey introduced Jack Lentz and others 
filled in details of his footbag influence. Afterwards, the evening shred 
session and social scene continued for hours.

Monday proved for exciting competition in all venues.  It was "Canada vs USA" 
in the doubles pro match that took several turns and either team could have 
taken the title.  You could see and hear the amazement of many new spectators 
watching the action at the net. The skills contests entertained all. People are 
definitely practicing their golf shots! There were some really amazing shots 
made!

The freestylers put on a great show as usual.  David Clavens performed a 
dropless routine to win top honors.  It is always so entertaining to 
watch festival goers passing by and at some point realize what they are 
seeing.  Their shock, excitement and pure amazement is priceless.  Several of 
the routines have already been uploaded to YouTube.

As most of you know, next year, the 25th annual will be the last Funtastik 
Summer Classic Footbag Tournament.  It will be held on Labor Day weekend in 
Harrisburg, PA and the venues will be the same as usual. The Holiday Inn will 
be the host hotel again and the rooms will be $79 plus tax.  The early entry 
fee is $25 and more events will be added to the Master?s division.  

If you have ever been to a Summer Classic Tournament, please make your plans 
now to come and please spread the word to others.  If you have never been to 
this event, 2010 is your chance to be part of its colorful history!  Hope to 
see you!

With over 23 years of tournament history, the Funtastik Summer Classic is the perfect tournament/festival to attend on the east coast to wind down your summer with a super FUN time. This tournament/festival is part of a three day festival in downtown Harrisburg called Kipona, which attracts thousands of people. There is always plenty of circle kicking and four square play for everyone, no matter what age or ability. Total beginners to regining World Champions are at this event. We also invite you to join us at the Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony on Sunday night to honor five of footbag's finest. So come out to watch, compete, learn, hang out, or just to see what the heck is going on. This event could possibly be the highlight of your summer and/or year!
Event Details:
Please note that if you have procrastinated the years away and still have not 
been to the Summer Classic, you have only one more chance after this year to 
attend.

Event Details:

Preregistration guarantees a player's pack.  Can be done by mail, email
or in person to Brenda Solonoski or Funtastik. Payment MUST accompany 
registration form in either check, cash (US dollar only) or credit
card. 

There is a $2 fee for credit card transactions.

FOOTBAG NET- The pool format- 2 out of 3 games to 11 
points. Semi final & final games- 2 out of 3 games to 15 
points changing sides at 8 points. Must win by two points 
in all games. This format is subject to change. 

FOOTBAG FREESTYLE- Time limit Pro- 2 min, Amateurs- 1:30, 
Novice- 1 min. Competitors might be required to judge. Hand 
in your music and introduction card before the event & pick 
it up after your turn. 

FOOTBAG GOLF- One 9 hole round of golf will be played that 
follows the standard footbag golf rules. 

SKILLS CONTESTS- $7 ($10 if not entered in tournament) 
Winner takes all basis. 2007 champions get a bye to finals 

FREESTYLE SHRED- 45 sec. max, most adds win, no choreography, generic music.
Rules- 1. Shred till you drop 2. 3 drops & you are done 3. Cannot do same
trick more than 5 times in a row 4. The top 3 make it to finals Yes, your
routine will be video taped to verify adds.

SERVICE POACHING- This is a two-person event. Preliminary 
rounds- the most blocks win. Final round- highest 
accumulative score from judges win. 1. Service block must 
occur at the net. 2. Blocker must be airborne to count. 3. 
The top 3 make it to finals. 

IRON MAN- The best time wins. Any drop in consecutive or 
toe delays, must restart from the beginning. 1. 100 
consecutive kicks, 10 toe delays, 1 around the world with 
each foot, golf accuracy shots, 1 net service & 1 set & 
spike (airborne contact above net required) 2. Two footbags 
allowed. 3. Body contact is allowed during golf shots. 4. 
The top 3 make it to finals. 

SUPER MAN- Completion of tasks in the best time wins. 40 
adds, 50 consecutives, net serve to gladiator until scoring 
a point, receive gladiator serve until scoring a point & 3 
various golf shots done within 4 mins. 1. Seconds added to 
score equal to distance 2. Any golf shot made cancels out 
distance from longest miss. 3. Net footbag must be used 
during net portion.

We are real excited to be part of the Muscular Dystrophy Telethon during the Kipona festival on finals day. They will be located in the Carosusel Field Pavilion that seats 350 people. Carousel Field will accommodate the finals court and the freestyle finals area. In the past years thousands of spectators have come past this area. We are donating $2 from each entry to MD. You can opt to donate via your entry form or in person. We will also be offering a special event open to the public to raise $. This event is know for its organization, prizes, $$ and fun!!

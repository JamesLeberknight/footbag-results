We are very proud to be sponsored by Legacy Marketing, The Store, and for three years running, Pro Kicker Footbags, the official bag of the Windy City Cup. PLEASE NOTE: PRE-REGISTRATION IS NOW CLOSED. However, don't let that keep you from coming out! There is still plenty of room left in the tournament. To those that did take the time to pre-register, we thank you! For those that didn't, there will be time for registration on Friday evening, June 18th. The Windy City Cup will be using Rally Scoring for the entirety of the event. All singles matches will be one game to 21 (except for the finals, which will be best two out of three). Doubles matches will be best two out of three to 21. Consolation matches will be one game to 21.

25rd IFPA European Footbag Championships 2025 from 06-08. June 2025 in Frankfurt!! The Footbag Club in Frankfurt is happy to host again the IFPA European Championship!

If the weather is good, we will play in Güntherburgpark, Frankfurt

Adress: Güntherburgpark, Wetteraustraße 7, 60389 Frankfurt am Main

On Friday, in case of bad weather, we have to start at the Fitness First Company from 12:00 - 17:00.

Fitness First, Karl-von-Drais-Straße 5a, 60435 Frankfurt am Main

Hall for bad weather on Saturday and Sunday:

IGS Friedrich Ebert, Arolser Str. 11, 60389 Frankfurt am Main

Google Map

We want to offer food for Friday and Saturday.

On Sunday you have to provide your own food, but there are plenty of options nearby. We will also provide free simple breakfasts, fruits and water.
For those who need a coffee in the morning, there are several cafes and bakeries on site and in the surrounding area.

The Texas Footbag League - Austin Style Footbag Club, Houston Skyliners and Dallas Footbag Club present the Annual Texas Footbag Championships. Open to all players. You can get a copy of the flyer at: www.footbag.org/gallery/showset/texas This year we will be at the Back Gate - Barton and Jaycee Fields on Robert E. Lee Road. All Day Saturday and Sunday. Register by sending e-mail to tinalewis@footbag.org. Late Registration on Saturday 10 a.m. at the site. Thanks to our sponsors! Brooks, Prokicker and Flipsider. So far we have some great Flipsider T-shirts for Freestyle prizes (Intermediate and Novice) and a nice variety of footbags (Net and Freestyle) for prizes. Brooks has donated water bottles for player packs (1st 35 entrants) and a gift certificate for a pair of shoes - as a prize for net. (Either open singles or intermediate singles net!). IFPA members can download the flyer and entry form at: http://www.footbag.org/gallery/showset/texas.

Driving Directions to the Park - 
Directions to Back Gate ? Barton and Jaycee Fields ? Zilker Park, Austin ?
Robert E. Lee Road. 

From Loop 1 (MOPAC) Heading South on MoPac South after you pass downtown and
immediately after crossing the river - exit right at the BeeCaves/Rollingwood
exit.  Stay in the left lane and a left at the light and go under the Freeway.
 Stay to the right so you don't have to get back on Loop 1.  Follow that
street into the park.  This is Barton Springs Road.  The road curves around to
the right.  Go through the park to the light at Robert E. Lee Road and turn
right (just past the soccer fields).  

You will pass the Umlauf Sculpture Gardens on the left and the little league
baseball field on the right.  The site: Barton and Jaycee Fields and the Back
Entrance to Barton Springs Pool is just ahead on the right.  The parking lot
entrance is on your right just past the little league field.  Look for footbag
players around the big tree in the middle of the fields.  


From IH 35 ? Take the Riverside Exit and head West (From the South ? San
Antonio, exit and go left over the freeway) (From the North ? Dallas, exit and
go right).  This is East Riverside Drive until you come to Barton Springs Road
? take Barton Springs road (veers left) and follow it to Robert E. Lee Road (a
couple of lights after you cross Lamar Blvd.) and turn left on Robert E. Lee

Road.  [If you miss the intersection at Riverside and Barton Springs you will
probably still be on Riverside (now West Riverside) ? just follow it to Lamar
Blvd. and make a left, then a right on Barton Springs Road.]

You will pass the Umlauf Sculpture Gardens on the left and the little league
baseball field on the right.  The site: Barton and Jaycee Fields and the Back
Entrance to Barton Springs Pool is just ahead on the right.  The parking lot
entrance is on your right just past the first little league field.  e-mail me
and I'll send you a map.  tinalewis@footbag.org

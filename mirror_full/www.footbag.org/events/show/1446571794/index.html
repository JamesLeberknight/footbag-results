FC Footstar's 18th Anniversary
January 29-31, 2016

Founded in January of 1998, FC Footstar Berlin has left puberty behind and finally come of age. Germany's oldest Footbag club is still alive and kicking, and home to many of the sport's greatest players.

As usual, the club's anniversary will be celebrated with that special combination of party and competition. Expect yet another Friday evening sight-seeing excursion, and a smashing Saturday night party. Go ahead and make your travel plans to come to Berlin!

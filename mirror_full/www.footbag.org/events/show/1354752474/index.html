You thought we were done ?!?

Well we are back !!!

The 13e Open de France de Footbag will take place in Paris on 8-9-10 February 2013. This time we will return to the Stade de Coubertin where the 2005 and 2010 editions already took place.

Around 100 footbag players and aficionados from North-& South-America and from all over Europe travelled to Paris in 2011. Thanks to all those players it was the biggest footbag competition that winter season. We will do our best to make the 13e Open de France de Footbag that big again. Don?t miss your chance to be part of it !!!

More details will be announced later.

Recap: What: 13e Open de France de Footbag, When: 8-9-10 February 2013, Where: Paris, France.

Make it real !

Freestyle & Doubles Net - all matches are played on Saturday*
09:00 Breakfast
10:00 Warm-up
10:45 Player´s meeting Doubles Net
11:00 zero serving zero
12:00 Player´s meeting Freestyle
18:30 Freestyle Finals
19:30 Doubles Net Finals

Sunday
Freestyle & Mixed Doubles Net - all matches are played on Sunday
10:00 Breakfast
11:00 Warm-up
11:45 Player´s meeting Doubles Net
12:00 zero serving zero
14:00 Player´s meeting Freestyle
15:00 *3rd Place Open Doubles Net
16:00 Mixed Doubles Net Finals
17:00 Awards on Closing Ceremonies

Friday:
- Singles Net (rally scoring)
- No Freestyle

Saturday:
- Doubles Net (side-out scoring)
- Freestyle (Routines & Shred 30)

- Mixed Doubles Net (side-out scoring)
- Freestyle (Circle Contest & Request)

Once again, footbag-vienna hosts the Austrian Footbag Championships. It is an open event, so foreign players are more than welcome. Given that enough players register, Intermediate and Open competition will be held, both in circle and battle. Accommodation will be provided on first-come, first-serve basis, we try to accommodate everybody for free. Happening right before Christmas, it will be Kickmas-Jam-Style, so prepare for some serious 4-Square action and Punsch!

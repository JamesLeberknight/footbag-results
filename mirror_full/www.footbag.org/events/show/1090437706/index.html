We're proud to announce that 3rd edition of our annual tournament called Zocha Jam is approaching quickly. This year we want to suprise all of the players and audience by organizeing many interesting contests. Where you can win prizes like two flight tickets from Sky Eurpe air lines(to avail till the end of the year) and lots of more. You can expect special guest from Flipsider Ellis Piltz(Canada) also Human beatbox, breakedance and yoyo exhibitions which will put us in good mood and emphasizes creative style of Zoch Jam. We also want to satisfy all fans of good music. Look forward to unforgetable entertainment and sport event. You just can't miss Zocha Jam 04!

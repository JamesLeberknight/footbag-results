Please take care of your reservations asap as this is a very busy time in Montreal. ACT NOW!!!!!!!!!!

As you can see the dates are set for july 26th to aug 1st now whereas it was written july 25th to aug 1st before. The actual tournament start on monday the 26th but the check-in will be on july 25th.

We will have very interesting finals sites next summer. The net will be at Jeanne-Mance park for 3 days featuring the quarter finals, semis and finals on a 750 seated places mini stadium. The freestyle will see it's finals happen at the Rialto theater, which is a great venue, seating 1200 persons.

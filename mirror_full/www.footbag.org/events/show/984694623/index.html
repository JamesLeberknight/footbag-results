MONTREAL SUMMER CHALLENGE

The Montreal Summer Challenge will be the biggest freestyle tourney ever held north of the border - besides Worlds of course. The site is the internationnally renowned bar Les Foufounes Electriques right downtown Montreal. The tournament will be held late afternoon on Friday and Saturday. We'll hold the usual Tam-Tam-Jam all day Sunday.

A lot of stars from the footbag community have confirmed they will attend unless they die or something really bad happens. The list includes:

Sebastien Duchesne --- 2nd place open - Windchill 2001
Scott Bevier --- 3rd place open - ECFC 2001
Samuel Jobin --- 1st place intermediate - CSS2
Kavin Thiffault --- 3rd place intermediate - CSS2
Kaiser Amhad --- Footbag legend!
Yacine Merzouk --- BAP 2001
Eli Piltz --- BAP 1998
Chad Devlahovich --- BAP 1997

ENTRY FEES

Intermediate: 20$
The player's pack includes T-shirt, Map of Montreal and bus tickets for competitors from out-of-town.
Open: 50$
The player's pack includes T-shirt, Map of Montreal and bus tickets for competitors from out-of-town + possibility of winning prize money.

LE CHALLENGE ESTIVAL DE MONTRÉAL

Le 1er Tournoi Estival de Montréal sera le plus grand événement de footbag-freestyle jamais organisé au Canada, mis à part les Championnats du Monde. La compétition aura lieu aux Foufounes Électriques les vendredi et samedi. Le dimanche sera réservé pour les Tam-Tams.

Plusieurs professionnels ont confirmé leur présence pour le Challenge dont:

HOST HOTEL INFORMATION

Our Host Hotel for the 1rst annual CEM is "L'appartement Hotel". It's so 
close to the tournament site that you can actually crawl from one place to 
the other. They offer appartments instead of just rooms. Each one has a 
kitchen, dining room, living and a bedroom with a bed for 2. There is a 
hide-a-bed couch in the living room. We also have rooms with 2 separate 
bedrooms + hide-a-bed. 

The rooms are just HUGE and ultra clean. And I checked out the TVs to 
make sure you can plug your video camera in them. Not all hotels allow 
that. 

1 bedroom = 134$CAN / night (up to 4 people)

2 bedrooms= 184$CAN / night (up to 6 people) 

The registration Deadline is June 15th. You need a credit card to make 
reservations. It's important to mention that you are taking the room for the 
footbag tournament otherwise you will not get the rates listed above. Just 
mention *foobag* to save a few $ ! The toll free number is listed below. 

L'APPARTEMENT HOTEL

Montreal, Qc, CAnada

HOST RESTAURANT INFO

We tried really hard to find a restaurant that had some freestyle move in 
its name.  So our host restaurant this year is "Le Café Barroque".  We 
have a whole floor of the restaurant to ourselves.  Everybody gets a 10% 
discount on their bill.  And last but not least there is a vegetarian as well 
as a vegan menu.  Oh! And it's less than 5 minutes walking from the site 
or the hotel!

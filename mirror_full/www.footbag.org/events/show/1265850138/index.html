The Sole Purpose Footbag Group is proud to announce the 2010 US Open Freestyle Footbag Championships. This is a long running tournament/festival dedicated to promoting the sport of freestyle footbag. This is one of the largest footbag events in North America, held in the birthplace of footbag, the Portland Oregon area. We invite everyone to come visit the home of footbag, and see the US Champion get crowned! Also attached to this event is the annual Water Temple Jam in downtown Portland.
Event Details:
The event will take place at the Nautilus World Headquarters in Vancouver WA.

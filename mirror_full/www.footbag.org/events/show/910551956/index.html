Manually Entered Results

1999 Western Regional Footbag Championships
Presented by Steve Goldberg
May 29-31, 1999
Stanford University, Palo Alto, California (USA)

Participants:
89 open & intermediate competitors
24 novice competitors

Sponsors and Benefactors:
Steve Goldberg, david Butcher, Infinity Toys, Wham-O, Albion Water,
Brent & Jody Welch, and Stanford University

Staff Members:
Zebulon Jones, Avery McCombs, Stephanie Balagot, Brent Welch, david Butcher, 
Jody Welch, Patti Caveney, Lonya Julin, Brian Kimball, Jim Fitzgerald, Kevin 
Fine, Deborah Low, Jimmy Caveney, Hung Chang, Jason Davis, Nitin Chopra, Lisa 
McDaniel, Jboy Gran, Will Guyette, PT Lovern, and other friends and members of 
the Bay Area Footbag League and Stanford Footbag Club

Awards:
Intermediate events: 1st-3rd place were awarded medals (gold, silver, bronze)
Novice events: 1st place was awarded gold medals, 1st-3rd were awarded
               prizes as well as certificates;
Open events: 1st-2nd place were awarded cash (total purse: approx. $1300),
             1st-3rd place were awarded medals (gold, silver, bronze)

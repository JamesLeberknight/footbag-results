Event Details:
Friday - We will check in at the CIC Headquarters in La Grange and begin 
shredding around 12:00-12:30.  The novice circle shred contest (start 
with circles of four, the two best from each circle will be put into 1 or 2 
more circles depending on the number of competitors, then the two best 
from the next circle(s) will do a 30 second shred contest.) will begin at 
4:00, followed by intermediate routines at 4:30, and then open routines at 
5:30.  After routines we will have a players dinner (cookout and pizza) and 
party at the CIC headquarters.   We will shred some more at a late night 
jam. (11:00 pm approx.) (Phatest two trick combo wins prize).

Saturday - An opportunity has come up and the second day will now be at 
The Museum of Science and Industry in Chicago (free admission and 
parking!) http://msichicago.org/temp_exhibit/sport/index.html.  We will be 
carpooling there from La Grange.  Set up and registration will be at 10:00 
am.  At 11:00 there will be a general footbag demo.  At 11:30 there will be 
a freestyle demo featuring 1999 World Freestyle Champion Scott 
Davidson.  Following the freestyle demo will be a consecutive demo 
featuring Guinness World Record Holder Ted Martin.  At 12:30 there will 
be a net demo featuring top net  players from the Midwest.  Following the 
demos there will be a 30second shred contest for intermediates and pros
(1:00).  We will cycle through twice in the same random order and only 
one of your performances will be judged (your best one).  After the shred 
contest (approx. 2:00), while judging is taking place, the top two finishers 
for routines from intermediate and pro will perform their routines as a 
demo.  When the routines demo is finished (2:15) we will announce 
winners and hand out awards for both days.  3:00 clean up!

*EVERYONE is welcome to stay at the CIC Headquarters.  Hotels are not 
a worry!  Contact Dan for directions (708)-785-2530.

*There will be prizes for the top intermediate finishers of Routines and 45 
second shred.

*Complimentary food and drinks will be served throughout the event.

Please let me know if you can make it, I hope to see you all there!!!

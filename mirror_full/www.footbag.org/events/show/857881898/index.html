The Summer Classic is the home of Service Poaching, Iron Man and Freestyle Shred competitions. The prize $$ are always one of the highest paid out and the merchandise prizes can not be beat. This event is held during a very large 3 day festival. There is plenty of fun, food, rides, games, swimming, fireworks, etc for all ages. Info and entry forms are now available.

RNH Contest 2024

Gymnase Léo Lagrange
68 boulevard Poniatowski
75012 Paris


Doubles Net - Saturday 10h-20h

We will use the Finnish hat drawing system to define the teams. It’s
pretty cool ! We already used at the last tournament in Nantes.

All players will be distributed in 4 hats and the teams will be drawn 
randomly.

We will play all pool matches using the rally-scoring system 1 game to
21 points.

Then we will use the same hats and randomly draw new teams for the 2nd
round pool matches.

All points, victories and defeats will be counted for the final
rankings after the pool matches.

The 2 players who had most wins and points will be the 1st seeded team
for the final bracket. The 2nd seeded team will be the players who had
the third and fourth most wins and points. The 3rd seeded team will be
the players who had the 5th and 6th most wins and points … In case the
ranking would set a team that already played together in pool play, we
will make a drawing to ensure that all teams for the final bracket are
new teams.

Bracket matches will be played rally scoring best of 3 games to 15 points.

During the afternoon we will take a break for a footbag discovery
workshop for the kids from the local football club.


Singles Net - Sunday 12h-18h

We will use the hat drawing system to distribute players into pools.

Pool matches will be played rally scoring best of 3 games to 15 points.

Depending on time and energy we might play as of semi-finals best of 5
games to 15 points.

Please contact me for any questions.

See you soon !!

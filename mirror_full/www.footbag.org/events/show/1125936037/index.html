After last year?s success the 7e OPEN DE FRANCE DE FOOTBAG will be hosted in Paris for the second year in a row. Last year more than 75 players from 15 nations participated at the biggest annual footbag event in France. And this year we expect even more players ! Over the years the OPEN DE FRANCE has established itself among the top European Freestyle and Net competitions. European Champion Flo Goetze and Hannes Daniel won Doubles Net and World Champion Vasek Klouda won Singles and Doubles Freestyle. This will be again one of the top footbag events this season. Make sure that you don?t miss it. Pre-registration soon on www.footbag.org

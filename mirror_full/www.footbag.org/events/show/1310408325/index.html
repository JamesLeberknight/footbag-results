Manually Entered Results

What a great weekend of Footbag fun in Vancouver. The sun was shining all
day long Saturday and there were a few clouds in the sky and a breeze on
Sunday. Saturday saw singles and mixed doubles played down to but not
including) the finals. A couple of things of note, for the first time ever
an Albertan made a podium finish in a net event in Vancouver. Yes, the
illustrious and almost indomitable Pat Quinn made it to the semis in
singles. He realized a lifelong dream in getting to play Kenny Shults in the
semis. Though Pat scored more than a few points in each of the two games he
found himself playing for third place shortly after that. Congratulations
Pat, we're all proud of you and what you accomplished this year. The stage
was set for a singles final with 6 time champion, Chris Siebert, and Kenny
Shults ready to do battle. Mixed doubles ended up with Evanne Lamarche/Andy
Ronald vs. Leanne Makcrow/David Bernard ready to square off on Sunday as
well. The day turned into evening and we convened at Leanne's house for a
BBQ and a few beers donated by the Spring Brewing Company.

Sunday started off with the seeding of our 9 doubles teams. All in all, we
had 20 participants this year. After some hotly contested pool play, quarter
and semi finals, the stage was set for a finals match between the 6 time
champions Steve Dusablon/Chris Siebert and Dave Bernard/Kenny Schults. In
the end, one streak was broken and another remains to be challenged for next
year at our 25th Annual Event. See below for the details of how it all
played out.

Thank you everyone for your participation and support this year and all the
years past. We'll see you all next summer.

If you're looking for some good quality competition you simply can't miss the Vancouver Open. 'Conan' - Chris Siebert and Steve Dusablon have been dominating our open the last couple of years along with the random showings of 'Dr. Flyhigh' - Dave Bernard but we've got local talent Jim Derricott, Andy Ronald, Ian Kobayashi, (just to name a few) with up and comers Lawrence Dumont and Marek Sobczyk who are sure to change that! Come for great competition and some tongue and cheek fun! And,... if you're an avid golfer, Geoff Hand and Jim Derricott always set you up right! Come show your skills - we can't wait to see you!

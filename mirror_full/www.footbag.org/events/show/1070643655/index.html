Hello baby,

I'm sorry that I forgot to send you the info about the first outdoor footbag event of the season in Europe. I promise it will never happen again. 30th April, 1st & 2nd May.

Yes, it's true that it's the third time in a row and it's getting bigger every year. The party rocks, and the crowd goes crazy when they see the surfers and kite surfers shredding the waves for the titels of the WorldCup. They also go wild while the Beachvolleyball ProTour!

Anyway, the Footbag-area is a lot bigger, because the organizers (footbag- vienna)decided to join forces with the Flying Disc (Frisbee) Crew, and are also planing a Disc Freestyle competition.
Ohhh!! They have also decided to have a Footbag Net tournament there. That'S soooo cool ! I heard some hardcore players from Frankfurt&Switzerland will attend.

The freestyle will be similar to the last years. "Simply the best" (thanx Tina T.) will be there.

Well I don't want to tell you everything, but I found this awesome website where you can find out everything about the Footbag & Disc Opening 2004

www.feelthespirit.net

Bytheway baby! If you want to come along and don't feel like playing, make sure to register anyway, since the entry fee will get loads cheaper.

Take care and don't hop around to much this weekend,
your boyfriend

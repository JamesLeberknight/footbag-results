This event is run during the Kipona Street Fair in Harrisburg. It is a huge event that draws hundred of thousands of people. The Summer Classic is known for lots of fun and great cash and merchandise prizes. This year the host hotel is the Harrisburg East Holiday Inn. It is a mini resort offering the max in fun and accomodations. Plenty on room for circles under the stars or under the monster canopy by the super oversized outdoor pool or on the lighted basketball and tennis courts, 15 person hot tub, indoor exercise room and sauna, lighted chip and putt course (great for footbag golf) and room for 2 net courts near the pool. A single, double, triple or quad room is $72 plus taxes. That is half price the normal fee. Call 717-939-7841 and say you are with Funtastik Summer Classic. DEADLINE FOR THIS RATE IS 8/13/99, so do not delay. For the campers, call 717-939-4331. Bring your family and friends. There is fun for everyo

ENTRY FEES- Pro- $55 (those wanting to win cash, add $5/event(
         Am. Intermed- $35   Am. Novice-  $28   Skills contest- $2/event
          Open Women- $35   Best bag golf- $15/team (winner takes all)
The first 60 players receive a tournament packet.  The Pro-$55 + $5/event, 
Women's, skills & best bag receive $ prizes.  The Pro $55, Am int and Am nov 
receive merchandise prizes.  Anyone can register for the skill contests & best 
bag golf.  Best bag golf is limited to the first nine 3 man teams registered.  
Team freestyle competition groups Pro & Am team together.  Am freestyle & Am 
golf are inclusive of Int & Novice freestyle and golf respectively.  Net will 
be run in pool format.  Pro freestyle is 2 min, Am is 1:30 and team is 3 min.    

  Freestyle shred is 45 sec. max, the most adds win, no choreography, no 
music,
 3 drops & you are done, can't do same trick more than 5 times in a row,
 top 3 make it to finals.  Routines are video taped. 
  Service poaching-2 person event.  10 serves, thus 10 block attempts, block 
must occur at net, blocker must be air borne to count,
 top 3 make finals.
  Iron man- best time wins.  Any drop in consec. or toe delays starts from 
beginning, 1)  100 consec kicks, 10 toe delays, 1 around the world with each 
foot,
 2 golf accuracy shots, 1 net service, 1 set & spike. Same footbag used. Top 3 
make it to finals.

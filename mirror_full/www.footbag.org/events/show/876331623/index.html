OOPS! FOOTBAG CLUB PRESENTS...

The 4th Annual Southern California Footbag Championships!

Watch as some of the world's best pull the hugest tricks and catch the biggest air.

Located at Hermosa Valley Park on Valley and Gould, in Hermosa Beach, California.

Sat and Sun April 4th and 5th 10:AM - 5:00PM

Event Details:
SCHEDULE OF EVENTS:

Saturday, April 4th
9:00am - 10:00am ---------------- Registration 
10:00am ------------------------- Players Meeting
11:00am ------------------------- Ruler of the court (first two rounds)
12:00pm ------------------------- 45-second shred (no drop limit, one round)
1:00pm -------------------------- Lunch
1:30pm -------------------------- Singles Freestyle, Intermediate and open (first round)
2:30pm -------------------------- Ruler of the court (final three rounds)

Sunday, April 5th
9:00am -------------------------- Mixed Doubles net. (first two rounds)

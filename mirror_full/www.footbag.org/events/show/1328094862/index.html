Event Details:


PLEASE SEE DETAILED DESCRIPTION ON 
http://footbag-aachen.de/

Date: July 13-15 2012

Check in on thursday will be at the "Kuckucksnest" 


its 2mins from the hostel and 2 mins from westpark where the welcome shred/net 
will be
see on map: 
http://goo.gl/maps/e6MV
on friday check in will be at the tournament site.

Tournament Site: We secured a terrific spot just outside Aachen (10mins bus
ride) with two large Sport-halls and 2 Soccer fields. 


Lodging:
1. PONTTOR (12eur)
We have secured floor space for 40 Persons directly at the main spot for this
event.
-close to the event-site-bus
-close to the main party area of Aachen, tons of Bars and all the nightlife.
-close to some supermarkets and loads of food-places.
2. HOSTEL (59 eur)
We have reserved 20 Places in a neat Hostel with community-kitchen and
internet and such. Some places here will be reserved for Tournament Staff as
Net/Freestyle Directors, Judges and Helpers (Apply NOW for any of those
positions and secure a hostel-place!)
3. HOTEL (50 eur /night for 2 person room)
We have negotiated an event-deal for a proper Hotel (Ibis) for as low as 25.-
EUR per night and Person. This really is the cheapest you can find during this
season all over Aachen. 

Party:
Fridays Party will be in the Apollo Cinema with 2 Dancefloors and
Shredspace/Mininet.
Also Screening of Footbag videos (send in your Vids and see them on the big
screen)
Saturdays Party will be at Jakobshof where the 2007 Freestyle Finals Show of
the german Champs took place.
A Freestyle show on Stage with Sponsor-Prizes is planned too.

Routes to Aachen:
By car Aachen is about 45mins from Cologne/Düsseldorf, 2.5hrs from Frankfurt,
3,5hrs from Paris, 1.5hrs from Brussels.
Closest Airport is Maastricht-Aachen Airport (MAA) but thats really small.
Other good options are Cologne (CGN), Düsseldorf (DUS), Weeze (NRN), Dortmund
(DTM) with many cheap airlines to/from all destinations.
By Train: Deutsche Bahn has always some specials, group-tickets, weekend
deals. We will investigate on that at a given time.

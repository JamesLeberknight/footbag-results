Director ? Eric Wulff. Some Highlights... Yves Archambault continues to dominate the east coast net scene with yet another sweep of open net titles. This time, even with a couple up and coming west coast powers in Chris Donofrio and Bart trying to de-throne him. Seven of the top 9 people in open net are from the Montreal Dynasty. This includes, of course, both open titles... again. Perhaps the most fitting nickname in all sport, the 'Executioner' Peter Irish executes another near flawless routine in the championship round to win his 2nd ECFC title in a row. Statements such as 'living legend' and 'one of the all time greats' are beginning to be associated with Peter even at this early stage in his career.

Events Offered: 
Freestyle, 4-square, Juggling. Some competitions for fun: 
shred 30, sick 3, sick 1, 4-square. No prizes unless 
sponsors donate some.
The Cal Poly Footbag Club and the Cal Poly Juggling Club is hosting a small freestyle jam at Cal Poly University on November 22. Music will be provided by Tech Yes (Sam Colclough).

Confirmed attendees include Eric Windsor, Chris Pinkus, Sam Colclough, and Chad Devlahovich.
Event Details:
TIME: Starting at 12pm (the room is reserved until 6pm)
      Competition will start at 1pm

PLACE: Cal Poly University in the University Union Room 220 

DIRECTIONS: 
(Maps can be found at http://footbag.calpoly.edu)

From Northern California: 

-Take US-101 South 
-Exit Monterey 
-Turn right then immediately turn left onto Loomis 
-Turn right onto Grand Avenue. 

From Southern California: 

-Take US-101 North 
-Exit Grand Avenue/Cal Poly 
-Turn Left onto Grand Avenue

PARKING: 

There is a parking structure on the left as you approach the school, just 
before the Performing Arts Center (big shiny building). 
No parking permit is required during the event. 

PLACE TO STAY:

Since San Luis Obispo is about 3-4 hours from San Francisco and Los Angeles, 
some people will just be driving home after the event. If anyone would like 
some floorspace to crash on let me know ahead of time. If you'd like to stay 
at a hotel, contact me and I'll find some information for you.

Let me know if you are attending. You can reach me at 805 786-4842 or 
digitalaudiotape@sbcglobal.net.

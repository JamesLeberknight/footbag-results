Turku Open is the official prequel to the 32nd IFPA World Footbag Championships in Helsinki just one week after Turku Open. Turku Open is the longest running all net tournament in Europe. Turku Open will be played under similar conditions as Worlds. Turku Open will be played indoors on a sports carpet with rally scoring. So no need to worry about twisting your ankle playing on wet grass. Come to Finland a week before Worlds to settle in on the scandinavian way of life. If you're planning on coming to Turku Open from abroad, please contact Matti for more details.
Event Details:
Turku is the official European Capital of Culture in 2011. That means there's

On January 12th 2005, one of Europe's oldest and biggest footbag clubs, FC Footstar Berlin e.V. will turn 7 years old. We will celebrate this birthday on the weekend of january 15 - 16. On both days, there will be some serious shred and net action at a gym located in central Berlin, at the Margarethe-von-Witzleben school in Berlin-Friedrichshain, Palisadenstr. 76-78..

Our annual birthday celebrations have become quite famous over the last years, and so will our 2005 party ... 5000 flyers are distributed all over Berlin ...
On Saturday evening we will have the YAAM winter location: a freestyle team battle will take place, footbag videos are going to be shown on several screens and DJs will play funky tunes from reggae, dancehall and hiphop to drum 'n bass.

we have private accomodation available for a limited number of people - first come first serve

stay tuned for more information or write kontakt@fcfootstar.de

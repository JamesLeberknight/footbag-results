Welcome back to Portland, Oregon for one of the premier footbag net events in the United States!
Event Details:
We offer the widest variety of competitive footbag net play for pros,
intermediates, and beginners alike.  Our Women's events are the biggest
around, so ladies come on out!

WIDMER BREWING has been our beer sponsor for the last 9 years in a row! 
Thanks WIDMER!!  Big thanks also to sponsors Fancy Footwork Productions,
Nomad's Crossing, World Footbag Association, Flying Clipper Footbags, Tim's
Cascade Snacks, and Footbag Hall of Fame.

Fun, friends, and good times in a beautiful riverside park.  Footbag was born
in Oregon in 1972.  Experience footbag in Great Northwest.  Flat playing
fields right on the river and close to downtown.  Grocery and shopping center
one block away. Easily the most convenient site on the footbag tour!

Entry Fees:

Open:  $50
Women's Open:  $40 (special rate!)
Intermediate:  $30
Athletic Supporters:  $20
Beginner:  FREE Foot-C play and Net Instruction

Events Offered: 
Net Play, Freestyle Circles, and 2-square
The Michigan Footbag Club is delighted to announce New Years Net/Freestyle Footbag Jam this December 29th 2017 beginning 8pm in Novi Michigan! Come and enjoy all the traditional New Years pastimes, like Whirlyball whom have been kind enough to donate court times and an open bar for the night, and New Years fireworks with BBQ and lots and lots of footbag. Plus, we've got brand new and exciting attractions like Adam Kieth's Casa de Kick and, for the first time ever in Detroit, a footbag net court! Saturday and Sunday will take place at the official event site (The Jam Handy) where we will hold freestyle and net competitions, serve light lunch, 4- square competitions, and all the sideline shred you can handle!!! Come stay for a while! For those who want to come early and/or stay late, Friday and Monday are open for just hanging out and kicking at club members' houses/apartments. Don't hesitate to ask for crash space. We've got plenty! All are welcome. Even if you have little or no experience with footbag/hackysack, come see what it's all about and maybe learn some new tricks. So come on over to our fine city and help us kick off the new year!

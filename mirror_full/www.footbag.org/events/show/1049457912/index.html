You are invited to the 20th Annual East Coast Championships, June 28-29th in Laurel, MD. We are working hard to make sure this will be a worthy celebration of 20 years of footbag on the east.

The founder of the tournament, footbag hall of famer Rick Kaufman will be joining us. Additional footbag legends attending include Eric Wulff, Joe and Brenda Solonoski, Bob Nicholson, Tuan Vu, Flash Kingsley, Ted Fritsch, Steve "4Square" Goldberg, Scott Davidson, Carol Wedemeayer, and the 4 time east coast net champion Steve Femmel.

The East Coast Championships is the second longest running tournament in footbag history and this year will be the biggest. We will be offering prize money for pros and awesome prizes for intermediates. Also, there will be plenty of non-competitive kicking with 4-square and pin-bag throughout the event.

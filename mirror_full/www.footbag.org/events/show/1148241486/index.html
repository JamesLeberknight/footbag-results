Come one...come all to the Steel City for some serious (and a bit of the non- serious) footbag shred and kicking. This event promises to leave eyes and mouthes wide open as the first footbag event in Pittsburgh stuns the public.
Event Details:
The event will be held at the William Pitt Union ballroom, this building is the student union for the University of Pittsburgh. It's located at the corner of Forbes ave. and Bigelow Blvd., and is just across the Blvd from the Cathedral of Learning (one of the tallest and most eye-catching structures in the city). This location is in the heart of Oakland, the cultural center of Pittsburgh displaying an excellent atmosphere and fun sites to see.

Mapquest directions work fine to get to the venue and site. City navigation is minimal because the site is about a 5 minute walk from the hotel and is directly between both main streets of Pittsburgh (Forbes and 5th Ave.). Also, meter parking is free past 6PM on Friday and for the entire weekend. So don't get suckered into finding a parking garage. Stick to the streets.

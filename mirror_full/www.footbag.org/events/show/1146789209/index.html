Directions:
take the Washington Beltway exit 25b for Route 1 South towards College 
Park
Maryland ( Baltimore Boulevard ).

to get to campus and the jugglers gym from the beltway exit, stay on 
route 1 for about two miles.  Ritchie Coliseum and Fraternity Row Field 
are both on the left soon after you pass a Jerry's Sub Shop on the right.  
Parking by the Armory is to the right at the 2nd light past the Jerry's 
(first light after the main entrance into campus).

to get to the washington beltway, route 495.  
from the northeast, take 95 south.  
from the northwest take 70 east/southeast to 270 south, 
from straight north, take 83 south to 695 to 95 to 495, from the east or west 
take route 50, if 95 or 70 won't work.

from 95 north of baltimore, as you near 495, the beltway splits in two- 
the
right lanes head towards the north side of the beltway, the left two lanes
continue as 95 south on the south side of the beltway.  you will want to 
take
the RIGHT LANES.

take the left-most RIGHT LANE- as the four lanes split, a left hand exit 
will
open up from the right two lanes for Route 1 South to College Park.  
Take this
exit to get on route 1.

from anywhere else, just look for exit 25b, route 1 south, from 495.  It is a
shorter route from all northern points ( including 50 east and 495) to 
take
the north and east sides of the beltway, though if its rush hour, the 
quickest
routes are inexplicable and probably through the city.  if you are driving in
on 95 north, flip a coin, but pay attention to traffic reports.  the beltway
can get real ugly.

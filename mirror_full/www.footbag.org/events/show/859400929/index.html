The 1997 Montreal International Footbag Championship brought together
47 kickers from as far as Vancouver, BC, Arizona, Texas and the 
San Francisco Bay area. Sunday's finals were held at La Ronde, Montreal's
biggest amusement park, just inside the front gates. What a site!  Sunday was
warm and sunny, and thousands and thousands of Montrealers got a taste of
some great kicking. The Montreal tournament is a great example of bringing the
sport to the people. Here' what happened:.....

This year the Montreal Championships will be held on the same site as last year's World Championships. Saturday net preliminaries will be at "La Plaine des Jeux", a big park on Ste-Helene island near downtown, and finals sunday afternoon at La Ronde (amusement park). The first round of freestyle will be held inside a circus tent at La Ronde on saturday evening. On finals day, net and freestyle will take place on the same turf (synthetic surface over pavement), which means freestyle will be in open air. It will be much easier to draw the crowd that way. If the weather cooperates, the place will be packed.
Event Details:
We don't have information about hotels, but if you need a place to stay just come
to Emmanuel Bouchard's apartment on friday evening  July 18, from there we'll
spread visitors around with the local players.

Directions:
From US through NY state - highway 87
After the border the highway name changes to 15. After 40 minutes follow
signs to 20 est or 132 est (same road). Take" Pont Jacques Cartier (bridge)" 
direction Montreal. After the bridge you will be on Delorimier street, 
after 5 or 6 blocks turn left on Sherbrooke street 
then turn right on St-Andre. 3677 St-Andre

From Ontario through 401
take the highway 40 est
(once in Montreal Hwy 40 is also called the "Metropolitan"). 
Take the exit St-Laurent. Once on the service road (Cremazie street)
turn right on St-laurent street. After about 10 blocks 
you will switch to Clark street (parallel to St-Laurent). 
Go all the way down and turn left on Sherbrooke street 
and after about 15 blocks turn left at St-Andre.

Emmanuel's apartment is at 3677 St-Andre  514-523-8454

p.s. If you know you're going to arrive after 12h.pm friday night, 
please call in advance so we can make arrangements

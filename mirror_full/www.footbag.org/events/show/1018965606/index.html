Events Offered: 
Net, Freestyle, Consecutives & Golf
Many events will be held at the site of Taste Addison at 15650 Addison Road. See Location Addresses below. This is an IFPA event and all OPEN competitors will receive IFPA membership with their entry - sponsored by the TEXAS FOOTBAG LEAGUE. Entry is free for Novice. Entry to Taste Addisson is also free for competitors. Check at registration at the net site for parking info for Taste Addision. Contact James Roberts for more info. You can also e-mail tinalewis@footbag.org to get e-mails with updates.

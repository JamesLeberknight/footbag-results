The 7th annual Hackrifice Open is being held in Winnipeg, the 3rd and 4th of September, 2005. The Hackrifice Footbag Club invites players of all skill levels to come out and enjoy a day full of footbag, live music, prizes and lots of FUN! Check www.hackrifice.com for updates!
Event Details:
Note: Open level players are eligable for Cash prizes.
The following events will be run:
Intermediate/ Open Shred 30, Sick 3, Sick 1, Most Rippin Run, Singles Net, Doubles Net


Schedule:
Friday, 2nd, Sept 2005: Out of town players arrive at their leisure.
Welcome To Winnipeg informal shred (venue TBA) 8:00pm-11:00pm.
Late night snack at a local restaurant (TBA).
Out of town players will be hosted at the houses of hackrifice club members.

Saturday, 3rd, Sept 2005: Start of competition at River Osbourn CC.
Farside Productions DJ Polly and Dj Domian will be spinning breaks all day!
All net events, Shred 30, Sick 3, Sick 1. 11:00am-12:30pm warm-up/sign-up for events.
12:30pm, shred 30 list announced, singles net pools announced.
1:00pm, competitions start.
Begin to leave the venue @ 5:00pm.
Special Dinner for visiting players, put on by the Hackrifice crew.
After Dinner we will all be bowling at the Academy Uptown Lanes (open to all ages) @ 10:00
pm- 1:00am. 

Sunday, 4th, Sept 2005: End of Freestlye events, and Net events if needed.
11:00am-1:00pm warm-up. 1:00pm, events start.
After all events have finished, Prizes will be awarded and will be followed by informal
shred.

Thanks to our Sponsors: Hempyrean, Farside Productions, Kustom Kulture, Urban Bakery,
Academy Uptown Lanes.

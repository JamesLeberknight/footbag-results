GENERAL INFORMATION

WHO: Anyone interested in kicking
WHAT: The Cinco de Mayo Footbag Fest
WHEN: May 5th 2002
            Registration 10am-12noon
            Kicking and Events 12noon-5pm
WHERE: The Pavilion @ Pendleton King Park
              Troup St. Augusta, Georgia.

Augusta Footbag invites you to take part in the Cinco de Mayo Footbag Fest. New players will be exposed to advanced footbag freestyle, as well as having the opportunity to kick with a variety of players from novice to professional.

The venue is the large covered pavilion located in Pendleton King Park. We will have access to our own restrooms and our own kitchen.  Pendleton King Park boasts a world class disc golf course. Please feel free to bring your discs, and go for a throw between kicking if you would like.

Augusta Footbag will have footbags available for purchase as well as T-Shirts and Stickers.  All proceeds go to the Augusta Footbag East Coast Championship Fund.

EVENTS,(REGISTRATION FEES):

Novice,($10): Consecutives, 4 Square
Intermediate,($20): Consecutives, 4 square, big 3, shred 30
Open,($35): Consecutives, 4 square, big 3, shred 30

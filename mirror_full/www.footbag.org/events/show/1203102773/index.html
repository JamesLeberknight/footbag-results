The Net players of the greater Chicago-Land area are proud to formally announce and cordially invite YOU to the "The 2008 Windy City Cup" Net Footbag Championships, to be held June 13-15th in the beautiful city of Chicago, IL. Our event will feature competition for all levels; amateur, professional, and women net events will be offered. Check the Inner Rectangle blog for more about what's happening with the Chicago Lake Coast Kickers.
Event Details:
Schedule of Events (Tentative)

12:00 PM - 6:00 PM  Pick-up games
6:00 PM - 8:30 PM   Larry Doyle Memorial Golf events
8:30 PM - 12:00 AM  Party at Tom's Place (Address TBA onsite)

Saturday, June 14 at Union Park
9:00 AM - 9:45 AM  Late Registration
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM  Singles Net Single-Elimination Bracket (Through semi-finals)
1:00 PM - 8:00 PM  Doubles Net Pools and Brackets (Through quarter-finals)
8:00 PM - 12:00 AM  Players Party at The Store, 2002 N Halsted St.

Sunday, June 15 at Navy Pier (See www.navypier.com for more info.)
11:00 AM - 1:30 PM  Doubles Net semi-finals
1:30 PM - 3:00 PM  Doubles Net 3rd place match
3:00 PM - 4:30 PM  Doubles Net Finals
4:30 PM - 6:00 PM  Singles Net Finals
7:00 PM - 9:00 PM  Awarding of the Windy City Cup and players party at Joe's BeBop Cafe at

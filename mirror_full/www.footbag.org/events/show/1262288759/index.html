Back for another year...the tournament of legends...in the birthplace of footbag Portland, Oregon.
Event Details:
We always offer competitive net play for pros, intermediates, and beginners. 
We are also known for our women's participation, so ladies come on out!

Fun, friends, and good times in a beautiful riverside park.  Experience
footbag in the Great Northwest.  Dates are final.  Make your plans now.  You
haven't experienced a footbag summer until you've been to the Northwest! 
Everyone welcome.

Beautiful waterfront park has been reserved.  Flat playing fields right on the
river and close to downtown.  Grocery and shopping center one block away. 
Easily the most convenient site on the footbag tour!

Entry Fees:

Open:  $50
Women's Open:  $40 (special rate!)
Intermediate:  $30
Athletic Supporters:  $20
Novice:  FREE Foot-C play

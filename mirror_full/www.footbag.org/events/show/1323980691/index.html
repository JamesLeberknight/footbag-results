Memphis Footworks is happy to announce the 2012 Mud Island Belt Footbag Net Tournament. It is to be held on April 14-15 on Mud Island in Memphis, Tennessee. As usual we will have pick up games on Friday the 13th and possibly Thursday as well. As most of you know, this is the most affordable tournament anywhere. We accommodate a free place to stay, almost every (awesome) meal, rides to and from site, t-shirts, beer, PRIZE MONEY, a trophy belt for both doubles winners(you keep for a year), and still do it for $40 entry fee. No other tournament can say that. Gotta love Southern hospitality and great sponsors!! Just buy your ticket!

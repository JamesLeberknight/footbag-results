Manually Entered Results

Well, we've got another Windy City Cup under our belts, and I'd have to say we 
outdid ourselves from last year. What a great weekend we had! Before I get any 
further, I want to thank everyone that came out to compete, and I want to 
express a HUGE thanks to the local crew, especially Tom Whitworth, Zeke 
Ibardaloza, and Matt Sweeny. We could have never done it with out you guys!!

Navy Pier was gracious enough to host our event again this year. We moved our 
finals court to the North side of the front circle, where we saw an 
unbelievable amount of foot traffic. This was by far the best tournament I've 
ever had the pleasure of being involved with. Excellent work by the entire CLCK 
crew!!

Thanks again to all that made the trip, to Navy Pier for hosting us, and to our

Event Details:
Once again, CLCK will be bringing Footbag Net to the Masses!  Prelims will be 
held Friday and Saturday at Union Park (at the corner of Ashland and Lake St 
on the near-west side of the city), and our finals will take center stage at 
the biggest summer tourist attraction Chicago has to offer, Navy Pier.  Our 
finals site will feature stadium-style seating, free food and drink for our 
competitors, and some of the best freestyle demos and high-quality net 
matches ever seen.  Don't miss it!!

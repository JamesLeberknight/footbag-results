Accommodation:
We've got 34 dorm beds available. The price is based on how long you stay. 
1 or 2 nights: 17 euros/night. 
3 or 4 nights: 15 euros/night. 
5+ nights: 12 euros/night.

We also have private rooms with shared bathrooms. 3 rooms for 2 people (double
beds), 1 room for 3 people (double and single bed), 1 room with 3 single beds,
and 1 room for 4 people (2 double beds). 
1 or 2 nights: 28 euros (per person/night). 
3 or 4 nights: 25 euros (per person/night). 
5+ nights: 20 euros (per person/night).

And finally, there are 2 rooms with private bathrooms, each with a double bed. 
1 or 2 nights: 60 euros (per room/night). 
3 or 4 nights: 54 euros (per room/night). 
5+ nights: 45 euros (per room/night).

Included in the accommodation price is a basic breakfast, all day tea/coffee,
towels and sheets, WiFi, a free welcome beer at the bar downstairs, and
discounted beer for your whole stay.

Please let me know if you have any special requirements - I'll do my best to
meet your needs.
 
There is no additional tournament fee. Please let me know if you plan to
attend the event but you won't be staying at the hostel. Maybe you're coming
just for a day, or maybe you're staying somewhere else. I'd ask that you make
a contribution to the event, but it's up to you to decide what or how much. 

All of the weekend activities will be in walking distance of the hostel so you
won't have to spend your time and money on public transport. 

We'll be planning lots of other fun stuff during the week for those who come
early (or want to stay on after the weekend). 

A schedule will be ready about a week before the event, based on the number of
people coming.

Net will not be part of the weekend schedule, but there are parks close-by
where you can play. Someone will need to bring net equipment.

Maps and directions will also be available closer to the time.

Contact Caroline Birch on carolinejbirch@gmail.com or via facebook to book
your place. Payment via bank transfer will secure your accommodation option on
a first-in, first-served basis.

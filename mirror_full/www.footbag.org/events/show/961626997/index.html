This is the first year of this tournament. Hopefully there will be many more editions. A minimum of $500 (cdn) in prize money is guaranteed. There will be more prize money depending on the number of participants. Registration is $10 fo novice and intermediate players and $15 for open players. The dates may seem funny, but they are chosen so it is cheaper for players to go to Montreal's AND Quebec City's events (also, I am mooving on July 1st).
Event Details:
To register, please email at stephane.comeau@menv.gouv.qc.ca

All players are welcome to stay at my place. 

Schedule on Sunday, July 2nd: 
12h30 late registration
13h00: players meeting
13h15 to dark: singles competition (up to finals)

Schedule on Monday, July 3rd:
12h00: players meeting
12h15: Singles finals (men and women)
13h30 to 17h30: doubles competition (up to finals)
17h30 to dark: Finals (mixed and doubles)

Note: when everyone will agree, I will try to accomodate players with the 
schedule as much as possible.

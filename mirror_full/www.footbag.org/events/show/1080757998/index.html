The Houston Skyliners Footbag Club presents the Space City Jam 2004, in conjunction with the Westheimer Street Festival & the Texas Footbag League.

This will be a freestyle tournament hosted in the middle of Houston's biggest annual festival, with 20k - 50k people expected to show up. It's going to be a fun, relaxed tournament, designed mostly around just hangin out, shredding, and having a good time at the festival. Freestylers from all over Texas are expected to show up. Everyone is welcome to attend / compete.  The center of the Westheimer Street Festival is at Westheimer and Montrose.  Here's a map.  But people are not advised to just drive there and look for a place to park.  Check out the website for details: http://www.westheimerfe stival.com/

Prize money event. [IFPA Sanctioned].

Event Details:
Registration:
Open: $25
Intermediate: $15
Novice: FREE
Online payment is disabled.  Pay in cash at check-in.  Registration for 
open and intermediate includes a t-shirt, a Freedom footbag [while 
they last], and a chance to win cash or prizes.  Extra t-shirts are $12.
Non-IFPA members who want to compete in the open division will be 
required to register with IFPA for atleast a year, which costs only $10.  

What to bring:
Bring a folding chair if you have one.  Also think about bringing a cooler 
with some drinks.  I can transport these items to the site before the 
festival gets started.  Once it does, you won't be able to park within a 
short walking distance.  Maybe consider bringing a bike, if you would 
rather ride it than take the bus from your car to the site.  I don't know 
how bad parking is going to be, so check the website 
[www.westheimerfestival.com].
Let me know if you need a place to crash, whether you're looking for a 
hotel or a couch / floor.  Either way, I'm sure we can accommodate you.

Friday night we're meeting at Dallas' high-rise apartment building in 
downtown to check in / register / hangout / shred.  Email me for 
directions and details.

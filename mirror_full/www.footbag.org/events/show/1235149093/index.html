The 7th edition of the RNH Contest is coming and it will be big. Everybody who ever attended one of our tourneys in Paris knows what I?m talking about. Some of the world finest footbag players will come to the French Capital to compete at this IFPA sanctioned event.
Event Details:
Saturday April 18, 2009

09h30	Free breakfast (Club House)
10h00	End of late registration Doubles Net (Gym)
10h30	Doubles Net Players meeting (mandatory, Gym)
10h45	Warm-up
11h00	Kick-off Doubles Net
15h00	Brackets and consolation rounds
16h30	1st Doubles Net semi-finals
16h45 	End of late registration and Freestyle players meeting (Gym)
17h15	2nd Doubles Net semi-finals
17h30	Mixed Doubles Net players meeting (Gym)
18h00	Freestyle Routine, Sick 3 and Big 1
18h45	Doubles Net 3rd Place
19h30	Doubles Net finals

20h30	Diner at the Club House (free for registered players)


Sunday April 19, 2009

09h30	Free breakfast (Club House)
10h00	Mixed Doubles Net Games, if not already played
10h00	End of late registration Singles Net (Gym)
10h30	Singles Net Players meeting (mandatory, Gym)
10h45	Warm-up
11h00	Kick-off Singles Net
13h00	Brackets and consolation rounds
15h30	Singles Net semi-finals
16h30	Singles Net 3rd place
17h00	Singles Net finals

18h00	Award Ceremonies at the Club House

Hi all of you Southern footbag players,

As most of you already know, the original Southeasterns planned for April was canceled. We have taken it upon ourselves to host the tournament and expect it to be quite fantastic. The event will take place in a suburb of Atlanta called Roswell, just north of the downtown area. More specifically, the event will be at Waller Park, which is an excellent little venue. Whether you're a veteraned pro or a total newbie, come on out! We'd love to meet and kick with you regardless of your skill level. With shredders coming from all over the southeast, it's bound to be a good time.

There will be an entrance fee for each level of competition.

Freestyle
Open: $15
Intermediate: $10
Novice: $5 (allows competition in both consecutives and 4-square)

If you plan on coming to this event, competing or not, please e-mail me, or call me, and just let me know. I'm trying to get a head count, so we know how much awesomeness to expect.

Net
Intermediate Singles/Doubles: $20 (allows competition in both events)
Novice Singles: $10

Elliot will be in charge of all Footbag Net Competition. If you are interested in competing in Net, just shoot him an e-mail. We need to have an idea of what the turn out will be, so please, if even you are just considering about playing net, e-mail him.

Hope to see you there!

I'll be updating this alot. So keep coming back for new info.
Event Details:
IMPORTANT UPDATE- The address for the venue is 199 Dobbs Drive, not 250 Oak Street.
The place is still the same, but the address was just incorrect.

Saturday- Freestyle Competition

Place: Waller Park

199 Dobbs Drive
Roswell, GA 30075-4567

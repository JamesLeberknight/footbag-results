Event Details:
venue location:
Sofia hall is located at the south end of Borisova garden. It is a sport
complex at the corner of "Dragan Tsnakov" and "Nezabravka" streets. Close to
parc hotel "Moskva". 

access: 10 minutes walking from "Joliot Curi" metro station. The red line.
        
Facebook event:
https://www.facebook.com/events/342714489257881/

24/06 - Wednesday - pick ups (tba)
welcome meeting point: 20h. in front of the national theater "Ivan Vazov"

25/06 - Thursday - Gym opens at 10h. Matches start at 12h - singles net
26/06 - Friday - doubles net, freestyle
Official party: Grindhouse, Open Funk Jam 

27/06 - Saturday - doubles, mixed doubles, freestyle, final singles net,
After party: Monkey bar(borisova garden), Camp sessions

28/06 - Sunday - finals freestyle, open and mixed doubles finals.



Lodging:

- guest house "greenhouse", in the heart of the city, will be on our disposal
(about 20 places , 5e/night). booking by message to the organizing team.
 
- hostels list:
  Art Hostel,Canape connection, Crosspoint hostel, Hostel Mostel - these are 
all great locations. Check them on www.hostelbookers.com 

- for those who rent apartements, stay on the east side, close to the center.
 
- lodging at locals is available, booking starts now

Finnish Open has been held annually since 1997. This year we're back in Töölö Sports Hall, the site of Worlds 2011 and many other spectacular footbag tournaments. In addition to competing in net and freestyle, we'll also host a Hall of Fame celebration for Ole Schnack and Jan Zimmermann! So, see you in Helsinki, right?

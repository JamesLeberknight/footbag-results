Location:

Host Club:
Austin Style Footbag Club - Texas Footbag League
Events Offered: 
Sunday only - Noon til Dark - Fun day of Net and Freestyle 
Warm up/Training Camp and just kicking footbag for players 
of all ages, abilities, etc.
Since Texas State is moving to Houston this year - we are having a casual fun warm up tournament/training camp, just kicking day for players of all ages/abilities on Sunday October 13. 12:00 Noon til Dark. If you can't make to Texas State in Houston - come out and play. If you are thinking of going to Texas State - come out and ask questions, etc. (Parents welcome too) And, if you are definitely going - get ready! Texas State is moving to Houston this year to be part of a big event. The Westheimer Street Festival - one of the biggest and best festivals in Houston. See event listing by the Houston Skyliners Footbag Club
Event Details:
Please e-mail me if you are interested in a training camp.  We will be in 
Zilker Park on Barton Springs Road near the front gate and the playscape from 
Noon til Dark.   If, for some reason it's reserved we will be at the back 
gate, which is on Robert E. Lee Road off of Barton Springs Road.  The back 
gate is on Robert E. Lee Road on the right side, past the Umlauf Sculpture 
Gardens and near the baseball/football fields.  (Barton and Jaycee fields).  
There are two parking lots back there. My cell phone that day after noon will 
be 512-680-7669.   
 Just e-mail or call.   Tina.

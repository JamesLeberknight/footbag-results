Footbag Finnish Open 2024 singles net edition takes place in Helsinki. Like doubles net edition this too takes place almost in 2024, on April 26th, 2025! The venue for this heartwarming event is again our beloved training site known as Kulis, or more officially Manadutum Center in Kulosaari. Games start at 12, and the podium should be conquered by the afternoon. xoxo Footbag Finland

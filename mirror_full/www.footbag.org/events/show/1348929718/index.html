This tournament possessed players' participation of Caracas and of the State 
Táchira, for a total of 20 rival participants.

 

The 4ta Copa Tachira de Footbag develop with a high sports level characterized 
by the healthy competition, technique, skill, strength, flexibility and 
resistance. The first day of competition was development the modality of Footbag 
Net's singles, with the participation of 14 competitors, divided into 4 groups 
in the preliminary phase.

 

The second day of the event was developed the modality Footbag Net's doubles, 
with the participation of 10 pairs, divided in 3 groups in the preliminary 
phase.

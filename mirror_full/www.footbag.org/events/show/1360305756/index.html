Event Details:
Thank you for your interest in the 2013 U.S. Open Freestyle Footbag 
Championships!

This year the prestigious event will be hosted by Rain City Shred in Seattle, 
Washington. Both the competition and host hotel are located at the Washington 
Athletic Club in the heart of Downtown Seattle: https://www.wac.net/

Request the "U.S. Footbag Social Pricing Rate" in order to receive their 
discounted rate.

An alternative hotel option in North Seattle near several of our club members 
can be found here: http://www.comfortinn.com/hotel-seattle-washington-WA217

Open-level registration is $40. Intermediate-level registration is $25.

Sponsors and ancillary events TBD. Stay tuned for more information.

The AkiSphere is undoubtedly the most beautiful trophy of the footbag universe. To deserve it, the winners must compete against the finest players in the world. Last year, PT Lovern and Emmanuel Bouchard won after a memorable final. Who will have the honor this year to kiss the big shiny ball? Come, Americans, Europeans, South Americans, we pride ourselves in taking care of you (on and off the court)!

The Southern California Footbag Championships is an annual that happens early in the season, featuring both Net and Freestyle events. The entire event, tournmanent site, to host hotel, to Saturday and Sunday night parties takes place in Hermosa Beach, a with a diameter less than two miles. It's a fun event located blocks away from the beach.

We also feature Intermediate events, depending on the Intermediate draw. We've been pretty successful in previous years.

Dear Footbag Athletes, We are thrilled to welcome you to the upcoming Footbag Tournament in beautiful Erie, Pennsylvania! Thank you for choosing to participate and showcase your skills in this exciting event. We have an action-packed schedule planned for you, filled with thrilling competitions and opportunities to connect with fellow athletes. Our tournament event site will be at Perry Square 17 S Park Row Erie, PA 16501 The park is large yet should be easy to find. Well be set up on the west side of the park. Light refreshments and limited snacks will be available. Food will be available nearby for a charge at restaurants below and more. Here's a breakdown of the tournament schedule: Please notify us youll be coming by Thursday 6/8/23 to 814-431-4139. Friday: - 4:00 PM: Early registration begins. We will meet at: 712 Marne Road Erie, PA 16511 - 8:00 PM: Early registration closes. Participants may still register day-of at the event site in Perry Square as early as 9:00 AM. Saturday: - 9:30 AM: Announcements and registration - 10:00 AM: Game play starts - 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch break - 1:00 PM: Freestyle - 1:00 PM: Intermediate footbag net - 2:00 PM: Freestyle Routines - 5:00 PM: Freestyle finals - 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Optional pick up play - 5:00 PM: Semi-Finals net Sunday: - 10:30 AM: Announcements - 11:00 AM: Finals - 12:00 PM: Award ceremony - 1:00 PM: Optional pick up games We hope you enjoy the tournament and make unforgettable memories during your time in Erie. To enhance your experience, we wanted to share some local information: 1. Restaurants: After a day of intense competition, you can indulge in a variety of culinary delights. Some top restaurants near Erie Bayfront include The Cork, Sara's Restaurant, and Calamari's Squid Row. For something convenient and close to the tournament site, Flagship City Food Hall is a great choice for delicious local eats that are affordable priced. 2. Campgrounds: If you prefer the outdoors, Erie offers several beautiful campgrounds for you to unwind and reconnect with nature. Some notable options include Presque Isle Passage RV Park & Cabin Rentals and Sara's Campground. 3. Hotels: For those seeking comfortable accommodations, there are excellent hotels near Erie Bayfront. Consider checking out Sheraton Erie Bayfront Hotel, Courtyard by Marriott Erie Bayfront, or the Cobblestone Bayfront Erie. We hope this information helps you plan your stay in Erie and provides you with options to relax and explore the local area outside the tournament. Once again, we are delighted to have you as participants in the Footbag Net Tournament. Your talent and dedication will undoubtedly make this event a thrilling success. If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our event staff. Wishing you the best of luck and an incredible experience in Erie! Warm regards, Anthony Ritz and Daniel Greer Event Organizers for 2023 US Open

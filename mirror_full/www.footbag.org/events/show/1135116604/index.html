The Frankfurt Footbag e.V. is thrilled to be organizing this years 27th IFPA World Footbag Championships. The IFPA Footbag World Championships are the largest international tournament in the sport of footbag. For footbag players from all around the world, the World Championships provide an opportunity to demonstrate their skills and ability. Approximately 400 participants from 60 nations are expected to take part at this years Worlds. The date for the 7 day competition is the 16.- 22. July 2006. PLEASE SEE THE WORLDS WEBSITE FOR UP-TO-DATE PLAYER AND SPECTATOR INFO!

Accommodation Info:

We are offering several accommodation options 
to suit the range of budgets people will have (ranging 
from very cheap accommodations all the way to nice hotels). 

For the players who look for a cheap accommodation we are 
offering rooms of 6? 10 people in the school ?Ernst- Reuter- Schule?. 
The price for one person per night will be around 5 Euros.
You will need to bring sleeping bags and a sleeping pad with you to stay there.

For those of you who like to stay in more comfortable hotels, please 
take a look at the hotel reservation form.

Another option is camping at the City Camp Frankfurt
http://www.city-camp-frankfurt.de/

Furthermore we like to point out, that as long as you're staying in the centre of
Frankfurt 
you won't be far from the event location. The public transport system is very good
and can take you everywhere within the city very quickly.

Events Offered: 
Prize money events will be Net and Freestyle.  I am also 
willing to have other events (Golf?Consecutives etc)on a 
"just for fun" basis, as interest demands.
Demo set up through YMCA and Philly's largest radio station 
(Y100 radio) to bring footag to the local potential players.
The Philly Open is geared towards supporting east coast play,as well as bringing the sport to a new community. Set up in conjunction with the Phoenixville YMCA and Y100-Philly's largest radio station-we have exposure that is not normally accessible to footbag on the east coast. With player support,a competitive and informative weekend will be had by all!
Event Details:
For players arriving friday night (March 26th), please follow these 
directions to my house for player registration, and a player's party.

1)From the PA turnpike:
2)Take exit 24 (Valley Forge)
3)Take rte 202 south
4)422 west
5)Collegeville/Phoenixville exit
6)Follow sign towards Collegeville (this is rte 29 north)
7)At 'T' in road (McDonalds on Left), go left (main street)
8)Go through two lights, and turn into the "Trappe Plaza" at the third light.
9)Go across the street to the Big White House (I live there) for registration.

For players arriving saturday morning, late registration will be at the 
competition site (the Phoenixville YMCA).  Follow these directions to get to 
the site..

1)Follow the above directions up and thru line number 5 (the 
Collegeville/Phoenixville exit)
2)Follow the sign towards Phoenixville (this is rte 29 south).
3)You want to keep following signs for rte 29 south.  This road takes a 
couple of turns, so keep your eyes open. Make sure you keep on rte 29 S!
4)Rte 29 south will shortly bring you to a stop light intersection with 
"Pothouse Road"  (There will also be a sign directing players in the right 
direction here)
5)Go left onto Pothouse road
6)The YMCA is about a 1/4 mile up on your right..You'll see the action!


TOURNEY HOTEL- Holiday Inn, Pottstown PA (15 mins from site)- 610/327/3300
Also, weather permitting, camping will be available on my two acres.

SCHEDULE- Friday March 26th- 2:00-whenever...Jamming,REGISTRATION,Party!!
          Saturday March 27th- 7:00-9:00..LATE REGISTRATION..events.
          Sunday March 28th- Main events begin at 9:00.  

We have radio exposure (Y100 radio) at this event in addition to the fact the 
YMCA's average weekend attendance hovers around 10,000 people from the local 
community.  Lots of exposure for our sport, so please try and come and 
represent the quality of athletes that we are!

Keep kicking!!!

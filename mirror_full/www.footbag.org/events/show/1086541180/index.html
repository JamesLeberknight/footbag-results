Event Details:
WHAT:

This is the 2nd Annual "Kick Til You're Sick"  Fall Freestyle Fest hosted by 
me, Jon.  In attendence last year we had the Philly Footworks Crew to which I 
am proud to be a part, a bunch of new local kickers, and of course some of 
our favorite advanced players including Bob Riefer, Josh Benham, Nick Miller, 
the "Outsider" himself Mr. Jonathan Schneider, Matt Cross and many More, not 
to mention the legend himself, the "Enforcer" Kenny Shults.  We had a great 
turnout last year with around 40 players and we are hoping to surpass that   
number this year.

There are some competitions with the possibility for Net on hard wood floors 
this year, keep checking the page for a definite answer on this soon.  There 
will also be the fun-filled Four Square.  We will be printing shirts, with a 
cost of no more than $10.00 to purchase one.  

This event is also a Fundraiser for the AMERICAN RED CROSS, whereby all 
proceeds will go directly to the Red Cross for Charity.  There is a $5.00 
entrance fee for this event as a mandatory donation to the Red Cross.

The Facility we are using as our Venue is a State of the Art Gymnasium in a 
new school with the following Attributes that make this the perfect venue:

     Awesome Traction
     Professional grade lighting (NO shadows)
     Temperature Controlled (Nice and cool with no wind)
     Locker Rooms
     Rest Rooms
     Showers
     Cold Water Foundtains
     Large Multiple Basketball Court Gymnasium
     (Possibly Nets for Footbag Net)

COMPETITIONS:

Competitions will take place depending on Number of participants, with a 
minimum of three (3) players to compete.  To Enter a competions, the cost 
will be free to Novice Players competing, $5.00 for Intermediate, and $10.00 
for Open Players.  Prizes will range from various footbags to videos to cash 
prizes, with some new possibilities depending on place taken and Level of 
competition.  Last year we awarded Over $100.00 in cash prizes, along with 
several videos and footbags.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND & WHY:

This event was a blast last year and will be even better this year.  We are 
expecting several of the best Footbag Freestyle and Net Players in the world 
to attend along with a lot of local players.  There will be a lot of people 
new to the Competative  Arena from the local area and both of the Council 
Rock High Schools.  It is very inexpensive, and a lot of fun whether or not 
you compete.  The majority of this is a Jam/Convention of Footbaggers from 
all over to have a great time.  If you 
Footbag/hackeysack/kick/shred/freestyle or have ever done so or are 
interested in learning more about it, this is the place to be.

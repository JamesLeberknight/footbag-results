todexon_web_img.jpg Todexon - Freesyte Footbag Tournament is here! as it was for the last past years. The Number thirteen is sometimes said to be an unlucky number in some cultures. On other hand several successful sports figures have worn the number 13. TODEXON is behind the doors so the following words in the next pages might be helpfull with a few important things you might find usefull. The day 6-7-8th April, Prague, K4_Club (Celetná 20). You! and of course the top players from around Czech lands and its surrounding coutries. As always you can count with a great hospitality and nice event, and crazy shred.

Registration:


For you to get started and have everything prepared from the organisation teams best people please visit and register by clicking this highlighted link :), for the price of 200,- CZK which is approximately 8 EUR you will get a players pack including a unique designed Todexon 13 t-shirt, soft drinks and food on the tournament day, and of course a great display of world class freestlyle.

Event Location:

Again we have selected a great venue for our premier freestyle event.

This year we are very close to the Old Town Square in an adjacent street Celetná 20 - Klub K4
Entrance is through a big wooden door, should be marked by Todexon 13 poster. Behind the door its just straight through the corridor and before the gatehouse turn left and see the entrance down the cellar :) (glass cube covered by posters).

Click here for a google maps l ink. or visit their webpage at http://www.k4klub.org/

Time Schedule:

Friday:

18.00 - 20.00 Shred at the National Theater

21.00 - 23.00 Dinner together at "the Restaurant"

Saturday:

11.00 - 12.00 Players Meeting and Registration at the K4 Students Club

12.00 - 13.00 Warm-up and so on

13.00 - 15.00 Qualification of all necessary disciplines

15.00 - 16.30 Sick 3, Shred, Big 1

17.00 - 19.30 Finals of all disciplines, Shows and Performances

20.00 - 20.30 Awards Ceremony

Sunday:

13.00 - 17.00 Shred a an unknown location, probably National Theater again, maybe Circle competition.

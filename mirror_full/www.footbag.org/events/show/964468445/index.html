****Special Instructions for Late Arrivals****
(General details follow)

If you are arriving after dark to the tourney site PLEASE NOTE:

WILLAMETTE MISSION STATE PARK is a "day use only" park.  That is, for 
everyone *except* those at the Beav'.  We have a special dispensation 
to camp and do crazy stuff.  

SO: After dark the park gate is "locked."  AND the gate is about 1 mile 
from where we camp.  That's a long walk, late at night.  Just ask Steve 
"Jungle Juice" Dusablon and Dave "Why did I Drink That Stuff?" Bernard.

HOWEVER:  The gate is actually "dummy locked" which means that unless 
yer a card-carryin' dummy, you can still get in.  

HERE'S THE SECRET:  On the gate there is a small cast-iron box and if 
you reach up under that box, you'll feel a padlock - a padlock that is 
*not* locked!  Take the padlock off, open the gate, and close the gate 
afterward, replacing the lock as you found it.  (It's best to drive your 
vehicle through the gate before doing this.)

SPECIAL NOTE: IF you LOCK the padlock after you close the gate it is 
NO LONGER "DUMMY-LOCKED" - then it's what we call; "LOCKED BY A DUMMY."  
Anyone arriving later than you will have a long hike to camp and will 
have a few choice words with you in the morn.

TO SUM UP:  Don't be a dummy.

*****
General Details:
When: Labor Day Weekend - For added fun and relaxation, plan to arrive early. 
We've reserved the park for an extra 2 days this year. Set-up as early as 
Wednesday, August 30th. Plan to stay until Labor Day, Monday, September 4th. 
See schedule of events for details of tournament.

Where: Willamette Mission State Park - 7 miles north of Salem. Take the 
Brookes Exit off I-5, head west until the road T's at Wheatland Ferry Road. 
Turn right and follow the signs.

How Much: Adults $60.00 / Ages 6 - 13 $15.00 / 5 years and younger free but 
with no t-shirt unless ordered separately. 

Per Night Fee: $20.00 - does not include shirt

Pre-registration is strongly but pleasantly requested.

Pre-registration will allow us to plan appropriate amounts of food and 
beverages for your camping pleasure.  

Pre-entry includes: T-shirt, beverages, daylight energy breakfast of muffins, 
fruit and coffee, campfire munchies, and meals as noted below.

Saturday dinner includes: Pasta Night!! Beaver Open provides pasta, sauces, 
and french bread.   Potluck salad buffet: please bring a salad of your choice.

Sunday dinner includes: BBQ Night! Beaver Open provides baked potatoes, corn 
on the cob, french bread and the fixins. Please bring your own meat for the 
grill.

Gentlemen's /Gentlewomen's games include: 
Draw Partner Net, Freestyle Energy Circle, Footbag Golf (day and glow), 
Cribbage, Bizz-Buzz, Juggle Golf and more!!

Other Ideas of Things to Bring: bubbles, twiddle sticks, mania, juggle balls 
and pins, Beaver memorabilia, musical instruments, drums, bota bags, whimsical 
doodads, wind toys, citronella candles, lanterns, old photos, beaver wood 
carving tools, frisbees and other flying objects, cameras, gaudy lawn 
ornaments, etc...

New ideas for this year's event include: 
Children's parade (kids may want to plan to bring costumes etc to 
participate), Brew Ha Ha (beer brewing contest by our own Beaver family's 
best), smoothie contest (non-alcoholic version of the brew ha ha), ice cream 
social, and maybe bringing back the adopt-a-hole but without a fee (Instead, 
decorate your golf hole the way you choose and then either bring or order 
enough glow to light it up if you need more than what 
Beaver Open would normally use on the course), Kid's campfire sing along (w/ 
s'mores), better organized adult sing along. 

If you are interested in participating, organizing and/or implementing any of 
these ideas or you have other ideas of your own please contact us by phone or 
e-mail asap.
**Remember to plan your act for the "We Love Annie" Not so Late Variety Show**
20th Annual Hall of Famers: Garwin Bruce, Cheryl Hughes, and Jeff "Animal" 
Johnson
Please send in your entries to addresses below no later than August 15th. If 
you plan to attend but cannot pre-enter, please call and make arrangements by 
the 15th as well.

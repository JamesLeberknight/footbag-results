The Santa Cruz Footbag Club (SCFC) is throwing a dope freestyle footbag event in conjunction with Emily's Birthday Pajammy Jam. Anybody who knows and loves Emily or is captivated by Jeremy (Senor Grommet) is expected to make an appearance. That includes YOU! We want everybody to come and experience the lifestyle of our community and the awesomeness of the SCFC. If you're a person, you should come to our event, because everyone should be here. We are expecting players from all over California and hopefully from several surrounding states.
Event Details:
(EVERYONE IS INVITED!)

This is the event you've been waiting for...
The First Annual Santa Cruz Invitational,run by two of your favorite footbag personalities
and overall attractive people (Moxie a.k.a. Emily Kulczyk, and 
Senor Grommet a.k.a. Jeremy Mirken), and an up-and-comer, who is also more attractive than
your average footbag player (FunkLovesFootbag a.k.a. Fletcher 
Funk). The Santa Cruz Invitational (AKA Emily's Birthday Pajammy Jam) will be held on June
10th and 11th, 2006, the weekend after my birthday. We will be 
celebrating that weekend so bring your presence, jammys, party hats, and Lavers. We are
expecting players from all over California and hopefully from several 
surrounding states.  Several high level players will be present including Jeremy Mirken,
Lon Smith, Big Add Chad, Nick Landes and others.


Our venue is the Aptos Academy which has a thinly carpeted gym floor and a stage.  The
weather will be nice enough for us to kick outside as well.  The 
high in that part of June is usually about 72F and the low is about 50F. So bring warm and
cool weather clothing.

Aptos Academy
1940 Bonita Drive
Aptos, Ca 95003

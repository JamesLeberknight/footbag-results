It's been 10 years since the first Hackrifice footbag tournament was held in Winnipeg back in '99, and 12 years since the inception of Hackrifice F.C. While other canadian events have come and gone we've managed to survive an entire decade of events, thanks mostly to the dedication of a small group of prairie kickers who, come september, drive 30+ hours down the most desolate strip of highway known to man for our weekend event. For our decennial anniversary, we want to throw the best weekend event we can to say THANK YOU to those kickers past and present who gave us a reason to keep throwing our jam. We also wish to invite every one across Canada, the USA, and the world to come out and celebrate our 10th birthday in style! WHAT: -The 10th Annual Hackrifice Footbag Tournament WHEN: -September 19-20-21st 2008 WHERE: Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada -Venues TBA: Expect a "preliminary" location and a "Finals" location for freestyle. Net and freestyle events will take place at the same preliminary location. EVENTS: -Net: Singles, Doubles, "Winnipeg Doubles" (Days have yet to be decided for singles and doubles, check back for updates. "Winnipeg doubles" is doubles vs. a single) -Freestyle: Routines, Shred 30*, Rippin' Run Elimination Tableau* (These will be the official events that may be included in the finals show. Currently in debate about rippin run and shred 30, but ROUTINES WILL BE AN OFFICIAL EVENT!!!). All official freestyle events will be run on saturday. Side events may be included for fun on sunday. SHIRTS: -We always try to bring something cool to the table, and this year will be no exception. We're also gonna be kicking it retro. If you never managed to get your paws on some of our originals, we're reprinting a few select throw backs from years gone by... SPONSORS -feeling generous? We're always looking for prizes to give away, or funds to help the show run smoothly. PM/e-mail me if you might be able to contribute to our cause! OTHER INFO and TBA's -Mansion party kegger? -Friday night welcome shred, spike, wine and dine locations TBA -Saturday night Spaghetti dinner for our out of town friends -Saturday night freestyle finals at a local club TBA ATTENDANCE: -Erik Chan -Derek Vandall -Andrew Grant -Jessica Archer -Manna Mpeppo -Joel Dion -Josh Wayborne -You?

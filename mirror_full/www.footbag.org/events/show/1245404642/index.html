The 6# Swiss Footbag Open was terrific, a sunny WE with a lot of action and a good atmosphere among the footbag family! After a peaceful friday with only single net qualifications we spent a great evening with dinner at Renato's place before going out to electrosanne, an electronic music festival in town! On saturday the doubles net qualifications were played before freestyle action did take place with an amazing DROPLESS routine from Jakob! Circle qualification was following routines one, leading to the selection of 4 players for final: Paul, Karim, Jakob and Yves, 3 Foostars and 1 Pieds-ŕ-Gilles! Freestyle finals (Routines + Circle) took place in the bunker-style espace 44 in between Yo-yo and contact juggling shows by Dominik and Vlad respectivly on the Funky dancefloor of DJ Swoop and DJ Renato Renato!! A Crazy Night! The WE ended sunday with golf competition and incredible single and double Net finals! Les Pieds ŕ Gilles were really happy to organize this event and hope that next time more players will come !!

This will be our 2nd Annual Footbag Tournament. Last year was a huge success, so we're doing it again! See our club website for more information.
Event Details:
The tournament will be held in the Main Room in the Lengyel Gym on the UMaine 
Campus with plenty of room for spectators.  Participants may compete in any 
number of these categories:  2-minute Shred, singles Freestyle, Endurance, 
Elimination, shred 30, and a Sick 3 competition.  There will be an 
entrance fee of $10 for each participant to be paid at the door or in 
advance.  The first and second place winners in each ability level will 
receive a new, professional-grade Footbag or related prize.

For driving directions, go to:
http://www.geocities.com/umhackysackclub/tournament_info.html

Schedule of afternoon events:

2:15 Lv2 2-minute Shred rounds begin

3:00 First demonstration
	
3:30 Lv1 Endurance rounds begin

4:00 Lv2 Endurance rounds begin
	
4:45 Second demonstration 

5:00 Lv1 Elimination rounds begin

5:30 Lv2 Shred 30 & Sick 3 competition

6:00 Announcement of winners, distribution of prizes, and closing remarks

Event Details:
For complete event details you must go to the 'events' page at chaosfootbag.com - see link
below event title above.

Brackets for Division A and B for both singles players and doubles teams will
be drawn up from those players/teams who do(Division A) and do not(Division B) advance
from pool play.

Our Housing Czar is Tuan Vu: tvu@chaosfootbag.com

Need roof and a ride?  Contact Tuan Vu, aka Chaos #7, and he will broker you a
deal with a local kicker for the weekend.  Rides not guaranteed but we'll give
it our best shot.

Who'll be here? Players from across North America from cities and states such as
Montreal, Portland, Tennessee, NYC, New Jersey, and Illinois are confirmed along
 with those of us from sunny California of course.  Current and former World 
Champions like Patrick Schrickel, PT Lovern, John Leys, Alexis Deschenes, Jody 
Welch, and Chris Seibert are confirmed. Rumors are still flying regarding the 
likes of Kenny Shults, Tricia George, Emmanuel Bouchard, Yves Archambault among 
others.  Of course, we're hoping Dr. Kern will be on hand serving up the world's
 best Margaritas on Championship Monday as usual.  Don't miss this Grand Slam 
event and party!

Oh yeah - categories may be combined if fewer than 4 participants/teams.

Brackets for Division A and B for both singles players and doubles teams will
be drawn up from those players/teams who do(Division A) and do not(Division B) advance
from pool play.

Prelim: Estuary Park at Jack London Aquatic Center - 115 Embarcadero - Oakland, CA 94606 -
Sat & Sun
Finals: Monday in Peacock Meadown in Golden Gate Park, San Francisco, CA

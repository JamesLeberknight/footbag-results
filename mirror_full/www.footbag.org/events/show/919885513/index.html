Event Details:
Friday night meeting place
This year Martin Côté will be the host for the friday evening registration. 
And for player from out of town who need a place to stay. Here's the way to 
get there

From the US (via Hwy 87):

After you pass the border, the road name changes to Hwy 15.
Take Hwy 132 or 20 east (same road), follow signs for
"Pont Jacques Cartier" (bridge), direction Montreal.
After the bridge, you'll be on De Lorimier Street.
Go up to St-Zotique Street, turn right on St-Zotique and then turn right 
at second street (Louis Hémon).
Martin Côté's house is at 6670 Louis Hémon, almost on the corner. 
 
From Ontario:

Highway 401 will change to Hwy 40 in Quebec. Once in Montreal
(hwy 40 is also known as "autoroute Metropolitain"), take exit
Delorimier/Papineau. Follow directions for Papineau, turn right
on Papineau (going south). Go down to St-Zotique Street, turn left on it then 
turn right at Louis-Hémon. (about 6 streets from Papineau)
Martin Côté's house is at 6670 Louis Hémon, almost on the corner. 
If you get lost just phone Martin at 514-727-3795

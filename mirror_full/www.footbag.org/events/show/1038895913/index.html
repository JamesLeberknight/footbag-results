The unofficial trials champ was Liam Johnston hitting various big tricks off 
ledges (incl. Mobius, Fairy Drifter, Fairy Same Symp. Whirl, Triple Over Down, 
Supa-Fly and some other mad stylings)

It's all go for the weekend of the 21st-23rd February 2003, the event is being held under the umbrella of the NZ Fringe Festival and is starting to look awesome. We have confirmed our prinicpal sponsor will be Seapa who are contributing heaps (both $$ and prizes) to the event. As many of you know Ryan Mulroney (2 time world champ) will be here in full force, and there are a few other internationals who are considering the trip. We are planning on having a video produced of the event, a professional photographer floating around snapping pics, heaps of prizes, an awesome after- party, event T-shirts and heaps more...
Event Details:
Freestyle Footbag Jam
Friday 21st Feb (8pm - 11pm)
Cuba Mall
Cuba Mall comes alive with insane freestyle footbag action including DJ's, 
prizes and competitions for everyone to have a go. Come down kick a sack 
around and win some prizes.

Fringe Freestyle Festival
Saturday 22nd Feb (12pm - 4pm)
Chaffers Park
The first day of footbag competition plus workshops from the pros, learn some 
tricks for yourself or just sit back and watch the everyone busting out huge. 
Also includes body painting workshops, skateboarding, breakdancing, DJ'ing, 
bombing and more...

NZ Freestyle Footbag Nationals
Sunday 23rd Feb (12-3pm)
Te Papa Amphitheatre (Level 1)
The third and final day of competition featuring big tricks competitions and 
choreographed routines. We scored large here with an awesome rain or shine 
venue and heaps of general public to show our stuff to.

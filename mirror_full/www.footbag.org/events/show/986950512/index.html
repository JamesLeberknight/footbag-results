Shred 4 Independence (i4)

Contact:

Eliot Piltz

Alias: flipsider@footbag.org
Location:
Vancouver, Bc
Canada
July 1-4, 2001  (concluded)
Location:
Belmont Park
San Diego, California, USA
Host Club:
Blurred Vision
Home Page:
http://www.zoharpro.com/Athletes.htm
Events Offered: 
Shred:30 contest
The i4 will be one dope jam! Top pros from around California and beyond will converge to push freestyle past its current threshold. Groms and amatuers are encouraged to join in the sessions, but know your place in the circle. Anyone who plays hack in the San Diego area should come out for this- it's gonna go off.
Event Details:
Most sessions will take place on the South side of Canes Bar & Grill in 
Mission Beach.
~Schedule is somewhat impromptu.  This event is loosely organised by 
players- we're here to have fun.  Sessions will form at 3pm each day, and 
last about three hours.  There will likely be evening sessions in front of 
Hamel's in Mission Beach.
~If you have not made plans for accomodations, then it's too late to hit up 
Blurred Vision.  We are at capacity.  If you have anyquestions, call 858-
488-2133.

~Driving Directions:
From Fwy-15 (either direction) or Fwy-5 (if heading north from Downtown),
take Fwy-8 West (Beaches);
EXIT West Mission Bay Drive (Mission Beach);
turn right at stoplight, and continue straight across the bridge;
shortly you will take a second EXIT West Mission Bay Drive,
and loop around; this will take you to Mission Beach;
You will see a Rollercoaster on the left, and a stoplight ahead of you;
that is Mission Blvd…

If heading South on Fwy-5, EXIT Sea World Drive, and turn right at the 
light;
drive straight until you see EXIT West Mission Bay Drive (just past Sea 
World);
take a second EXIT West Mission Bay Drive, and loop around;
this will take you to Mission Beach;
You will see a Rollercoaster on the left and a stoplight ahead of you;
that is Mission Blvd…

*note:  It’s easiest to find parking before 11 am.  CAR POOL, and take 
public transportation if available.

…to Blurred Vision Shredquarters: 732 ½ Jamaica Ct.; turn Right on 
Mission Blvd.;
Jamaica Court is the Third court- we are Beach-side; park as close as 
you can.
…to i4 Jam spot: South-side of Canes Bar & Grill against the Boardwalk 
(Oceanfront).

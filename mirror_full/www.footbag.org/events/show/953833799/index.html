Events Offered:

Singles Freestyle
Pro Freestyle EXHIBITION
Event Details:
This event is geared towards introducing novice and intermediates to the sport 
of footbag.

This is not a PRO competition.  The big event for Pro's in the Chicago area is 
July 7-9th (tentative) and will be split between Mayors Cup and Navy Pier, 
much like worlds 1999, with less time to spare.

Please plan on a fun afternoon, with music, barbeques and many people from Mt. 
Prospect as our spectators.  Bring a cooler and plenty to eat and drink, as 
there will be no breaks during the day.

Pro's are welcome to come out and meet the newest players, encourage and train 
them in the sport.

Novice and Intermediate competitors must be registered by 10:00 a.m. Sunday to 
compete. 

Entry Fee: 
$5 includes all events.

Directions to Lions Park:
Golf Road to Route 83 (Elmhurst Road) North to Lincoln Street, East to park.

Please call for more specific information.

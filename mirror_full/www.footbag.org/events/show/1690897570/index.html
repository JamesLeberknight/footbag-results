Today, 1st of august, the Basque Association of Beach Tennis and Minority Sports will
organize the IV. Basque Tournament of Footbag (Doubles). We will gather 6-8 players so we
will make 3 or 4 pairs and play. If we are three pairs we will play all against all. If we
are 4; semifinals in the first round and in the second round, 3º and 4º position match and
the finakl. We will start more or less at 18:30 pnm.


Manually Entered Results

Finally the last tusday we met 6 players so we made three pairs. Egoitz Campo and 
Lander Campo won the tournament because they made more points than the other 
pairs. All of them fot 1 victory but Egoitz and Lander got 1 more point than the 
second position pair. These were the results:

Egoitz Campo and Lander Campo 8-5 Felipe Haase and Unai Pérez
Jesus Carballeira and Andi Erromo 8-6 Egoitz Campo and Lander Campo
Felipe Haase and Unai Pérez 8-1 Jesus Carballeira and Andi Erromo

Classification of the IV. Basque Tournament of Footbag Net (Doubles)
1-Egoitz Campo (Basque Country) / Lander Campo (Basque Country) 1 victory - 14 
points.
2-Felipe Haase (Colombia) / Unai Pérez (Basque Country)  1 victory - 13 points.
3-Jesus Carballeira (Galicia) / Andi Erromo (Basque Country) 1 victory 9 points

Congratulations to Egoitz Campo and Lander Campo, and also to the rest of players!

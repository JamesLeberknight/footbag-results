The Santa Cruz Sole Jam was a huge success thanks to the many faces, past and 
present, who came out to support the event. The furthest travelers were John 
Leys from New York/New Jersey and Chris Seibert and Steve Dusablon from 
Portland. All three played superbly, Johnny making it to the quarterfinals with 
Jody Welch, and Chris and Steve reaching the finals, ultimately losing to the 
powerhouse team of Alexis Deschenes and Patrick Scrickel of Chaos. 

I would like to thank everyone who attended for making the Sole Jam a great 
event. Our turnout of high level players was excellent thanks mostly to the 
Chaos crew and surrounding Bay area kickers. The best performance by a Santa 
Cruz Footbag Club team was Angel Rodriguez and Chris Young, who finished 7th 
overall. Congrats guys! I hope to see many more of you next year. It appears as 
if this event is a keeper and a great warm up for the Green Cup in Oakland!


The Santa Cruz Soul Jam will take place May 5-6th, 2007 in Santa Cruz, California. Hosted by the Santa Cruz Footbag Club, it will showcase a weekend of footbag net, drawing players from all skill levels, all locations, and all time periods.

Every week isolated groups of footbaggers make the calls to round up a foursome, set up their nets, and play.

Soul Jam is the opportunity to unite in beautiful Santa Cruz, CA and to indulge in that same ritual of high flying, tattoo delivering glory. For new and old footbag players, amateurs and pros, and players who've not touched a footbag in several years, this is the chance for the ultimate non tournament-feeling kick.

Combining pool play or round-robin structure, loads of pickup games, and chillaxing in true coastal California style, the Soul Jam is already enveloping your soul as you read the event description.

It will be an event to experience.

Event Details:
The venue for the Soul Jam will be Monterey Avenue Park, located at 688
 Monterey Ave. in Capitola, just above the Capitola Village. The venue offers
 convenient access to the beach, several fine dining establishments, and a
 shopper's haven (i.e., less than 1 mile). On Saturday, play will begin by
 10:30AM and conclude by no later than 6PM. On Sunday, free play may begin as
 early as 10AM and must conclude by 5PM.


Driving directions: 
Coming from NORTH:

Take I-880 which turns into CA-17 S 
Merge right onto CA-1 S toward WATSONVILLE/MONTEREY.
Take the Porter/BAY exit.
Make right onto Bay.
Travel past the 3rd stop sign.
Make Left onto Monterey (there is no light or stop sign here, only a left hand
 turn. If you hit a 4th stop sign you've missed the left turn).
End at Monterey Park on your right.


Coming from SOUTH:

Take HWY 1 North.
Exit Park Ave. 
Turn Left onto Park Ave. 
Turn Right onto Kennedy.
Stay Straight to go onto Monterey Ave.
End at 688 Monterey ave on left.

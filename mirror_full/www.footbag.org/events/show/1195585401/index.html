Net Info:
---------

Official 
Schedule for The Green Cup - Net Schedule

Open singles, open doubles, open women's singles, open women's doubles, open
mixed doubles.
Singles events are single elimination - no pool play.
Categories may be combined if fewer than 4 participants/teams.

Brackets for Division A and B for doubles teams will be drawn up from those
teams which do(Division A) and do not(Division B) advance from pool play.

Prelims: Saturday & Sunday (Lakeside Park at Lake Merrit, Oakland, CA)
Finals: Monday (Peacock Meadow in Golden Gate Park, San Francisco, CA)

Note:  This is a professional sports event.  Uniforms are required for all
open net players!

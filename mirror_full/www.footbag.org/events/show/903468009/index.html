The 5th Annual Southern California Footbag Championships!

Watch as some of the world's best pull the hugest tricks and catch the biggest air.

Located at Hermosa Valley Park on Valley and Gould, in Hermosa Beach, California.

Sat and Sun April 10th and 11th

10:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Event Details:
SCHEDULE OF EVENTS:

Saturday, April 10th
9:00am - 10:00am ---------------- Registration 
10:00am ------------------------- Players Meeting
11:00am ------------------------- Singles Net (pool play)
12:00pm ------------------------- 45-second shred (no drop limit, one round)
1:00pm -------------------------- Lunch
1:30pm -------------------------- Singles Freestyle, Intermediate and Open
                                  (first round)
2:30pm -------------------------- Singles Net(elimination play)

Sunday, April 11th
8:30am -------------------------- Mixed Doubles Net (first two rounds)
10:30am ------------------------- Open & Intermediate doubles net (Pool play)
1:30pm -------------------------- Singles Freestyle, Intermediate and Open
                                  (final round)
2:30pm -------------------------- Open & Intermediate Doubles Net Semifinals  
4:00pm -------------------------- Mixed and Intermediate Doubles Net Finals
5:00pm -------------------------- Open Doubles Net Finals
6:00pm -------------------------- Awards Ceremony.


DIRECTIONS TO SITE

From the 405 North, exit 105 West, exit Sepulveda South, to Artesia/Gould (4 
miles), turn Right, through 2 stop signs, Hermosa Valley Park is on left.

From the LAX Airport, take Sepulveda South, to Artesia/Gould (4 miles), turn 
Right, through 2 stop signs, Hermosa Valley Park is on left.

From 405 South, exit Artesia West, turn into Gould at Pacific Coast 
Highway/Sepulveda, continue straight through 2 stop signs, Hermosa Valley 
Park is on left.

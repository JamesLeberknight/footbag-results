The weekend for this years prime freestyle footbag event is 5.-6. April in Prague, Czech Republic. After 15 years of this competition we have a big change for all the players: this year will be the first time players will compete for prize money! Czech Footbag Association and Prague will be ready to host yet another great annual event and will be more than happy to host all the players as best as we can. For the first time in history we offer prizemoney!

READ ME!!!
I'm excited to announce Los Angeles, California will be kicking off the new 
year hosting Shred-Is-Not-Dead New Years Jam 2024!!!

If you're looking to shred the ol' stitch biscuit on the west coast, you

rentals as well as Bird/Lime/Scooters to get you around more conveniently. The 
beach is a 15 minute bike ride from my house on a safe bike path too!

Events Offered: 
Doubles Net, Freestyle Shred, Golf
This annual event offers a chance for Bay Area players to have some fun playing Net, Freestyle, and Golf. Saturday will feature a Doubles Net bracket at Eagle Park in Mountain View. Saturday night there will be a party at the Welch's house. Sunday features "draw-partner" net that will match up new players with more experienced ones. There will also be a Freestyle Shred on Sunday that will show off some of the world's best freestyle talent. Both days start off with a warm-up and net drilling session where all can learn some new skills.
Event Details:
Saturday, October 7, 2000 -- Eagle Park, Mountain View
    11:00 am Warm Up and Drills
    12 noon sharp Doubles Net Begins
    5:00 pm net concludes
    6:00 pm 'til 10:00 pm Party at the Welches' (2540 Dell Ave, Mountain View)


Sunday, October 8, 2000 -- Peacock Meadow, Golden Gate Park
    9:30 am Golf
    11:00 am Warm Up and Drills
    12 noon sharp draw-partner Doubles Net Begins
    3:00 Freestyle Shred
    4:00 More Net Fun


Directions to Eagle Park:  Take the Shoreline Blvd.
exit South (away from the Bay) off 101.  Eagle Park is on the
left, just before you get to El Camino Real.  Turn left on Church.


Directions to Peacock Meadow:  This meadow is at the very
East end of Golden Gate Park, near the intersection of Fell and
Stanyan.  Park along Fell and walk into the park where the road
is closed, and find Peacock Meadow on your right, just before the
white Conservatory of Flowers.


Directions to the Welches':
   From Eagle park.  Go North on El Camino Real to San Antonio Drive.
   Go right on San Antonio to the light at Nita.  Turn right on Nita one block,
   then turn left at the stop sign.  Go to the end of the street
   where it T's into Dell Avenue at our house.


   From US-101, take the San Antonio Road South exit in Mountain View.
   Go 4 lights to Nita and make a left at the left-turn light.
   Go one block, then turn left at the stop sign.
   Go to the end of the street where it T's into
   Dell Avenue at our house.

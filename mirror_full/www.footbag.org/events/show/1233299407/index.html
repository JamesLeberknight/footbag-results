The Sole Purpose Group is proud to announce the 2009 US Open Freestyle Footbag Championships in Vancouver, WA (near Portland, OR), on July 11-12, with a special event on July 13 in downtown Portland, OR.

This event will be dedicated to the sport of freestyle footbag. This year the event will be hosted at the Nautilus World Headquarter sport facility, in the PDX area. Many events will be offered, and it is all open to the public!

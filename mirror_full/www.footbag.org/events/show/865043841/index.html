Manually Entered Results

I am glad to report that the 2nd Annual Chaos Outdoor Footbag Festival was
an awesome event. A major highlight was the first round of freestyle, which
was held in the middle of the Carbondale B-BQ Pig out. Our freestyle
competitors got to thrill a crowd of over 500 people. It was pretty smooth.
We were invited back to perform at this event for at least the next two
years, so we are pretty excited about it for next fall already. It can only
get bigger and better. Hopefully see ya sooner than that. Here are the

This outdoor event will be the best in the Midwest. This festival will involve competitions in both Footbag and Flying Disc. This is a prize money footbag event! There is also a novice division for free! There will also be a solid intermediate bracket in all categories. We are again shooting for a separate women's division in all footbag categories. This event will also be the first event in a series of ICFL meetings in the fall of 1997. Come join us in the lands of Chaos--the grass is greener in Carbondale. It will be blast!

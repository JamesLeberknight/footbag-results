Events Offered: 
Freestyle Footbag Jam and Marathon Minutes.  With 
Enough interest, I will have a 45 second shred contest 
with 3 levels:  Novice, Intermediate, and Open.
This will be just a couple of days of great jamming in the great Okanagan. Come down and meet some cool people, and enjoy yourself. Kelowna City park is an amazing venue, as it is onlooking Okanagan Lake, with some of the best weather in BC. Hopefully the weather will cooperate, or the jam will move to Bankhead School.

Once you come into Kelowna, directions from the Welcome to Kelowna 
Sign to the Host Hotel are as follows:

Turn right on Abbott St.  It is the first road on the right after you come 
over the floating bridge.  Follow about until it turns into Lakeshore Rd.  
Keep on Lakeshore, it will take you into the mission area.  Pass the Dairy 
Queen on your left, then Gyro Beach is coming up on the right in the next 
minute, you cant miss it!  Gyro Beach Resort is on the left hand side!

Kelowna City Park is right on the left after coming across the floating 
bridge, so from the host hotel, just head back towards the floating bridge 
the same way you got there.  We will meet at Gyro first anyway just in 
case people are lost, and I will hand out maps of Kelowna.  If you arent 
coming from around these areas, please e-mail me, and I will put them up!

HOTEL INFO:

The Guest hotel will be Gyro Beach Resort, right across from Gyro 
Beach.  The rate for a room is $66.25 per night.  Rooms can have a 
maximum of 4 people.  Please book ASAP.  The number to call is 1-250-
762-3227.  I have booked 5 rooms for this event, but I guarantee it will be 
easy to find accomodations.  For those that are really tight on cash, 
please e-mail me and I will let you crash at my place.  You might have to 
bring just a tent and sleeping bag and crash in the back yard.  I can only 
house a couple people, so first come first serve.  Drop me an e-mail.

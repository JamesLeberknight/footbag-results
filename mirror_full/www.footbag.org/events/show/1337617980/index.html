The Footbag Dresden e.V. invites you for a weekend full of shredding, spiking and partying. We expect sunny weather, so the event is going to take place in Alaunpark, the favourite park in downtown Dresden with nice grass courts, a concrete freestyle area, and a huge audience. We reserved a quality gym with 7 net courts for the unlikely case of bad weather. Breakfast and water and fruits during the day will be provided.

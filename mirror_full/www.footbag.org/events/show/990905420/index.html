Come One - Come All ? to the 14th Annual Vancouver Open! We will be holding Open singles, Open doubles Net and Golf competition. If enough people show up we will have intermediate and/or novice divisions. For the possibility of a freestyle competition or shred, keep reading.

NET/GOLF EVENT

Entry Fees:

$30 for Open/Advanced with tournament shirt

$20 for Open/Advanced w/o shirt

$5 for novice w/o shirt

$15 for shirt only

If not enough registrants for novice, $5 fee will be refunded and there will be net courts available for pick-up games.

If there are enough registrants, it may be possible to run the Advanced or Intermediate or Womens categories, in singles or doubles. If there are not enough, then welcome to Open! :-)

Registration will be held from approx. 7pm to 9pm at the Sunset Grill Restaurant on Friday August 24th. Late registration will be held on site at Jericho Beach Park East from 8am to 9am on Saturday August 25th and (*) Sunday August 26th.

SCHEDULE OF EVENTS

Friday August 24th

5:00pm - until... Pick up net at Kits Beach
7:00pm - 9:00pm Registration at the Sunset Grill Restaurant

Saturday August 25th

8:00am - 8:30am Late registration at site
8:30am - until... Open singles
Saturday night social TBA

Sunday August 26th

8:00am - 8:30am Late Doubles Registration
9:00am - 3:00pm Open Doubles Pools and Brackets
3:00pm - 6:00pm Finals Matches/Awards Ceremony

Other Net events will run concurrently with the Open brackets.

The schedule is subject to change to accommodate other potential brackets.

FREESTYLE

As was discussed with Chard Cook. If enough freestylers show up, Chard will run a Freestyle competition at the tournament site. The number of competitors will dictate whether this is a formal or informal competition - maybe just a shredfest. This will in no way impede the progress of the Net tournament. That is to say that it may not be possible to compete in both Net and Freestyle as the schedules may conflict.

If you have any Freestyle questions, please call Chard Cook of the Canadian Footbag Alliance @ (604) 736-0384.

DIRECTIONS/MORE INFORMATION

The tournament takes place at the east section of Jericho Beach Park, the stretch of grass that borders upon 4th Avenue.

For directions or questions please call Jim Derricott at (604) 736-2716 or email jimijazzd@telus.net. A speedy reply will come your way.

Driving DIRECTIONS from U.S.A. to REGISTRATION & Kits Beach:
The I-5 takes you across the border and becomes the 99.  The 99 (not the 99A) 
crosses the Oak St. bridge and turns into Granville St. if you follow the "99" 
signs (if you don't follow the 99 signs, you will be on Oak St. - which is 
fine as well).
Keep going (about 60 blocks) until the intersection with Broadway.  Take a 
left onto Broadway, and continue for approx. 14 blocks, then take a right onto 
Yew St.
Follow Yew St. all the way down to the bottom and you are at Kitsilano Beach. 
 
Unless you see a good spot to park, the simplest is to use the Kits Beach 
parking: take a right onto Cornwall from Yew, then take the first left, and 
then left into the parking lot (do buy a parking ticket as they check often).
At this point you will see the nets set up at the Park within view.  Or
if it's after seven p.m., walk one block up Yew, and ten feet right on
York and there is the Sunset Grill where registration takes place.

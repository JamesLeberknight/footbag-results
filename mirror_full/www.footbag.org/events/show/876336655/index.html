Events Offered: 
We have *only* net events at our tournament.  The events will be Men's Open 
Singles, Men's Open Doubles, Women's Open Singles, Women's Open Doubles, 
Mixed Doubles, Intermediate Singles, and Intermediate Doubles.  The entry fee 
is $35.  There will be prize money.

This is our second annual net tournament. Last year we had 28 open competitors from Montreal, California, Seattle, Oregon, Texas, and many other places. The average temperature in Phoenix in January is 68 degrees and last year it was in the 70s. It is a beautiful time of year to come to Arizona, and it is an opportunity to enjoy some outdoor net fun! We expect many top competitors this year.

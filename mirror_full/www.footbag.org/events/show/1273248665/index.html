We are please to announce the annual NYFA Symposium will be taking place this year on Roosevelt Island in New York City. We are very excited to be offering both freestyle and net competition in a beautifully renovated gymnasium and well kept grass fields. Roosevelt Island is easily accessible by subway (F train) from Manhattan and Queens and offers gorgeous views of the Manhattan skyline.

Hello Friends, This years German Footbag Open will happen in Southern Germany close to Nürnberg. All players from the South of Germany and Europe don`t have to travel so far this year. The hosting club will be the FC Footstar Berlin e.V., working for the first time out of his hometown city Berlin. We are working close together with the organisers of last years Footbag Festival. What you can expect: Footbagevent, DJ`s, Live Bands, at least two dancefloors, Hip Hop, Ragga, "elektro boogie lounge" with tasty d n´b and breakbeatz. ...and of cause lots of shredding and spiking. Also there is a campground included in the eventarea for free camping. Hope to see you all at the beginning of July, for the "kick in" to the footbagmonth of the year 2003, European Footbagsummer July 2003. See this Event as a last chance to test your skills before the two major european happenings Euros and Worlds appear. YOU FIND MORE INFORMATION SOON ON THE EVENTS HOMEPAGE. ...choose da ball... Ole Schnack

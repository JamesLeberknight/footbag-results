DC All-Star's Fall Footbag Jam

Contact:

Jeff Bowling

Alias: shtataff@footbag.org
Location:
Alexandria, VA
USA
October 21-22, 2000  (concluded)
Location:
University of MD campus - McKeldin Mall
College Park, Maryland, USA
Host Club:
D.C. All-Stars
Events Offered: 
A weekend of net, freestyle, and good times.
To continue an new East Coast tradition, all are invited to DC for a weekend of footbag fun on October 21st and 22nd. It will be an informal gathering of mostly East Coast kickers. It's not a tournament so there are no fees involved. Plus, this will be a welcoming event for the DC area's newest kicker, Amy Noller!
Event Details:
Contact Jeff Bowling or Vince Bradley to let us know you are coming so 
we can figure out where you can crash (Jeff's house, Jason & Ann's 
house, and Neil's place).  We are all spread out around the DC 
metropolitan area either in the city itself or its surrounding suburbs in MD 
and VA.  Unlike Bob Riefer, none of us have the luxury of having a soccer 
field in our backyard so we will be kicking on the campus of the University 
of Maryland where there's lots of space and parking is plentiful.

The plan...
 
- You may come as early Friday night.  

- Saturday:  Starting around NOON, we kick all day (net and freestyle).  
We tried to get a badminton gym for indoor net in case the weather does 
not cooperate but could not so be prepared to play outside.  For the 
freestylers, there are plenty of indoor spaces on UMD's campus.

- Saturday night:  Party at my place - time TBA.  Maybe a group 
dinner/cookout at beforehand.

- Sunday:  Starting around NOON, we kick (net and freestyle) all day until 
you have to return home.

Directions to the University of Maryland:

FROM POINTS NORTH,
- Take I-95 South to I-495 (D.C.'s Capital Beltway).  
- On I-95, as you approach I-495, the road splits East or West.  
- Stay right (I-495 West) at the split, then take exit 27.  
- Follow signs to Exit 25 (U.S. Route 1 South toward College Park).
- Proceed approximately two miles south on U.S. Route 1.
- Turn right onto Campus Drive.
- Go around the big "M" continuing up the hill on Campus Drive.
- McKeldin Mall will be on your left as you are going up the hill.

FROM POINTS SOUTH,
- Take I-95 North to I-495 (D.C.'s Capital Beltway).
- Continue North on I-95/I-495 toward Baltimore.
- Take exit 25 (U.S. Route 1 South toward College Park).
- Proceed approximately two miles south on U.S. Route 1.
- Turn right onto Campus Drive.
- Go around the big "M" continuing up the hill on Campus Drive.
- McKeldin Mall will be on your left as you are going up the hill.

PARKING,
- You may park at any meter without paying since it's a weekend.  
- There are also parking lots on campus in which you may park for free - 
make sure you read the sign first!

If you are lost or confused, call Vince or Jeff's mobile phone.

Event
Hey Shredders,
We, a bunch of Estonians, Finns and a single Dane, had an awesome 
and very chill-outish event on the top-floor of the Tallinn RadissonSAS 
hotel on April 24th. After a hard day's shred with a view over Tallinn on the 
background, we relaxed in the sauna, puffing a water-pipe with tasty fruit 
tobacco and prepping for a night out on the town. Having had pizza and 
beer for late dinner, some cruising around the old town of Tallinn was 
done, which later turned into night-clubbing and night-pubbing. A perfect 
day it was and we can't thank RadissonSAS enough for enabling us to 
use their facilities. Shercle Family is looking forward to meet you all at 
Shercle Session vol 2!

On April 24th something unparallelled will be going down - or should we say up - in Tallinn, Estonia. That's when a new force in footbag, Shercle Family, will be hosting Shercle Session #1 - a shred sessions such as the footbag world has never seen before. Freestylers of the nearby countries will gather for a chance to spend eight unforgettable hours in the luxury of the RadissonSAShotel, sharpening their blades for the season in one of the highest possible locations in Tallinn - the 24th floor conference centre of the town's prime hotel.

Imagine a sweet carpet under the ever-shredding feet, the best shred tunes, a bunch of cool kickers, a stunning view over the old town and gulf of Tallinn, and a relaxing sauna after furious freestyling..
This session can't go wrong.
Event Details:
Venue

The RadissonSAS Hotel is situated smack-dub in the very centre of 
Tallinn, so you can't miss it, especially when you are accompanied by 
the local shredders. Just in case though, the address is Rävala pst. 3. 


Duration: 12.00 - 22.00

Participation fee: 100 EEK (?6 / $8) 
Accomodation: players house / hostel / hotel, contact for further 
information



The schdule of the session can be found below, but due to the free 
nature of the event, changes might occur.

12.00 Doors open

16.00 Shred 30

17.00 Ironman

18.00 Sick 3

19.00 SickTrick

20.00 Sauna (2 hours)

22.00 Prize-giving ceremony 

After-hours downtown




Warm-ups and after-hours are being dealt with, so get ready to spend 
a long weekend in Tallinn!



We are expecting to host players from the Baltic Sea countries but 
welcome anyone from anywhere. Shredders from Finland and 
Denmark have already shown interest in coming and we are expecting 
some big names. The maximum number of players who can attend the 
event is set to 30, as the room is simply not big enough for more. So 
be quick to let us know whether you are considering the trip!




Important!

RadissonSAS has provided us with a privilige to use one of the most 
exclusive conference halls available in Estonia. The offer includes a 
supply of drinking water for the participants, but it is crucial to 
acknowledge that consumption of personal bewerages and food is 
strictly forbidden within the hotel. Violation of these conditions will have 
serious results!

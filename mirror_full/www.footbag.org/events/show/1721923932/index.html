Manually Entered Results

The last thursday, 25th of july, the Basque Association of Beach Tennis and 
Minority Sports organized the V. Basque Tournament of Footbag Net (Doubles). We 
met in Aiega, an area of Ortuella (Basque Country) and we set up a court to play 
Footbag net. We got together 7 players and made 3 teams. We played three games at 
15 points each. The last game was very close and went to 21 points. 

Egoitz Campo and Eneko Aiega were the winning pair but the others did very well. 
Before the end of the year we will do one last tournament and say that possibly 
the new tournaments that we do from 2025 onwards, if everything goes well, will be 
official and will count for the world ranking. Hopefully so. 

These were the results:

Egoitz Campo/Eneko Aiega 15-11 Daniel Barrientos/Ander López
Joseba Fernandez/Lander Campo/Andi Erromo 15-12 Daniel Barrientos/Ander López
Egoitz Campo 12-10 Joseba Fernández/ Lander Campo/Andi Erromo

Classification of the V. Basque Tournament of Footbag Net

1-Egoitz Campo (Basque Country) and Eneko Aiega (Basque Country)
2-Joseba Fernández (Basque Country), Lander Campo (Basque Country) and Andi Erromo 
(Basque Country)
3-Daniel Barrientos (Basque Country) and Ander López (Basque Country)

Today the Basque Association of Beach Tennis and Minority Sports will organize a new Basque Tournament of Footbag Net, the fifht edition. We will meet in Aiega area, in Ortuella.

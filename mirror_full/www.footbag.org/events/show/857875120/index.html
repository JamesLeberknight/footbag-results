The 1997 WhoWhere? Western Regional Footbag Championships are now over!  We had
an *awesome* 3-day weekend event, with perfect weather, over 100
open/intermediate competitors, hundreds of spectators, and much more!

Believe it or not, this year's Western Regionals actually ended 1.5 hours
EARLY, despite the huge number of competitors -- we had 16 different events,
and awarded over 80 prizes.  Prize money was $1,600.00 dividing among 1st and
2nd place winners in open and women's events.

This annual event is one of the largest footbag tournaments in the world, situated on the beautiful campus of Stanford University in Palo Alto, California. It is a prize-money event with a full offering of net and freestyle competition for all levels of play, as well as free training and play for newcomers.

For more information, see the WESTERN REGIONALS HOME PAGE.

A full-time DJ and other fun activities make this a great way to spend your weekend -- even if you're not planning to compete. There will be a non-stop freestyle jam on the volleyball court right next to the DJ area. Footbags (and other products) will be available for purchase at the various vendor booths on site.

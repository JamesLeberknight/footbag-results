    What:
    Freestyle singles: Players choreograph a routine to music, and are judged on both technical difficulty, and artistic aspects. Open competitors have 2 minute routines and intermediate competitors have 1-1/2 minute routines.open/intermediate
    Shred 30: Players have 30 seconds to accumulate as high of a score as possible. The score is determined by number of moves, difficulty of moves, and variety of moves.open/intermediate
    Big 3: Players deliver the best 3 moves combos as they can, and the results are judged.open
    Circle Contest: players compete in a fashion that is made to emulate circle shred, which is known to be the best demonstration of what footbag really is.open

US OPEN RESULTS:

We had another great US Open! Nearly 40 registered net players in Open,
Intermediate, Novice, Women's, and Mixed divisions. The big story of the event
was our spectacular Women's net participation - 10 women competing, making it
the largest women's net tourny outside of worlds in many years. Thanks to the
women for rocking it! Due respect to our longest travellers: "Hacky Sack" Jack
Lentz from Erie, PA (new hall of famer!), Kevin Fura from PA, and the Texas
girls Kelly Kelley and Heather Thomas.

Many thanks also to our sponsors: Fancy Footwork, Widmer Brewing, Flying
Clipper, and Tim's Cascade Snacks. Kendall Kic worked her usual magic as
hospitality queen and we were all the better for it.

Make your pilgrimage to footbag mecca. Experience the great Northwest and play with legends. "The Great One" Kenny Shults has confirmed that he will play! Come to the U.S. Open!!
Event Details:
Dates are final.  Make your plans now.  You haven't experienced a footbag
summer until you've been to the Northwest!  Everyone welcome.

Beautiful waterfront park has been reserved.  Flat playing fields right on the
river and close to downtown.  Grocery and shopping center one block away. 
Easily the most convenient site on the footbag tour.

We are pleased to announce that FLYING CLIPPER is the official footbag of the
2009 US Open.  Each net court will be provided with a FLYING CLIPPER net bag
for use as the official tournament bag.

We are also very pleased to announce that WIDMER BREWING will be the official
beer of the US Open for the 5th year in a row!!  Thanks WIDMER!

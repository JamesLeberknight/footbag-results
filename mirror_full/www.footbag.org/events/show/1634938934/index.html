Tomorrow we will organize the II. Basque Open Footbag Net Touranment (Doubles) with players
from Basque Country, Venezuela, Nicaragua and Canarian Islands. We will make 3-5 pairs and
we will play semifinals and final rounds.


Manually Entered Results

The last saturday, 23th of october, we met 8 players to play Footbag Net in 
Barakaldo (Basque Country, in the field next to the football court of Zuazo). We 
played the II. Basque Open of Footbag Net (Doubles). Initially one player from 
Venezuela and another one from Nicaragua wanted to play but finally they couldn´t 
come.
We made 4 pairs and we played two semifinals and the final. Finally the best 
players were Egoitz and Alberto who made a good tournament. Here the results:

Semifinals
Egoitz Campo and Alberto Romero 15-11 Felipe Haase and Naiara Ortiz de Pinedo
Jesus Castro and Daniel Barrientos 15-9 Aitor Armiño and Andi Erromo

Final

Egoitz Campo and Alberto Romero 10-7 Jesus Castro and Daniel Barrientos

Last classification

1-Egoitz Campo (Basque Country) and Alberto Romero (Basque Country)
2-Jesus Castro (Canarian Islands) and Daniel Barrientos (Basque Country)
3-Felipe Haase (Colombia) and Naiara Oritz de Pinedo (Basque Country)
4-Aitor Armiño (Basque Country) and Andi Erromo (Basque Coutnry)

History of winners Basque Open Footbag Net (Doubles)

2019: I. edition Felipe Haase (Colombia) and Darlyn Mendoza (Basque Country)

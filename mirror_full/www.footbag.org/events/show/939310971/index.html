Free F.L.O.E will host a tournament in Eugene, OR, October 30-31st with a Halloween party on Saturday night at our house (costumes preferred and any effort is honored).  

As far as events this will be a net and golf tournament.  We may run everything doubles; doubles golf, mixed doubles net, doubles net, and possibly Ruler of the Court.  We are undecided at this point.  This will be a very FUN event and we will play hard.  We plan to keep entry fees minimal $5-10/person.  

Sorry for the delay in announcing a date.  Dennis and I have had family trips and plans change a few times for Sept and Oct which complicated things. 

We can arrange floor space if you let us know in advance.  There are affordable motels and hotels nearby also.  Hope you can make it.  Please drop me a line if you are thinking of coming.  Thanks,     

                                Becca

Event Details:
DIRECTIONS:
University of Oregon, Education Field is behind the Knight Library, next to 
cemetary, behind School of Education.  Turn into parking lot off 16th and 
Alder and go to the top lot.

SCHEDULE:

Saturday 10/30

Doubles Golf 

Mixed Doubles pending enough teams; Ruler of the Court otherwise

Singles Net

Novice Doubles Net (Arrive by 12:30p, play will start at 1:00pm)

All these events will run to completion if possible and until dark otherwise.


Sunday 10/31

Singles Golf.  The couse will be set up all day.  Players will need to play a 
round with at least one other person.  Best round wins.  You can play as much 
or little as you like.

Doubles Net.  Open and Intermediates start at 10:00am. Play through finals.

Novice Doubles at 1:0

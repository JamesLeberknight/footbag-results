2001 marks the 16th running of the Summer Classic. This tournament has been held in conjunction with the Kipona Street Fair on Labor Day weekend for seven years. The Fair draws hundreds of thousands of people. This tournament has a history of drawing large numbers of competitors, offering top prize money, offering tons of merchandise prizes, entertaining large crowds of spectators, and supplying the environment that allows compeititors and spectators to have a "funtastik" time.

Invitation Letter
To revieve your invintation letter that you will need for your visa,
please do the following:
1) Send an email to comradehostel@gmail.com and have "visa support
wi5" in the subject of your letter.
2) Attach a copy of your passport pages with all data 
3) State the dates of your arrival to Moscow and departure from Moscow.


Accommodation!!!
As far as accommodation goes, there are two options that we are offering to 
you. The first one is Comrade Hostel (www.comradehostel.com) located only 300 
meters away from metro, so it will not take you more than 30 mintues to get
to the event site. The rate is 600 rubles (around 15 euros) per night and your 
get a 10% discount in the "Ginger bar" located nearby. If you would like to 
make a reservation, please send an email to Mihail at 
mihail.muhortyh@gmail.com with "Comarade Hostel Accomodation" in the subject 
of your letter. May 30 is the last day to send your reservation request, so 
please do it as soon as you can since there are only 30 spots available in the 
hostel.
The other option is "Hotel Beta" (www.hotelbeta.ru). It is also around
30 minutes from the event location and the rate is 2400 rubles (around 60 
euros) per night. Please visit the website to find all the neccessary details.

The Brothers from Different Mothers and The Rain City Shred Footbag Club are proud to announce the 2nd annual Seattle Juggling and Footbag Festival sponsored by Flying Clipper Footbags. The only event of its kind anywhere in the world, the Seattle Festival was a huge success last year. This year there will be more workshops from the top professional footbag athletes and jugglers from across the nation. This will be the biggest footbag event in North America this year.

The highlight of this year's Festival is the big show on Saturday night called
Using All Four Limbs Featuring...
International Celebrity Artis the Spoonman
Clinton Holzhauer, The Russian Juggler
Christian Swenson Human Jazz
5 time World Footbag Champion Peter Irish
Juggling Comedians Brothers from Different Mothers
Music by The Mud Bay Stompers
Emceed by Comic Daredevil, The Checkerboard Guy
And Featuring Head to Head Footbag Competition
Event Details:
The Festival will take place at the Hec-Ed Pavilion on the University of 
Washington Campus on the 14th, 15th and 16th. The big show will be at Pigott 
Auditorium, Saturday November 15th at 8:00.

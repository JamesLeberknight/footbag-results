Manually Entered Results

The 3rd X-PRO Cup was a great tourney, about 30 participants played in the 
differents categories, really the participants showed a very technically game 
and a high level into the courts.

Mixed final was between Ivaneidy/Roberto and Jessica/Reinaldo, was a good final 
but in the end Iva and Roberto Win the game. Yalis the partner of Victor had a 
lesion, nothing to care about.

In open single there was very good games all the pools had good contenders, the 
final was again between Victor and Roberto, our two best singles players, 
showed us a very technical game and skills, Victor won in three difficult sets 
(24-22) (19-21) and (21-16).

In open doubles there were very strong couples, this time two couples from 
Táchira visit us but the final was disputed between Roberto/Eduardo and 
Elias/Nelson. Was a great final with many spikes, digs and defense, in three 
sets Elias and Nelson won, the score was (15-05), (10-15) and (15-10).

In general was a great tournament, all the players of the different categories 
showed the result of their training, with technical games, with offensive and 
defense abilities, I can say that every time is more difficult win a game.
Thanks to all members of the IFPA for supporting us.

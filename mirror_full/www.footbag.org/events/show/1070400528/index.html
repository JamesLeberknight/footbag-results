Location:
Milberry Union, UCSF Campus, 500 Paranassus
San Francisco, California, USA
Host Club:
Sponsored by UCSF Footbag Club and the University of California, San Francisco, Milberry Fitness Center, this is a free event for everyone. Spectators are welcome! No admission charge, just footbag fun for anyone interested. (Recommended ages, 13 and above.) This event takes place in the City Lights conference room behind the main reception desk just at the main entrance to the Milberry Student Union at the University of California, San Francisco (500 Parnassus, San Francisco).

Parking is available in the Milberry Union Garage (enter at 500 Parnassus), for a mere $1 (with validation from the Milberry Fitness Center).

Our special guest of honor is none other than the legendary Ale? Zelinka from Czech Republic.

This is a must-see event! The doors open on Sunday at 1pm, and the music and freestyle will go on for 4 or 5 hours. We'll end at 6 or 6:30pm. We'll have a special D.J. (none other than "el dopo" Ryan Mulroney) all day.

I hope everyone in California can make it there, to help us celebrate the winter season with some of the best freestylers in the world!

Event Details:
DRIVING DIRECTIONS:
------------------

Coming from areas north of San Francisco:
  Take US-101 south across the bay bridge, and continue on Park Presidio south through
  Golden Gate Park. Once you exit the park, you'll be on 19th Ave. You'll need to turn
  LEFT onto Lincoln Way, which is the first intersection after the park. (If you can't
turn left,
  use the old 3-rights rule.) Follow Lincoln Way (going east) until you get to 7th Ave.,
  and TURN RIGHT onto 7th Ave. Go a few blocks until you see Judah on your right,
  which is really Parnassus to your left. Turn LEFT (eastbound) onto Judah. Go through
  some stop signs and up the hill 'til you get to the UCSF Campus at the top of the hill.
  Watch for pedestrians. Turn LEFT into the Milberry Union garage just past the
pedestrian
  crosswalk (which has a light at it). You can miss it so be careful and go slow. There's
  a left-turn lane just where you need to turn. Watch for oncoming traffic; it's a little
  confusing the first time. The garage is directly under 500 Parnassus which is where the
  event will be. Park anywhere in the garage by following the signs for public parking,
and
  then find your way back to the main street level of 500 Parnassus. There's a big desk
  just inside the main entrance, and we are behind that desk (to the left). If you can't
  find us, ask at the desk about the "footbag event".

Coming from areas south of San Francisco:
  Take I-280 north all the way to San Francisco, and then follow the Golden Gate Bridge,
  19th Avenue exit (basically go straight while 280 veers to the right). However, that's
  where you stop following the signs to GG Bridge/19th. Instead of turning left with all
  the traffic onto 19th, you want to be in the RIGHT LANE and go STRAIGHT across the
  big intersection at 19th, continuing on Portola Rd. (northbound). Follow Portola Rd.
  until you get to a left-turn light at Claremont. Turn LEFT onto Claremont. (If you see
  Laguna Honda, you've gone too far. But you can turn left on Laguna Honda and meet
  back up with the other plan.) If you follow Claremont for a bit, going as straight as
  you can through a big traffic circle, you will merge with Laguna Honda. Go STRAIGHT
  at the light onto Laguna Honda, which takes you down a hill and you pass the hospital
  on your right and a transit station on your left. Follow that road through the valley
and
  it emerges as 7th Ave. (You don't turn; you'll just end up on 7th Ave.) Follow 7th Ave.
  for quite a few blocks until you get to Judah. The tricky part is that Parnassus
  changes names just around that intersection. You may see a sign for Judah or Parnassus.
  Take either one to the right. It will become Parnassus right away. (So, turn RIGHT onto
  Judah/Parnassus heading eastbound). Go through some stop signs and up the hill 'til
  you get to the UCSF Campus at the top of the hill.  Watch for pedestrians. Turn LEFT
  into the Milberry Union garage just past the pedestrian  crosswalk (which has a light at
it).
  You can miss it so be careful and go slow. There's a left-turn lane just where you need
  to turn. Watch for oncoming traffic; it's a little confusing the first time. The garage
is
  directly under 500 Parnassus which is where the event will be. Park anywhere in the
  garage by following the signs for public parking, and then find your way back to the
  main street level of 500 Parnassus. There's a big desk just inside the main entrance,
  and we are behind that desk (to the left). If you can't find us, ask at the desk about
the
  "footbag event".

Coming from anywhere else, use Mapquest and read the detailed part above as you get
to the Milberry Union building.

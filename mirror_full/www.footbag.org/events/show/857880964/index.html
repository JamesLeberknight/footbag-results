Events Offered: 
Novice, Intermediate and Pro categories, Mens and Womens divisions planned.
Novice start and finish on Saturday.
Intermediate start on Friday and finish on Saturday except finalists in net.
Pro's start on Friday and finish on Sunday.

Two rounds of freestyle for Pro's, and time spent on Net is not to exceed time
spent on freestyle, i.e. no freestyler persecution by casual omission of
freestyle event to be last.

Midwest Footbag Association


Have tons of fun at the longest running tournament in the Midwest. Every year we draw top pro's and intermediates from around the country!

Prize Money Event - Usually a $1,000.00 Purse
All prize money is disbursed between Net and Freestyle evenly, with doubles events getting the same amount. Prizes in all categories.

1,000 Kick-A-Thon to raise money for American Heart Association. Raffle to raise money for American Heart Association.

Event Details:
Pro players who wish to do consecutive events, must do so on Friday
night.  Pro Consecutives is done on Friday night, no exceptions.

Golf is Saturday morning starting promptly at 9:00 a.m.
Intermediate and Pro players who wish to do golf, must
be registered and present by 9:00 on Saturday or you will be scratched.  No exceptions.

Intermediate and Pro net begin immediately following Golf.
Novice net events start after intermediate and pro.

Saturday freestyle will cover Novice freestyle contest, and Intermediate Freestyle.
Saturday will be the first round for Pro freestylers.

All Novice players are done on Saturday.
All intermediate players, except finalists, are done on Saturday.

Players party is at the same place as last year.
Two for one pizza's on Sunday, place orders by 7:00.

Cool prizes for most money raised for AHA!

This event counts towards overall points in the Illinois
Collegiate Footbag League, Spring '97 Session.

Come to this event and kick it high with Schwa!

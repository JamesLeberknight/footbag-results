This is it boys and girls! The big one! The 25th Vancouver Open! We're celebrating over 25 years of Footbag in our fair city this year and we hope you'll all come and help us honour this quarter century anniversary in style. If you've been here before you know what to expect. If you haven't then ask someone who has or give us a call so well can fill you in and make sure you are well taken care of while you are here.

Manually Entered Results


The 2013 Mud Island Belt was a huge success. Teams were represented from
Portland, Erie, San Fran, Oakland, New Jersey, New York, Montreal, Chicago,
and the hometown boys from Memphis. This year the site was outdoors on
Saturday and indoors on Sunday. Singles took place first with Walt
defeating Chris and Jeremy defeating Ben to advance to the final. Doubles
pools took place on Saturday with Walt/Ben, Jeremy/Jack, Manu/Tuan, and
Chris/Ken advancing to semis. On Sunday Walt and Ben fell to Chris and Ken.
Jack and Jeremy fell to Manu and Tuan. Doubles final was set which led to
the reigning champs keeping their title. Congrats to Ken Shults and Chris
Siebert! Singles final ended with Walt beating Jeremy. Thanks to all the
competitors!

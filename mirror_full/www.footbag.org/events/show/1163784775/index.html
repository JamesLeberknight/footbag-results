The World Footbag Championships will be held in the Disney World Resort in 
Orlando, Florida and will be the biggest footbag event ever!  The Wide World
of Sports Complex is one of seven theme parks at Disney World and will be the
home of the 2007 IFPA World footbag Championships.

Go to:
http://www.disneyworldsports.com

Also check out Disney's event announcement:
http://disneyworldsports.disney.go.com/dwws/en_US/moresports/events/detail?name=MoreSports2007FootbagEventDetailPage

Tickets:
Tickets into the Wide World of Sports Complex can be purchaced at the gate.  
Day passes get you into the complex where several other sporting events will
all be going at once.  
Tournament participants and volunteers will be credentialed and get into the
complex for free.
Ticket Prices: Day Pass
$11.25 Adults, $8.50 Child (3-9), under 3 free.
Week pass; $37.00 Adults, $26.00 Child.

TRAVEL AGENT

The Worlds 2007 official travel agent is online and ready to help you
with room reservation, air travel discounts, rent a car and theme park
tickets.

Anthony Travel, based in Texas, will be acting as our travel agent.
They have been working with Disney for years and works with most of the
sporting events at the Disney sports complex.

Go to: http://www.anthonytravel.com/disney_footbag07.cfm
Follow the menu and instructions on our travel web page.

Anthony Travel will help with:

Room reservations:
We will be staying at the Disney Allstar Sports Resort on the campus of
Disney World.   We can reserve rooms for $65 per night with up to 4
occupants per room.  These rooms can be reserved at this price from
August 8th to August 21st.  If you plan on reserving any rooms before or
after the event dates (Aug. 11-18), you must call Anthony Travel (800) 736-6377
to verify that rooms are available.  It would be nice if everyone reserved rooms
though Anthony Travel because we have blocks of rooms reserved and the
accounting of those rooms will be easier.

Note:  There are many other Hotels available in the nearby area if you
have a problem with the Allstar Resort or want a kitchen or something
different.  Anthony can help you with that also or search the internet.

Air Travel:
Air Travel can also be reserved though Anthony by calling an agent and
talking out your situation.  If your club or combination of nearby clubs
have more than 10 players coming, you want to mention this and a 10% to
15% group discount is available.

Note:  I would also look at the internet for flights.  You may find a
better deal.

Rent a Car:
Discount Car reservations are available.  I encourage people to rent cars or
find a group of players that will share a car.  The transportation from the host 
hotel to the sports complex takes a long time and your timing requirements at 
this event could prove to be a problem.  This transportation freedom will enable 
you to enjoy the Orlando area.  Rental cars are cheap, between $35 compact to 
$49 Mini van per day.
  
Call Hertz at 1-800-654-2240 or book online at hertz.com and use with group No.CV#022L2383
for footbag discount.

Note:  Also check other, if you have one, discount memberships like AAA 
or Credit Union for possible discounts.

Airport Transportation to Disney Resort:
Disney's Magical Express will be available to bring you from the airport
to the Allstar Resort and back after the event.  You need to reserve
your place on the bus when you reserve your rooms.  It is not automatic.
These reservations need to be made before July 5, 2007.

Disney World Park Tickets:
We have special summer discount tickets for all the theme parks in
Disney World.  There are several different packages depending on how
many times you want to go.  Read carefully.
Note:You need to be part of this event to be eligible for this discount
as a player, staff member or volunteer.

QUESTIONS?
Please call Anthony Travel at (800) 736-6377.

I hope this helps everyone find discounts for travel and the ability to
come to this years IFPA World Footbag Championships.

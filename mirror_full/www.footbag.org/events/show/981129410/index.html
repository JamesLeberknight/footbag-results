This year the 2001ECFC will again participate as part of Maryland Day at the University of Maryland. Additionally, freestyle will again be held as part of the DC Congress of Jugglers Juggling Convention, also on campus.
The Kicking and Juggling will start the night of the 27th, and not finish until the night of the 29th.

Please use the online registration system provided by the International Footbag Players' Association, with financial services operated by the World Footbag Association, to register for this event before the pre-registration deadline of midnight, Thursday, April 26th.

PRE-REGISTRATION FEES
Open$50.00
Womens Open$30.00 Intermediates$30.00 Novice$15.00

Payment of these fees before the pre-registration deadline guarantees a players pack, t-shirt, and entry into any or all events for the level paid for, including events that would cost less.

Last day registration will take place at the director's house and in the Juggler's Gym on the 27th, but additional fees will be applied per event for last day registrations.

Directions
College Park is on the North-East side of Washington DC.

The University of Maryland has posted directions to the College Park
Campus.


College Park Metro is a fifteen minute walk from the net site, and a
half hour walk from the Freestyle site/Juggling Convention.

Last Day Registration  will occur Friday night with the start of the
Juggling Convention in room 2240 of the 
Health and Human Performance Building( HHP Building) on 
campus.
Please use the link for directions, and if you end up on campus
wondering where to go, many people refer to it as the 'North Gym'.

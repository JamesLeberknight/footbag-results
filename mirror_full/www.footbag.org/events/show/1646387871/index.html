Welcome to 41st IFPA WFC in Kraków, Poland! After 2-years covid break, be finally a part of great real event!

From July 27 to 31, 2022, at the Jordan Park, Wisla Sport Hall, Hype Park and in front of Bill Hickman bar in Kazimierz district, you will be able to cheer and personally take part in competitions where the main character is a small bal - in Poland often called zo¶ka, but all around the world known as Footbag.

https://worlds2022.footbag.pl/

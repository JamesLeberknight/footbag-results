We are pleased to officially announce the 2021 IFPA Online Freestyle Footbag World Championships! Like 2020, this online tournament will feature events like those that would be held at an in-person World Championships. However, players will now compete remotely from home and not together at the same venue. Due to the ongoing global pandemic situation, our community’s ability to travel and gather has been significantly hindered. So, for the second year in a row we are creating an online event that we hope will reach the entire world of Freestyle Footbag players. This event is being run by the International Footbag Players Association (IFPA) and will be guided by the highest standards of organization and judging. Official Hashtag: #FootbagWorlds2021
Event Details:
This event is officially sanctioned by the IFPA, and the winner of each

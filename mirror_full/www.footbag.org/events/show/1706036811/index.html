Thank you everybody !!! Merci à tout le monde !!!


The footbag club in Montreal is happy to host again the World Championships !

This year we are preparing an outdoor event in the wonderful neighbourhood of Hochelaga-Maisonneuve. You ready ?! So pack your shorts, strap your shoes and bring your game ON !!!

Le club de footbag de Montréal est fier d'accueillir à nouveau les Championnats du monde !

Cette année, nous préparons un tournoi extérieur dans le superbe arrondissement d'Hochelaga-Maisonneuve. Z'êtes prêt.es ?! Zouzous les kikis !!!

Event Details:
Net events

Please take note of the following scoring systems in Montreal :

Doubles and Mixed will be played in SIDE-OUT ;

Singles will be played in RALLYE.

More details will follow.




Freestyle events

The following events will take place (* only if enough participants) :

Request contest ;

Sick 3 ;

Last man standing ;

Battles (Open, Women*, Intermediate*) ;

Circle contest (Open, Women*, Intermediate*) ;

Routines (Open, Women*, Intermediate*, Doubles*, Doubles mixed*).

Routines length will be 90 seconds for ALL categories, EXCEPT for doubles 
which 
will be 120 seconds.  The buffer for shorter/longer routines is 7 seconds.

PLAYERS please send your songs for your routines at worlds2024music@gmail.com


Unofficial events : 2 square and Golf

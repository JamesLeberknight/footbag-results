Manually Entered Results

Thank you to all the great footbaggers who competed in the Northern Illinois
Footbag Open this past weekend.   The weather was good and the competition
was good also.    Thank you to the sponser of the tournament, The Medicine
Shoppe of DeKalb, who donated cash for this event.   The total amount of the
prize pool was $ 400, pretty good for Malta, don't you think?  Special
thanks to Juli Smith for providing beverages and lunch on Saturday, and
fruit on Sunday morning for all competitors.

Extra thanks to our international competitor, Justin Sexton, who rearranged
his schedule to attend this tournament before returning to Finland.   Extra
thanks also to Tony and Jeremy Glick from Cleveland, OH for attending, and
forsupplying donuts on Sunday.

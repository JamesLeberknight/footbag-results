Event Details:
This is a one-day event, with the second day (Sunday) just a freestyle jam in Aotea 
Square in downtown Auckland.

The first day is in Herne Bay Racket Club, starting at 1pm. Directions are as follows:

From State Highway 1, take the Fanshawe St. Exit, and go right onto Beaumont Street. 
Follow that until it T's at the Foodtown, and turn right onto College Hill. Continue until

College Hill turns into Jervois Rd. (no turns), then eventually turn left onto West End
Rd. 
Herne Bay Racket club will be on the left soon thereafter.

Look for us in the racquetball courts or in the main lounge area.

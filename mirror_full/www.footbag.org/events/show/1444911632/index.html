important Information:

Due to the short time and the refugee situation in Frankfurt we could not get
a hall big enough.
The sports hall from the Frankfurt University of Applied Sciences is very very
small!!
We have only 3 Courts and they are very close together and the ceiling is not
very high.
Means we have to expect a lot of "Bag on" and we have a tight schedule to have
as many games as possible.
Maybe some of the Consolation games must be canceled!

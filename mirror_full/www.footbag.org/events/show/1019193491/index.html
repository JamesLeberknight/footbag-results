Event Details:
Team Finnsta will host a two-day footbag event 
in Helsinki, Finland, on the 15th and 16th of June!! There will be both net 
and freestyle events for open players, so people, start 
making plans for the summer, because the best players from northern 
Europe 
are coming to one place on the 15th & 16th of June: Helsinki. It's 
definitely the place to be, especially on summertime. So, mark your 
calendars and book your flight, train, boat, bus or whatever-tickets to 
Finland... now. 

Something to highlight of the event itself: We have got an insane place in 
the heart of Helsinki for the 2nd day of the event: an old warehouse that's 
been modified into a huge party house which is known for hosting many 
lifestyle and Funk and Rap -oriented partys. We are going to hold the 
freestyle finals and net exhibitions at the site on Sunday.


Be there !!

Event Details:
The Bay Area Footbag League are the proud hosts of the 2002 World 
Footbag Championships (WFC).  We are pleased to be presenting the  
WFC for the second year in a row and are looking forward to hosting all 
of you on our home turf.

The dates are August 5-11, 2002. The sites and host hotel have been 
finalized.  They are all located in The City (San Francisco).  

Please stay tuned to the footbag announce email list and 
2002 World Footbag Championships Home 
Page 
for regular updates and more information.  

If  you have any questions, please feel free to contact us by e-mailing 
worlds@footbag.org.

See you there,

Julie Symons, Lisa McDaniel, and Eric Wulff

*REGISTRATION NOW OPEN UNTIL 15/3/09. GO TO THE WEBSITE, CLICK 'REGISTER' AND ENTER YOUR DETAILS.*

Its on again! And for the first time, the Australian National Footbag Championships will be hosted by the Sydney crew. Our host this year is the Royal Easter Show, attended by thousands of people each day during its two week run at Sydney Olympic Park, Homebush.

We will be welcoming international players such as Andrew Grant (CAN - 2nd Place Shred30, Worlds 2008) and Phil Morrison (NZ - 6th Place Routine, Worlds 2008) and hopefully several more, as well as all the familiar Aussie talent such as Jeremy O'Wheel (Multiple Australian Champion, World Record holder).

Besides our event hosts, the Royal Easter Show, we have Youth Hostels Australia onboard as Accommodation Sponsors, and Elemental Footbags has just joined us to help provide some much deserved prizes for our competitors. So get practicing!

Exact scheduling details are TBC, however a lose idea follows:

Friday 17th April: Meet and greet, sight-seeing, BBQ, Footbag Golf. Players party in the evening.

Saturday 18th: All registered attendees will receive a full day pass to the Easter Show, and the competition begins at 15.00hrs, for one hour. Events to be held here will be Shred30, Request Competition, Shred Battle and possibly Sick3, in front of a 500+ capacity crowd.

Sunday 19th: We move to McMahon's Point community hall for the Routine and Circle competition events, as then all-out shred and awards ceremony.

All those wishing to attend the show, and/or compete, will be required to register and pay entry fee of $30 which will cover Show entry, all events over the two days, and membership to the Australian Footbag Organisation.

Check the Website for the event for more information on Accommodation (get in quick for discounted hostel bed thanks to YHA!), Scheduling, Merchandise and Event details.

The Boise Footclan is proud to announce the 2016 US Open Freestyle Footbag Championships in beautiful Boise ID. This event will be held in the heart of the city and display the best freestyle footbag has to offer. Many of North America's top players will be in attendance, there will also be intermediate and beginner events as it goes. More information to be announced, we look forward to seeing you in the circles!

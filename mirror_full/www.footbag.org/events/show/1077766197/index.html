THIS IS NOW A PRIZE MONEY EVENT. Thank you to our main sponsor SUNY New Paltz (State University of New York; pronounced SOONNEE). This is going to be an excellent time and I invite you all to join me in my fun. We have some excellent fields for net and some tennis courts for freestyle. Also reserved are some indoor accomidations for freestyle such as a gymnasium and a large carpeted room. New Paltz is about an hour and a half North of NYC, hopefully conveniently located between the middle colonies and French Canada. This is a beautiful town with a lot of youthful energy so I think that it would make an ideal location. Come to New Paltz April 24 and have a blast with me, not to mention get into some tournament shape before this summer's exciting schedule. Cheers Friends! - Benny
Event Details:
Since our main sponsor is SUNY New Paltz, cash prizes will be mailed 
directly from the school in the form of a check following the event.
Thanks to Freedom Footbags, Lonn Irish, and James Hermey we will have many 
other great prizes (bags, T's, and videos) to hand out the day of the event. 

SCHEDULE OF EVENTS

SATURDAY
Player registration and warm-up begins at 9 a.m. and end around 6 p.m.
Net matches begin promptly at 11 a.m.
Freestyle events will all be held in the early afternoon on Saturday so that 
we may free up the rest of the day and Sunday for Shredding.
Players Party begins around 8 p.m. (see below for more details)

SUNDAY
Net matches begin promptly at 10 a.m. and end when one person stands 
victorious
Freestyle fun all day.

Entry Fees:
Open:   $15 (includes entrance to any Open event and Players Party)
Intermediate:     $10 (includes entrance to any Intermediate event and 
Players Party)
SUNY New Paltz Students Enter Free with Valid Student ID.

Players Party will be held at Brad Kaplan's House on Saturday Night.  
Directions will be handed out at the event site.  Food and Drink and late 
night shred will be on hand.  Non-competitors are asked to put in $5 towards 
food and drink. 

Some Floor space is STILL available; please call or e-mail for info.  

THE HOST HOTEL:
Super 8
Terwilliger Lane
New Paltz, NY 12561
845-255-8865

ROOMS are $59.85 + tax per night.
This is the corporate rate.  When you make your reservations make sure to 
tell them you are attending a function at SUNY New Paltz and they will quote 
you this rate.
We do not have blocked rooms so please make reservations as soon as possible.
Without a credit card they will hold your room until 6pm the day you check 
in.  With a credit card they will hold your room through the whole night.

DIRECTIONS TO EVENT SITE AND HOTEL FROM NORTHERN AND SOUTHERN NEW YORK
(PAY ATTENTION TO ALTERNATE FREESTYLE SITE DIRECTIONS IN THE EVENT OF RAIN)
New Paltz is right on Interstate 87.  
Connecting with I-87 to the South are: I-287 at Exit 15a and I-84 at exit 
17.  
Connecting with I-87 to the North is: I-90 at exit 24 or 25.     

Take I-87 (NYS Thruway) North or South to Exit 18 (New Paltz/Poughkeepsie).

After Toll Plaza turn left onto Route 299 West (Main St. in New Paltz)

(Host Hotel: Once on Route 299 take left at first light onto S. Putt Corners 
Rd. then IMMEDIATE left on Terwilliger Lane.  Super 8 Hotel is on the Left)

(Event Site)
From the Highway ramp, take left towards New Paltz

At 3rd traffic light turn left onto Route 32 South (S. Manheim Blvd.) (From 
Hotel, take left at light and go two more lights.)

Park in Lot 32 on the left side of the road.  When you reach the street, 
turn 
left and walk 30 seconds.
The Event site is a tennis court and grass area on the SUNY New Paltz Campus 

Approximately 3/4 mile (from Main Street) on the right hand side.  In the 
event of rain we will 
move to an indoor location on the campus

ALTERNATE LOCATION IN EVENT OF RAIN (Elting Gymnasium on SUNY Campus)
 
From the Highway ramp, take left towards New Paltz

At 4th traffic light turn left onto Route 208 South (S. Chestnut St.) (From 
Hotel, take left at light and go 3 more lights.)

SUNY entrance is your 3rd left hand turn... TAKE IT.

is on the right hand side and parking is on the left.

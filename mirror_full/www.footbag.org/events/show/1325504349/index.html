The EUROS were fun ? thanks to our friends from Aachen! But it is time to move on ? to Bembel Cup 2012 in Frankfurt! Since we were experiencing troubles moving our homepage from one provider to another, this year will have to work without the internet!

Since most of you have been to Bembel Cup before ? it is same same as always, the locations haven't changed. Here ist our schedule, if you have any problems, pls call me or send an e-Mail (bartsch@bembel.de).

By the way: It will be sideout-scoring as in Aachen :-)

2012 Schedule and adresses:

Please notice: In case of heavy rain, the event will take place at the Mehrzweckhalle Kalbach (Am Martinszehnten 2, 60437 Frankfurt), as in years before. All events listed below for the Brentanobad will then switch to the gym! If lost, call Alex (+49-170-3453998)

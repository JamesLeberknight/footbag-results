Registration and payments

In a few days an online registration will be available. Every player must
register online! Please register only if you're sure you'll come to the
event. You'll have to check-in on site and pay your registration fee. It will
be 20 PLN for every player. You don't need to pay if you play only Golf or 2
Squares.

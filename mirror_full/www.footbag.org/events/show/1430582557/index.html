Hi Buddies!
Greetings to all Footbag net community, in this opportunity is a pleasure for 
me announce the final results about our last tournament in Venezuela...
The event was developed in great shape despite had many problems weeks before 
with the location. Many players showed a great performance E.G Diego Chaves has 
shown amazing skills in doubles and single too! But the MVP was Daniel Ernesto 
Gonzalez who reached to win one set against Victor Lezama, in fact him got 
fifth 5th place in doubles and singles!
After almost 6 years Oscar Loreto & Roberto Da Silva have not been playing 
together, but they were showed a nice combination on the court. They have won 
the doubles final in two set against the last champs.
We are very proud and enthusiasm in next competitions because there are many 
new players with huge potential and skills!!!
We held a tournament for keeping alive the flame of ours passion.

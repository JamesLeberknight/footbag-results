The AkiSphere is undoubtedly the most beautiful trophy of the footbag universe. To deserve it, the winners must compete against the finest players in the world. Last year, Gilles Demers and François D.-Pelletier won after a memorable final. Who will have the honor this year to kiss the big shiny ball? Come, Americans, Europeans, South Americans, we pride ourselves in taking care of you (on and off the court)!
Event Details:
Open: 40$CAN. Intermediate: 20$CAN with T-Shirt, 10$CAN without T-Shirt

La Fontaine Park, facing Notre-Dame Hospital
 
Friday July 1: Singles prelims only. Registration at 17:00. 
Saturday July 2: Late registration for doubles at 11:00.
Sunday July 3: Last game ends around 18:30

Montreal players. There is room for everyone!

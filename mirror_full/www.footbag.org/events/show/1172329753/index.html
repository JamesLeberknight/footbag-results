People of Footbag, Years ago the Estonian footbag community arranged notorious footbag camps known for their hardcore shred and.. cheap socials. In Vinni, a small town near to the middle of nowhere, Estonians honed theis skills with fellow tribesmen from Finland. Fewer years ago a star event - Shercle Session - rose to the skies of the European bean bag scene. From the players to the players, a luxurious event in the Party Capital of Year (New York Times said so!) - Tallinn - took place. Shredding on the top floor of Tallinn's prime hotel RadissonSAS and hitting the nicest of clubs, a swell time was had by many. It is now high time time to combine the two glorious events and invite y'all to Shercle Session #3 - Estonian Footbag Camp. Over the weekend of April 6th-8th we expect around 20 playas from Estonia, Finland, Germany, Denmark, and anywhere else to gather in Vinni Sports Complex (www.vinnisport.ee/index.php?lang=eng) for a long weekend of heinous shred, sharp spikes, and ultimate chill-out. Expect full-day shred and net sessions, good old bunker-bed accomodation, cheap prices, included meals spiced up with a swimming pool, a gym, Estonian sauna, and perhaps visits to night clubs of a bigger small town known as Rakvere. This is to be preceded by a footbagging Thursday in Tallinn, including a nice warm-up shred and a tasting of unparalelled night life. International partcipants should therefore plan to arrive on Thursday April 5th and depart on Monday April 9th to get the best of the weekend.

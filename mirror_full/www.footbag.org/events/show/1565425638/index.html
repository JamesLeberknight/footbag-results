Our event "Swiss Footbag Open", 29th-30th October 2022, was a great success with 
23 net players from 5 countries and 8 freestyle players from 3 countries, 
wonderful weather, a great friendly atmosphere and a memorable raclette-cheese 
meal and Party on Saturday.
The level of play was high, thanks to the presence of former and current world 
champions that have been highly challenged from other very good players and 
teams!

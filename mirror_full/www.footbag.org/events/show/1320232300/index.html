Manually Entered Results


The RNH Contest 2011 was big fun. Like last year it was an international 
tournament with competitors from several European countries (Bulgaria, France, 
Germany, Sweden and Russia, but no Swiss players?)

Saturday ? Doubles Net ? classic side-out scoring

10 teams competed in 2 pools with top 3 making to the next round. 
In 1/4-finals Quentin Isackson (RNH) and Guillaume Degat (Nantes) beat seed 4 ? 
Vincent Rousseau and Marco Brunet.
No surprises occurred in 1/2-finals. Seed 1 ? Arthur Ledain and Florian Götze 
beat the up-setters Quentin and Guillaume. Greg Lima and JF Lemieux (seed 2) won 
easily against Lino Landau and Grischa Tellenbach (seed 3) ? sorry again for our 
worst tournament performance this year.
The bronze medal went to Lino Landau and Grischa Tellenbach over Quentin 
Isackson and Guillaume Degat.
Eventually the gold medal was taken by Arthur Ledain and Florian Götze over Greg 
Lima and JF Lemieux.

http://www.dailymotion.com/video/xmuszz_dn-final-game-3-4_sport
http://www.dailymotion.com/video/xmuu9o_dn-final-game-4-4_sport

Sunday ?Singles Net ? rally scoring

14 players competed in 4 pools with top 2 making it to the next round. There 
were 2 up-sets in the pools: Olivier Baillon beat Miquel Clemente in 2 straight 
games and Quentin Isackson (this guy again) beat Marco Brunet. The top 4 seeds 
went through 1/4-finals in 2 straight games.
We had decided that 1/2-finals and finals would be played best of 5 games to 15 
points. Arthur Ledain beat Seb Maillet in 4 games and Greg Lima beat Lino Landau 
in also 4 games. Seb Maillet beat Lino Landau in 5 games for the bronze medal 
and Arthur Ledain beat Greg Lima in 4 games for the gold medal.

Thanks to all players who participated at the 9th edition of the RNH Contest and 
see all you next time.

Dear footbag players,

The registration fee for the RNH Contest 2011 is 10.00 Euros (which includes access to the tournament & some food and beverages).

Saturday: Tournament play starts at 11h00. We will play ALL doubles net matches including finals and all consolation rounds. Scoring system: Side-out scoring best of 3 games to 11 points. ˝ finals and finals best of 3 games to 15 points. Matching outfits: Doubles net teams are requested to wear matching outfits i.e. each team should at least have matching shirts. The higher seeded team chooses its colour.

Sunday: Tournament play starts at 11h00. We will play ALL singles net matches including finals and all consolation rounds. Scoring system: Rally scoring best of 3 games to 15 points. ˝ finals and finals best of 5 games to 15 points. Reminder: Everybody has to be outside the gym with all their stuff at 17h59 !!!

Venue: Gymnase Choisy 4bis Avenue de Choisy 75013 Paris, France

Directions

From Ferry - Driving

  • Drive off ferry onto highway and keep going straight until you hit town and it turns into Blanshard Street.
  • Turn left on Hillside and drive approximately six very long blocks. Until you reach Richmond Avenue.
  • You'll see Lansdowne Junior High school on your right (you can park there for free), keep going another block and Camosun College is on your left.
  • The tournament is in the gymnasium on the first floor of the Young Building

From Ferry - Bus

  • Take the #70 Downtown bus to downtown Victoria - ask the driver for a transfer when you get on. The fare is $2.75.
  • Get off at the Bay Center (the bus will practically emtpty at this stop)
  • Walk back (north) one half block and catch the #14 UVic bus in front of Chapters.
  • Ask the driver the let you know when you get the Camosun College
  • When you get off the bus, walk up the long stairs and the building directly on your right is the building. If you don't see a bunch of freestyler's outside, go in the building and down to basement where it will be obvious where we are!

From Seattle Clipper

  • Walk out of building and turn left on Bellville.
  • Keep going straight and it will curve to the left and turn into Blanshard.
  • Walk up Blanshard about 5 blocks to Fort St. and turn right.
  • There is a bus stop right on the corner there.
  • Wait for the #14 UVic bus
  • Ask the bus driver to notify you where you need to get off for Camosun College
  • When you get off the bus, walk up the long stairs and the building directly on your right is the building. If you don't see a bunch of freestyler's outside, go in the building and down to basement where it will be obvious where we are!

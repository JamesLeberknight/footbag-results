Events

  • Open Singles/Doubles Freestyle - This division is for participants who do not wish to compete in any of the other divisions. If you are not already a member of the IFPA, then you must pay $10 for the membership costs to compete in this division.
  • Open Mixed Doubles Freestyle - This division is for participants who do not qualify for any other division or do not wish to compete any other division. If you are not already a member of the IFPA, then you must pay $10 for the membership costs to compete in this division.
  • Intermediate Singles/Doubles Freestyle - This division is for participants who are not considered pros and maybe have just started freestyle.
  • Open Singles/Doubles/Team Consecutive - This division is for participants who do not wish to compete in any of the other divisions. IF you are not already a member of the IFPA, then you must pay $10 for the membership costs to compete in this division.
  • Novice Singles/Doubles/Team Consecutive - Division for participants who have never competed or are considered begginers.
  • Intermediate Singles/Doubles Net
  • Open Singles/Doubles Net
  • 4-Square - This is a 4-Square competition for all types of participants.

Pre-Event Information

The 1995 Mattel Sports World Footbag Championships will be held in Menlo Park and San Francisco, California. A preliminary schedule is below. (Click here for a detailed player's schedule.)

Preliminary rounds and semifinals (July 31-August 5) will take place at the Burgess Park facility in beautiful Menlo Park, California. FInals will be held on August 6 in Peacock Meadow of the world famous Golden Gate Park in San Francisco.

If you are interested in entering this year's event, print out, complete, and mail in the ENTRY FORM as soon as possible.

If you are coming, consider getting pledges for the Cystic Fibrosis Kick-A-Thon by filling out the enclosed PLEDGE SHEET.

1995 Mattel Sports World Footbag Championships
Summary

July 31-August 5: Burgess Park, Alma St. behind 501 Laurel St., Menlo Park, CA
August 6: Peacock Meadow, Golden Gate Park, 1 block E. of Conservatory of Flowers

July 31 (Monday): Registration and consecutives events

Preliminary Rounds

August 1 (Tuesday): Singles Net preliminary rounds
August 2 (Wednesday): Mixed Doubles Net preliminary rounds

9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.: Net Semifinals

10:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.: Net FINALS - in Golden Gate Park

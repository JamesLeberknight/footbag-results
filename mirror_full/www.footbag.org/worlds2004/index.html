 august 01 

Manu defends the title! In a tight, final match, Emmanuel Bouchard narrowly defeats returning contender Kenny Shults, holding on to win his 7th consecutive world champion title in Singles Net. Julie Symons wins gold in Women's Singles, defeating Montrealer Geneviève Bousquet. Team Chaos - P.T. Lovern and John Leys - wins gold against Emmanuel Bouchard and Patrick Asswad in Open Doubles. Full results coming soon.


 july 31 

Freestyle Finals goes BOOOOM! La Résistance packed the house tonight (Saturday), putting on a monumental footbag show, as Vašek Klouda takes gold in Singles Freestyle, followed close by Honza Weber and Gordon Bevier- all 3 with dropless performances!!! Vasek tops out Shred:30, and Toni Pääkkönen rips open the Sick3 contest with a Beast: Blurry Symposium Whirl > Whirlwind > Spinning Paradox Blender! Carol Wedemeyer defends her title against up-and-comer Verena Lenneis of Austria. Full story and Video highlights coming soon.

SOUND FOOTWORK
CLUB OF SEATTLE

Sound Footwork is a community club designed to promote the sport of footbag. We hold weekly practices throughout the year, host annual local footbag tournaments and events, publish the "Kickin' News" newsletter, and maintain a 24 hour automated hotline. We welcome anyone interested in playing or learning about footbag.

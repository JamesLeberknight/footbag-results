FootbagDenmark is the central footbag organization in Denmark, initiated by experienced players. The purpose of the organization is to make footbag more recognised and more popular in Denmark. We encourage and support everybody, to start their own regional or local footbag club. FD hosts the national championships and represents Denmark in EFC with two members. The club is located in Copenhagen where most activities take place. We are approx. 35 members and more to come. The style is new as well as old school, and you can find everything from beginners to champions among FD?s members.

Footbag.org Feedback

Report a problem with Footbag.org:

Having trouble signing in, or wish to delete your account?
   You should use our account help form instead of the form below.

You can use this form to report a problem you're having with Footbag.org.

Please understand that any time you submit this form, you are creating a request for someone to do work.

Also, please understand that Footbag.org is a "labor of love" and that there are very few people who work on it; nobody is paid to do this work. It's all voluntary. So, please use a positive tone, be respectful, show your thanks, and give as much information up-front so that you're not wasting a volunteer's time researching things you could have just said to begin with.

The form below attempts to ensure you will follow this request, but nothing is perfect. So please do your part to help us help you.

1. Summarize your problem in English, using just a few sentences:
(Example: "I am having trouble adding my event to the event list; every time I do it, I get an error (below). The URL is below as well. I think I need your help with this, thanks very much.")
2. Type in (or copy from your browser) the exact address (URL) of the page in question on our site:
(Please make sure you have everything, not just part of the address. For example, if you are having trouble understanding instructions, or you received an error, paste the exact URL of the page here, e.g., http://www.footbag.org/foo/bar/?x=5)
(Leave the section above blank if no specific page to refer to.)
3. If you got an error message, please copy and paste the entire error message here:
(I.e., go to the browser page where you got the error, select the entire contents of the page with your mouse, and then use the "copy" command in the Edit menu to copy the text, then "paste" it here.)
(Leave above blank if no error message.)
4. Now, this is the most important part: explain step-by-step exactly how you got into this mess!
(We can't guess! Try to remember as much detail as possible, and give us step-by-step instructions so that we can attempt to reproduce this same problem on our end -- that way, if we can get it to happen, we can fix it much easier. If the problem isn't reproducible, or is specific to your account, then leave this blank.)
Note: If the problem was not on your behalf, but on someone else's, please make sure their name and user ID are in the summary above!
5. Submit this problem report by clicking the Submit button.
An e-mail will be sent to the administrator, and you'll get a confirmation after you submit the form.
Note: We will e-mail the confirmation to davidleberknight@gmail.com. MAKE SURE YOUR E-MAIL ADDRESS IS RIGHT! If your e-mail is not davidleberknight@gmail.com, please click here to edit your profile and fix it before you hit submit!

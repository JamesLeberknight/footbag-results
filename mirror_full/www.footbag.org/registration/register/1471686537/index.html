Master Singles Net
For players 40 and older. (Note: in the current rule book [12th edition, April 2010], the "master" division starts at age 35, and the "grand master" starts at age 45. However, IFPA is considering changing these classifications in a subsequent edition of the rule book. Pending that official change, we are testing whether offering a single "master" division starting age 40 and up will serve the footbag community better. So please bear with us for this experiment, and we apologize to any players who wanted to compete in the "master" division this year but who will not be aged 40 or more by the time Worlds begins; in that case you will need to choose another division, e.g. "open", "women's open", or "intermediate" as applicable.)

FREESTYLE MOVE LIST

3-Add Moves  
Barrage
ba-razh'
3 adds 		 
SET > OP IN [DEX] > SAME IN [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]

A double dexterity mirage. Set from either clipper or toe, then quickly plant the setting leg and bring the support leg over the bag from in to out then over again to land to support a toe delay with the original setting foot.

Butterfly*
3 adds 		 
SET > SAME or OP OUT [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

A leg over straight to a clipper delay. E.g., from a right toe set the footbag up and slightly towards yourself. Hop off your right leg and pass your left leg over the footbag- delay the footbag with the inside surface of the right foot in a cross body position ie a clipper delay.

 		  [dex] [xbd] [del]
Butterfly Kick
3 adds 		 
SET > JUMP [BOD] > SAME or OP OUT [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD]

A leg over straight to a flying clipper. E.g., from a right toe delay, set the footbag straight up and slightly towards yourself. Hop off the right leg and pass the left leg over the footbag- Hit the footbag with the inside surface of the right foot and then in this case it is easiest to land on the left leg.

(Cross-Body Sole Delay)
3 adds 		 
SET > FLAP [UNS] [XBD] [DEL]

A cross body sole delay. It is easiest to get a flat surface if you point you knee straight down.

Double Around the World
3 adds 		 
TOE > SAME IN [DEX] > SAME IN [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]
or
TOE > SAME OUT [DEX] > SAME OUT [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]

Circle the footbag twice between toe delays. Pointing your toes down will prevent the circling leg from snagging the footbag as you come around for the second time.

Double Leg Over
3 adds 		 
SET > OP IN [DEX] > OP OUT [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]

A leg over straight after a mirage. The mirage can be set either from a clipper or a toe delay. From the set hop off the support leg then the setting leg and pass both over the footbag and finally catch the footbag with the setting foot on a toe delay.

Double Pickup
3 adds 		 
CLIP > OP IN [DEX] > SAME IN [DEX] > SAME TOE [DEL]

Basically an in to out double around the world set from a clipper delay. E.g., from a left clipper delay, set the bag just above waist height. Quickly plant the left leg and circle the footbag from in to out twice landing on right toe delay.

Drifter
3 adds 		 
SET > OP IN [DEX] > SAME CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

A miraging clipper delay. From a clipper- quickly plant the setting leg and bring the support leg over the bag from in to out and in one motion- keeping this leg close to the other leg- into a clipper delay on the other side. Or, setting from left toe, quickly plant the left leg and bring the right leg up and around the bag from in to out. Turn slightly in the direction of the dexterity (in this case, to the right) to help bring the right foot back around into the cross body position to end the move on a right foot clipper delay.

Paradox
Illusion
3 adds 		 
CLIP > SAME OUT [PDX[DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]

A Paradox Revese Mirage. From a clipper set, set the bag about waist high. Turn toward the bag and complete an out to in dexterity with the setting foot. Complete the move by catching the bag on your opposite toe.

Paradox
Mirage*
3 adds 		 
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]

E.g., from a right clipper delay, set the bag so it's moving across your body from left to right, then bring your right leg around the left leg and around the footbag as it falls. You must in this case circle the footbag from the left of the footbag to the front and finally to the right ending on a left toe delay. Basically the paradox leg completes a tight sharp 'S' shape with the support leg in the first loop and the footbag in the second loop of the letter 'S'.

Mirage*
3 adds 		 
SET > (no plant while) OP IN [BOD] [DEX] > OP TOE [DEL]

E.g., from a right toe delay- jump hard off the support leg and over the footbag when it's falling down. Scoop the footbag up before it hits the ground with the setting foot which never touches the ground.

Osis*
3 adds 		 
SET > (back or front) SPIN [BOD] > SAME or OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

Spin into a clipper delay. It is easiest to set this from a clipper stall on the other foot or from a toe delay with the other foot, straight in front of you (not behind you). E.g., from a right toe set, set straight in front of you, about waist high, and turn to the left so the footbag passes behind your back... Keep your head down with your eyes on the footbag until just before it lands on the inside surface of your right foot. Then turn quickly to the left (counterclockwise) and catch the footbag on a clipper delay.

Refraction
3 adds 		 
SET > OP INSIDE [DEL] > OP OUT [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD]

Start by inside delaying the footbag. Now while wrapping the footbag complete a 360 degree spin towards the support leg. Eliminate any pauses between the initial inside delay and spin to get the move looking nice and smooth.

Whirl
3 adds 		 
SET > OP IN [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

From either a clipper or a toe delay, set the footbag up- then with the support leg circle the footbag from the front up and over the footbag. Hop off of the original setting leg as you finish the dexterity and catch the bag on a clipper delay. It is easiest to point your toes down as you circle the bag because this makes it faster and less likely to snag the bag as you circle it.

Reverse
Whirl
3 adds 		 
SET > OP OUT [DEX] > OP CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

This move is a whirl with the direction of the dexterity reversed. As in a whirl, the dexterity is performed with the knee with the ankle circling the bag. This is unlike a butterfly where the hips are used to make the upper leg do the dexterity.

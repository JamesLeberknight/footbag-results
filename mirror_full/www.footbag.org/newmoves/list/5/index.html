Barfly Swirl
5 adds 		 
CLIP > SAME OUT [DEX] > SAME OUT [DEX] > OP BACK SWIRL [DEX] > SAME CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

Eg. From a right clipper set the bag around waist height travelling across your body. Circle the bag from out to in twice with the right leg. Then hop off the left leg and swirl the footbag with this leg ending in left clipper delay.

Paradox
Blender
5 adds 		 
CLIP > SAME IN [PDX] [DEX] > (BACK) SPIN [BOD] > SAME CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

The setting leg does all the work in this move. Set a clipper delay across your body- bring the setting leg from behind the support leg around the footbag in a Paradox Whirl motion. As the setting leg moves behind the footbag turn so the footbag passes behind your back into a setting foot Osis delay.

Spyro Gyro
(Gyro Butterfly Swirl)
5 adds 		 
CLIP > (BACK) SPIN [BOD] > SAME OUT [DEX] > OP BACK SWIRL [DEX] > SAME CLIP [XBD] [DEL]

A gyrating butterfly swirl delay. Eg. From a right clipper set somewhat behind you- turn to the right passing the footbag behind your back and bring the right leg over the footbag from out to in (butterfly motion) without ever putting the right leg down. Now land on the right leg as you complete a swirl with the left leg.

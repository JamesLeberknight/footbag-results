Well... Born '84, hacked since summer of '99, freestyled since summer of '01. Love it. Since the summer of 2004 where I was at my first international tournament (Euros, held here in Denmark), I've been re-learning all my basics, focusing on cleaness, bothsidedness and the ability to link out of them. Currently, I do no whirls and I do no doubledexes. Other than footbag, I like to draw and I enjoy my university education a lot, philosophy. I like almost enjoy everything that has to do with tricks, though I havn't learned anything other than footbag yet - not seriously anyways, because of the time it takes, I spend too much on footbag! Once my legs break down (someone forbid), I'll become the meanest card-shuffler and pen-spinner in.. at least my neighborhood! If there is anything I can do, please don't hesitate to ask.

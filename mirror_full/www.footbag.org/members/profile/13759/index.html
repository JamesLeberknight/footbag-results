I began to play footbag in in 1998 with a few partners in UNET (Universidad Nacional Experimental del Táchira). Has been a long journey since that, but with great rewards 'til today. Actually I'm three times southamerican Net Champion, in tournaments made in Venezuela (2006-2007) and Bogotá, Colombia (2006), and Coordinator an Founder of FOOTBAG TACHIRA, the biggest and more hardworking footbag club in Venezuela, creators of FOOTCAMP, one of the biggest efforts to the date for expanding this sport in latin america. Now my goal is to participate in a World Championship, making of that the starting point for the worldwide recognition of the Venezuelan Footbag KEEP SPIKING HARD!! peace.

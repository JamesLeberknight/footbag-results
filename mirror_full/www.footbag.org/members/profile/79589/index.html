Hey everyone, my name is Nick Polini and I'm originally from Mont Clare, PA. Graduated from PSU Main Campus May 4th, 2013 with a bachelor's degree in Wildlife Biology, minor in Biology. I love Freestyle Footbag. I play as much as I can. I also love to teach new players. Even if you have never kicked a bag before, I'm down to teach you if you want to learn. I'm president of Southeastern PA Shred and if you live close by or even 30 mins to an hour away, give me a call, I'd love to meet up with anyone. My club often meets up with a few other local clubs (a crew from Bluebell PA, a crew from Bethlehem PA, and a crew from NJ) near Bethlehem to make big jam sessions that are a lot of fun. Seriously, don't be shy, call me or drop me a text or email (I respond to texts or calls quicker). 484-995-5639

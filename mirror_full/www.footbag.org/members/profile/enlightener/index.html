Hi! I've been a tournament director since 1985, running the annual Midwest Regionals event every year. I've been at every "Worlds" since then (except Helsinki and Frankfort) as well. Montreal 2004 was my 20th year in a row at worlds, and while I had always tried to win the overall event, recently I have focused on freestyle, finally emerging victorious as the World Champion in 1999. I have been in the printing industry for over 20 years, and have recently become a software trainer for Apple, Inc. If you are ever passing through Chicago, drop me a line, maybe we can get a nice shred in while you are here! Check out: http://web.mac.com/enlightener for my footbag portfolio. I am proud to have been on the Freestyle Committee of the IFC since 1997 and being the Education Director of the IFPA for the past several years. During that time, I have also spearheaded the Certified Instructor program, created the content got the first few groups of instructors certified. Please contact me on any freestyle issue or any program you envision that could educate the public on the sport of footbag. See ya!

Hi everybody, some of you know me already and some don't. I've been involved as a player, manufacturer, trainer, performer, organizer in the marvelous sport of Footbag since 1981. You can visit my web site at (http://www.prokicker.ca) I hope I'll see a lot of you in tournaments. Train some new players and don't forget that girls can play too. We need more women in this sport. Long life to Footbag, I hope to see you all soon on a net court and don't forget the key words: never give up.

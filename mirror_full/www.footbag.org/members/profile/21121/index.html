I've been playing footbag for 7 years. I've took last year off to pursue my footbag interests and to really share this game with a lot of different people. In the first half of last year I created the Footbag 4 Fun program (www.melbournefootbag.com/footbag4fun) which went to 10 schools in Victoria and reached over 4000 students in both Primary and High schools. I went to Orlando for the World Championships in August 2007 and was lucky enough to make the final in Freestyle Routine. Currently I am continuing to teach and perform at schools all over Victoria in the second year of the Footbag 4 Fun program. I am really motivated to help promote footbag in Australia and I am very interested in ways to improve this process. I have a personal website www.danednie.com which has all the updates on what I have been up to or am about to do next! email me if you are interested in kicking with the Melbourne Footbag Club www.melbournefootbag.com keep kicking, dan

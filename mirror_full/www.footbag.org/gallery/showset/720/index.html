Worlds 2005 - my best shots

Best footbag net players spiking it hard and heavy. This gallery has other related information; click here to see the related information.

 
Florian G?tze (GER, left) in an air battle with Jani Markkanen (FIN, right)
G?tze and his partner Patrick Schrickel (GER) won against Markkanen and his partner Jukka Peltola (FIN) in a quarter final match in two games (11-8, 11-1). Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Florian G?tze (GER, right) in an air battle with Jukka Peltola (FIN, left)   (0.27M)
G?tze and his partner Patrick Schrickel (GER) went all the way to the finals in the open doubles net. This shot is from the quarter final match. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Florian G?tze (GER, middle) spiking
G?tze spiking against Jani Markkanen (FIN, left) and Jukka Peltola (Fin, right) in a quarter final match. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Florian G?tze (GER, left) vs. Jani Markkanen (FIN, right)   (0.25M)
Shot from Open Doubles Net quarter final match. G?tze and his teammate Patrick Schrickel (GER) won in two games. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
J.F. Lemieux (CAN, right) spiking
Lemieux and his partner Emmanuel Bouchard (CAN, far right) won their quarter final match against Jaakko Inkinen (FIN, far left) and Janne Uusitalo (FIN, left). Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Jani Markkanen (FIN, middle) spiking
Markkanen and his partner Jukka Peltola (FIN, right) lost against the mighty Germans Patrick Schrickel and Florian G?tze. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Jani Markkanen (FIN) with a powerful pulldown   (0.26M)
Markkanen and his teammate Jukka Peltola (FIN) lost the open doubles net quarter final match to Patrick Schrickel (GER, far left) and Florian G?tze (GER, left) in two games. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Janne Uusitalo (FIN) with a pulldown spike   (0.26M)
Chris Ott (USA, right) waiting to dig the hit. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
John Leys (USA) with a toe reverse spike
Leys and his partner P.T. Lovern (USA) won their quarter final match against Finns Tuomas K?rki and Olli Savolainen in two games. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Olli Savolainen (FIN, left) putting one down   (0.36M)
Savolainen and his partner Tuomas K?rki (FIN, right) lost their quarter final against John Leys and P.T. Lovern, both from USA. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
P.T. Lovern (USA) showing off one of his spikes
Finns Olli Savolainen and Tuomas K?rki are trying to dodge these bullets in their quarter final match. Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
P.T. Lovern's (USA, right) toe reverse is lethal   (0.27M)
Lovern and his partner John Leys (USA, far right) took the 3rd place in open doubles net. This shot is from their quarter final match against Tuomas K?rki (FIN, left) and Olli Savolainen (FIN, far left). Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Patrick Schrickel (GER, right) killing the bag with a toe reverse
Jani Markkanen (FIN, far left) and Jukka Peltola (FIN, far right) lost the rally and the match to Schrickel and Florian G?tze (GER, left). Photo by Matti Pohjola.
     
Tuomas K?rki (FIN, left) against John Leys (USA, right)   (0.25M)
